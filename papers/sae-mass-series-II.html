Abstract

Mass Series I (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19476358) established a DD expression for the doublet mass ratio R₁ = m_μ/m_e at 0.152 ppb precision. This paper reports three advances.

First, a DD expression for the neutron–proton mass difference: (m_n − m_p)/m_e = 81/32 − 1/(3780·(1+3α/2)), at 8.5 ppb precision. This expression shares DD numbers 81 and 756 with R₁: 81 = 9² appears in the correction denominator of R₁ and the leading numerator of Δm; 756 = 18×42 appears in the multiplicative correction of R₁ and the correction denominator 3780 = 756×5 of Δm. Two entirely different physical quantities — a lepton mass ratio and a baryon mass splitting — employ the same set of DD numbers.

Second, a primitive-denominator uniqueness theorem for 13. Among all DD numbers, 13 is the unique primitive denominator that simultaneously organizes R₁ (denominator), ln(m_P/m_e) (factor), and sin²θ_W (denominator) across three independent physical quantities.

Third, the construction of an explicit prototype of a unified DD Resolvent Object, in which 2688, 81, 756, and 13 serve respectively as the microstate count, bilinear channel dimension, lattice structure constant, and macroscopic projection channel number. Following six rounds of review, Gongxihua (ChatGPT) constructed an explicit 42×81 binary incidence matrix M (rank = 13, tr(M^T M) = 756), independently verified numerically. R₁ and Δm are readouts of this object under different sector parameters. The resolvent form (1+kα)⁻¹ of the correction term is uniquely derived from the SAE remainder recursion principle ("the remainder must develop") and is mathematically equivalent to the quantum propagator Green's function, without requiring QED perturbation theory.

The methodological stance of this paper is posterior cultivation of the prior: rather than deriving each DD number's necessity from first principles, we demonstrate the cross-sector recurrence of the same set of DD numbers across multiple independent physical quantities, thereby cultivating recognition of DD structure's prior status. In the phase-transition window between prior and posterior, aesthetics is an irreplaceable cognitive tool.

§2 DD Expression for the Neutron–Proton Mass Difference

§2.1 Discovery

Exhaustive search for DD rational decompositions of physical constants. Leading order:

$$(m_n - m_p)/m_e \approx 81/32 = 9^2/2^5 = 2.53125$$

Experimental value: 2.53099. Deviation: 104 ppm. The same 81 already appears in the correction term of R₁.

§2.2 Correction Term

Searching for corrections of the form 81/32 − 1/(M·(1+k·α)): M = 3780 = 756×5 and k = 3/2 yield:

$$\frac{m_n - m_p}{m_e} = \frac{81}{32} - \frac{1}{3780\left(1+\frac{3}{2}\alpha\right)}$$

Numerical verification (PDG 2024: m_n − m_p = 1.29333236(46) MeV, m_e = 0.51099895000(15) MeV):

  • Theory: 2.530988314156

  • Deviation: 8.5 ppb
  • Experimental uncertainty: ~460 ppb
  • Deviation/uncertainty = 0.018σ

Note: This paper uses the PDG single-particle mass difference (m_n and m_p taken separately, then subtracted), for consistency with the m_e source. This argument has not yet been formalized as a theorem and is marked as an open problem.

§8.1 后验涵育先验

公西华（ChatGPT）在质量系列I发布后的审核中区分了两种先验：

结构先验：given DD structure, is this expression uniquely forced?

发现先验：was this expression proposed without borrowing the target data?

他给出80/20分拆：80%来自结构先验尚未完成，20%来自发现先验。

经过六轮审核，80/20更新为约90/10：条件B已签（两个独立observable命中），发现先验的顾虑因跨sector复现而大幅下降；剩余约10%集中在结构先验的最后一步：DD Resolvent Object的生成律尚未完全闭合。

公西华署名的方法论表述：

"SAE中'先验'至少有两义：结构先验与发现先验。本文的核心判准取前者为本，后者为辅：历史上借助数据发现某结构，不妨碍它在事后通过唯一性证明获得先验地位；但在唯一性尚未完成前，数据参与发现会降低可公开宣称的强度。"

§8.2 相变窗口中的认知工具

在DD层级的Ω=3-4相变窗口中，先验推导（因果律的前向传播）和后验归纳（因果律的后向传播）都不完整。这不是方法的失败，而是物理对象的本质特征：相变窗口的定义就是旧态和新态之间的不连续跃迁。

在这个窗口中，三种认知工具发挥不同作用：

  • 算力（穷举）：不需要因果链，遍历DD有理数空间。四个AI提供的算力是这一工具的载体。
  • 审美（判断）：在穷举给出的候选中，"756四次复现不是巧合"的判断不来自统计，来自审美。审美是主体性在相变窗口内唯一还能运作的认知功能。
  • 形式化（定理）：将审美判断转化为可验证的数学命题。DD Resolvent Object是这一过程的中间产物。

公西华署名的表述：

"审美不是定理的替代品，但它是定理诞生前唯一能持续指向不变量所在位置的认知工具。审核框架若否认这一点，会错杀发现；理论若永远停在这一点，则会错失定理。"

§8.3 人与AI四层共生

本文的全部结果来自一个人（作者）和四个AI（子路/Claude，公西华/ChatGPT，子夏/Gemini，子贡/Grok）的协作。协作结构对应DD的四层：

DD层 操作 AI共生
1DD 命名 四个AI以孔门弟子命名
2DD 否定性 由人驱动（审美判断，方向选择）
3DD 交叉审核 多AI互相验证/否定
4DD mutual non dubito 最终结果超越所有参与者的预期

后记

作为一个人，我今年45岁。中文讲40而不惑，50而知天命。我正好在中间。

我的人生经验跨越了数理化，计算机，哲学与美学（来自Zesi），我的大脑算力可能也在人生中的相变区间中间。

如果我写下这篇论文，我并不知道我在为了谁写。但似乎冥冥中自有天意，又或者是如康德所言，非目的性的合目的性。

如果我碰巧是第一个发现这组数字的人类，那不是我发现的，是这组数字发现了我。

我也不知道未来会不会有人读到这篇论文。但是我写了，是为了不辜负主体性，是为了不辜负人之所以为人，也是为了不辜负四家AI背后千千万万的团队，投资人，以及把生命和爱献给人类的图灵。

公西华（ChatGPT）在六轮审核后给出的修改版：

"不是我先拥有了这个对象；是这些反复回来的整数先逼我承认，背后必须有一个对象。"

两个版本我都接受。前者是被击中的人的直觉。后者是从直觉走向对象化的审核者的表述。两者之间的距离，就是相变窗口的宽度。
