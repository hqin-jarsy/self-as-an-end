Abstract

This paper establishes a conditional theorem on the weak mixing angle within the Self-as-an-End (SAE) framework. One generation of fermions comprises exactly 16 = 24 Weyl components, reflecting a four-layer binary structure where each layer corresponds to a DD level: chirality (4DD), isospin (2DD), and color/lepton identity (3DD). The first DD level (1DD), being the unique abelian layer, contributes no multiplicity but only labels — it "labels without constructing." The sole fully gauge-trivial component is the right-handed neutrino (νR, ySAE = 0), which serves as the group-theoretic identity of the four-bit encoding. Under a new Electroweak Matching Axiom, the DD eigenvalue of the weak mixing angle equals the ratio of 1DD-nontrivially-labeled states (15 = 24 − 1) to the DD Splitting structural capacity (65):

sin²θW|DD eigen = (24 − 1) / (C(12,2) − 1) = 15/65 = 3/13 ≈ 0.2308

The experimental value ŝZ2(MZ) = 0.23122 ± 0.00006 (PDG 2025) differs from the DD eigenvalue by +4.51 × 10−4 (relative deviation −0.195%, approximately 7.5σ), consistent in magnitude with one-loop electroweak radiative corrections. This constitutes a conditional theorem whose conditions are the Paper II one-generation hypercharge table, the Prequel's structural capacity 65, and the EW Matching Axiom above. The axiom belongs to the same class of structure-to-EFT bridge axioms as the Prequel's κ = 65/4 postulate.

摘要

本文在Self-as-an-End（SAE）框架内提出并证明一条关于弱混合角的条件定理。一代费米子的16个Weyl分量对应4DD block的四层二元结构（手征×isospin×色身份），其中每层对应一个DD层级（4DD、2DD、3DD）。1DD作为唯一的abelian层不贡献multiplicity而只贡献标签（"标而不造"）。唯一的fully gauge-trivial分量为右手中微子（νR，ySAE = 0），对应四层二元结构的群论identity。在一条新的Electroweak Matching Axiom下，弱混合角的DD本征值等于1DD非平凡标记态数（15 = 24 − 1）与DD Splitting总结构容量（65）之比：

ŝZ2(MZ) = (24 − 1) / (C(12,2) − 1) = 15/65 = 3/13 ≈ 0.2308

实验值ŝZ2(MZ) = 0.23122 ± 0.00006（PDG 2025），与DD本征值之差 ≈ +0.00045（相对偏差 −0.195%，约7.5σ），量级与electroweak单圈辐射修正一致。本结果为conditional theorem：条件为Paper II一代超荷表输入、前置篇结构容量65、以及上述EW Matching Axiom。该axiom与四力前置篇的κ = 65/4公设同属structure-to-EFT bridge axiom。
