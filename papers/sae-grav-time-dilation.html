Gravitational Time Dilation in the SAE Framework: Causal Cell Throughput Derivation
SAE 框架下的时间膨胀——因果细胞吞吐量推导

Han Qin (秦汉)  ·  Independent Researcher  ·  2026
DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.19836185  ·  Full PDF on Zenodo  ·  CC BY 4.0
Abstract

Derives gravitational time dilation within the SAE framework using the causal cell throughput mechanism. The 4DD reading mechanism established in Four Forces Paper 0 implies that causal cell throughput varies with gravitational potential; this variation generates the standard gravitational time dilation formula. The derivation provides a structural (non-geometric) origin for time dilation grounded in the information-reading ontology of the SAE four-forces framework.

Keywords: SAE physics, gravitational time dilation, causal cell throughput, 4DD reading mechanism, information reading, four forces, general relativity
