T_ѕБ has three sorts: H (history terms), N (natural numbers), W (cost values). N-sort satisfies PA. 1. Design Principles

All axiom schemata and induction principles use standard ZFC tools. No new meta-theoretical frameworks are introduced. The term model M_cћД is a concrete set-theoretic object inside ZFC.
