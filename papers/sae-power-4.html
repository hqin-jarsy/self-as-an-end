Abstract

Paper 4 (限, Limit) is the fourth paper of the SAE Power Theory series, belonging to the same one-way dependent conceptual system as the Prequel and Papers 1 (origin), 2 (morphology), and 3 (recognition). It introduces no new theorem and re-establishes none of P1's propositions; it unfolds, along the dimension of remainder, the complete dynamics of T3 (the power-remainder theorem, P1 §8) within the domain of power.

The central claim of P4 is this: the remainder is the structural limit of power. Any power structure, however complete, meets its structural limit at ρ ≠ ∅; and this limit is not peculiar to the side of the one over whom power is exercised. It is ineliminable at every node of the power relation. The one over whom power is exercised carries a remainder; the one who exercises power carries a remainder; the 14DD apparatus that bears the power, qua construct, also carries a remainder. There is no remainder-exempt position.

P4 establishes four criteria. L1 (ontological absoluteness of the remainder) defines the remainder as the ontological-layer topological differential between a 13DD subject's unbounded DD-possibility and the finite option grid it occupies, symmetric across any subject in the relation and independent of subjective awareness. L2 (the three states of the remainder) partitions the remainder's expression, by amplitude, into the latent, manifest, and boundary-crossing states. L3 (monotonic irreversibility of remainder accumulation) gives the law that the remainder cannot be zeroed and cannot be losslessly absorbed by power. L4 (the ontological cut between remainder and back-binding) separates the remainder (T3), borne by both subjects, from back-binding (T6), borne by the one who exercises power, and assigns the apparatus's constructive-layer remainder to the remainder family rather than to back-binding.

The remainder is value-free, and its modes of expression are unbounded. What this paper gives are the invariants that run across every mode of expression, not a catalogue of those modes; the three states are a structural frame, not a taxonomy. The discrimination between severing-mode and cultivating-mode is given not here but by whether the T6 locking action is triggered; its complete dynamics are left to P5 (Cultivating) and P6 (Severing), and resistance and dissolution to P7 (Dissolution).

§I Introduction: The Remainder as Limit

Power theory is the dual series to the SAE Moral Law theory. The Moral Law proceeds from the inside outward, treating cases where DD-positions are symmetric or mutually sustaining; power theory proceeds from the outside inward, treating cases where DD-positions are asymmetric and not mutually sustaining. The Prequel cleared the referential space of power; P1 established the 13DD point of origin and the three sources, made the power–rights duality explicit, and foreshadowed the closure of the three theorems of remainder, duality, and back-binding. P2 unfolded the geometric, phase, and dimensional axes of power along the dimension of morphology, and treated the topological legitimacy of the apparatus as a 14DD narrative-bearer. P3 separated the registers of recognition and unfolded the state-machine dynamics of operational recognition. The task of P4 is, on this foundation, singular and definite: to unfold, along T3 (the power-remainder theorem, P1 §8), the complete dynamics of the remainder within the domain of power.

P4 introduces no new T-theorem. T3 was established in P1; P4 unfolds it, depending one-way on P1, P2, and P3, and does not in turn define the prior papers. P1 §9 already named P4 as the full unfolding of the remainder's application in the domain of power; P2 §IX.3 handed P4 the dynamics of the remainder across the various forms; P3 handed P4 the T3 remainder produced by each operational-recognition operation. This paper makes good on these three.

The central claim, in one sentence: the remainder is the structural limit of power. Any power structure, however complete, meets its structural limit at ρ ≠ ∅. This limit comes not from an external constraint, nor from the psychological discontent of the one over whom power is exercised, nor from moral sanction; it comes from the ineliminable remainder at every node internal to the power relation.

This limit is global, not peculiar to one side. The one over whom power is exercised, call him B, carries a remainder; the one who exercises it, call him A, carries a remainder; the 14DD apparatus that bears the power, qua construct, also carries a remainder. There is no remainder-exempt position, and the party that declares itself the holder of power is no exception. Power can neither fully capture the object on which it acts, nor reduce its own agent to a pure instrument, nor bring the apparatus that bears it to a remainderless closure.

The side of this claim that concerns the one over whom power is exercised is the depth, on that side, of T5 (the power–rights duality theorem); it is not a re-establishment of T5. T5 says that the constitution of power requires that B's subject-position be retained, and that stripping it to the limit makes power withdraw. P4 then says: the subject-position so retained necessarily carries an undigestible remainder. T5 gives the condition for power's constitution; P4 gives the structural consequence of that condition; the two stand apart. The remainders on the side of the one who exercises power and on the side of the apparatus do not run along T5 but along the generality of Via Rho, namely that any 13DD subject and any construct have ρ ≠ ∅; this already surfaced in P1 §8's T6, in the words that the deeper the locking, the more A's own subject-remainder is compressed. P4 makes this global distribution explicit; it remains an unfolding of T3, not a new establishment.

Two clarifications about the remainder itself come first.

First, the remainder is value-free. The remainder is neither good nor bad; the remainder is merely ineliminable. It does not stand opposed to power, is not the resource of the one over whom power is exercised, is not the kindling of resistance, is not a vessel of free will, and is not power's adversary. It is only the appearance, in the domain of power, of the structural fact ρ ≠ ∅, the ontological obverse of the subject-position on which power operates. Being ineliminable is not the same as exerting force in any direction, and carries no value orientation. The three states introduced below are therefore not a value progression: the latent state is not more oppressed than the manifest, the boundary-crossing state is not freer than the latent, resistance is not preferable to drift. The three states are intervals of expressive amplitude, not a moral ladder.

Second, the modes of the remainder's expression are unbounded, and this paper does not enumerate them. In a power relation the remainder can unfold in unboundedly many ways. What this paper gives are the invariants that run across every mode of expression, namely where the remainder comes from (L1), how it accumulates (L3), and how it is cut from back-binding (L4); it does not give a catalogue of modes. The three states used below to organize the discussion are a structural frame cut by expressive amplitude, not a taxonomy; the six phenomena gathered under the three states are illustrative anchors named by T3 in P1, not an exhaustive list. This discipline shares a root with P2 §I's confinement of morphology to the mechanical-topological sense rather than the biological-taxonomic sense, and runs in the same direction as P3 R2's minimal generating set rather than an exhaustive list.

A further boundary must be drawn to keep this paper from over-generalizing. Via Rho says that any construct has ρ ≠ ∅; this is SAE's general remainder discipline. This paper does not restate that general remainder theory; it treats only how the remainder becomes a limit within an already-registered power relation, that is, how the side of the one over whom power is exercised, the side of the one who exercises it, and the side of the apparatus each make power unable to become total. This paper is the dedicated unfolding of T3 in the domain of power; it does not overlap with Methodology 00.

One last guard, placed up front. This series does not predict, does not encourage, and does not evaluate any specific political event. The three states of the remainder below are a mechanical description at the structural level, not a prescription for action and not a prediction of events. The words resistance, backlash, escape, and migration refer in this paper only to the structural forms of remainder-expression, not to any specific historical event or call to action.

§II The Ontological Placement of the Remainder

Before tracing how the remainder unfolds, the remainder must be placed at the ontological layer. What the remainder is, and why it is ontologically absolute, is given by L1; how the remainder is cut from back-binding, and where the apparatus's constructive-layer remainder belongs, is given by L4.

> Criterion L1, ontological absoluteness of the remainder. The remainder (ρ) is the ontological-layer topological differential between a 13DD subject's unbounded DD-possibility and the finite option grid it occupies; ρ ≠ ∅ holds universally. This criterion is symmetric across any 13DD subject in the relation: B, the one over whom power is exercised, is confined by the grid A draws; A, the one who exercises power, is confined by the finite grid of exercise the structure affords (the finite action-set A can invoke); each side has an irreducible remainder. This differential does not depend on whether the subject is subjectively aware of it, does not depend on whether it produces discomfort, and does not depend on whether the subject is prepared to resist or to alter anything.

L1 invokes the ρ ≠ ∅ of Methodology 00 Via Rho directly and brings it down into the power relation. A 13DD subject's possibility is unbounded, while what any power structure can give it is a finite option grid. Between the unbounded possibility and the finite grid there is always a topological differential. That differential is the remainder. However dense the grid, it cannot be dense to the point of being equinumerous with unbounded possibility; hence ρ cannot be empty.

This criterion is symmetric across both sides of the relation. The remainder is not the privilege of the one over whom power is exercised. B is confined by the grid A draws, and B's unbounded possibility overflows that grid: this is B's remainder. But A is likewise a 13DD subject, and A's exercise can occur only through the finite grid of exercise the structure affords (the finite action-set A can invoke); A's unbounded possibility overflows that grid of exercise just as well: this is A's remainder. The remainder is distributed symmetrically across both sides of the relation, favoring neither (the unfolding of the side of the one who exercises power, and of the apparatus, is given in §VII).

The remainder is at the ontological layer, not the psychological layer. The remainder is not pent-up grievance, not a suppressed impulse, not the consciousness of resistance. It is a topological differential at the ontological layer; it does not depend on whether the subject is subjectively aware of it, does not depend on whether it produces discomfort in the subject, and does not depend on whether the subject is prepared to resist or to alter anything. Even where the one over whom power is exercised is subjectively in perfect compliance, without friction, this topological differential still exists (the perfect-compliance state under algorithmic power in §VIII proves this positively). The same holds on the side of the one who exercises power: A's remainder is not some psychological impulse of A's; A's off-script exercise is not an impulse, A's staying-the-hand is not mercy; they are the structural expression of A's possibility overflowing the grid of exercise. The remainder can leak through observable low-amplitude behavior; P3 §V.4's figure of people who, under repression, dare exchange only glances on the road is its standard form: the signal in averted eyes and posture is a leak at the behavioral layer, not some suppressed psychology.

> Criterion L4, the ontological cut between remainder and back-binding. The remainder (T3) is the undigestible residue of any 13DD subject in the relation (both B and A) and of any 14DD construct; ρ ≠ ∅ holds universally and is not confined to moments of locking. Back-binding (T6) is the self-binding on A's side that arises from the locking action, namely the compression of A's subject-remainder under locking (P1 §8); it accumulates only when locking is triggered. A's remainder (always present, present even without locking) and A's back-binding (present only with locking) stand apart: back-binding is the compression that locking performs on A's remainder, not A's remainder itself.

L4 cuts apart two things easily fused: remainder and back-binding. P1 §8's T6 says that the deeper the locking, the more A's subject-remainder is compressed. That sentence contains both remainder and compression, and if they are not separated, T3 (the remainder, ever-present) and T6 (back-binding, only under locking) get read as one thing. L4 locates their relation precisely: the remainder is the object, back-binding is an operation on that object.

The remainder (T3) is the undigestible residue on the side of the one over whom power is exercised, on the side of the one who exercises it, and of any construct; ρ ≠ ∅ holds universally and is not confined to moments of locking. It is ever-present, whether or not power performs locking. Back-binding (T6) is a specific dynamic on A's side alone: when A performs a downward locking on B, that is, tries to fix B's DD-position downward, A must continuously invest DD operational space to maintain that structure, A's own subject-remainder is thereby compressed, and A is back-bound by the structure A has locked. Back-binding accumulates only when the locking action is triggered. Hence A's remainder (ever-present, present even without locking) and A's back-binding (present only with locking) are two things: back-binding is the compression that locking performs on A's remainder, not A's remainder itself. When A does not lock, A's remainder freely unfolds as off-script exercise, modification, staying-the-hand, and so on; when A locks, this portion of A's remainder is compressed, A is bound into the structure A has locked, and at the limit A is locked into that structure together with the one over whom power is exercised.

The remainder on the side of the apparatus is of a different nature from that of the two subjects. The 14DD apparatus has no subject-remainder ρ; by SAE ontology, the apparatus is not a 13DD subject and has no field of self-consciousness (P2 §V.1). But the apparatus, qua construct, by Via Rho's principle that any construct produces a remainder, still produces its own constructive-layer remainder (mediator cost). This constructive-layer remainder belongs to the T3 remainder family and is heterogeneous with T6 back-binding. It must be stressed that the constructive-layer remainder is not a subject-remainder; it is not the apparatus feeling something, nor the apparatus wanting something; it is the interface failures, the rising complexity, the maintenance costs, and the brittleness-deposition the construct produces in maintaining its own closure. The apparatus must not be personified.

From this a key question can be handled: where does back-binding land for a locking applied through an apparatus? It lands on the set of 13DD key nodes that invoke the apparatus and perform the downward locking; it does not land on the apparatus. The apparatus has no ρ and cannot bear back-binding. Back-binding does not require a single final administrator to bear it; it is distributively anchored on every 13DD subject who invokes the apparatus and performs the core locking action on B: the engineer who writes the code, the manager who signs the order, each bearing in his own 13DD field one full share of T6, with back-binding not diluted across headcount (P2 §V.5, non-dilutability). The machine produces only physical wear (constructive-layer brittleness); living persons bear the topological back-binding. An apparatus grown so large that no 13DD subject any longer invokes or administers it, a fully headless apparatus, is by M2 no longer social power; it withdraws into an 11DD or 12DD physical phenomenon. The invariant is this: back-binding is always on a 13DD subject, and the apparatus never bears back-binding. The complete dynamics of the apparatus-layer remainder are not unfolded in this paper; they are left to P7 (Dissolution), the division of labor having been set in P2 §V.5. This section only establishes the peg for it, confirming that it belongs to the T3 family and is heterogeneous with back-binding.

§III The Expressive Structure of the Remainder: The Three States and Accumulation

After the remainder is placed at the ontological layer, the question is how it expresses. This section establishes L2 and L3: L2 gives the three-state frame of the remainder's expression, L3 gives the law of its accumulation.

The three states are derived from two features of any containing structure. Any structure that contains a subject has two features. The first is the absorption threshold: how much remainder the structure can absorb before the expression manifests. Every structure has some give and can absorb low-amplitude leakage up to a certain amplitude without letting that leakage cross out of containment into the open. The second is the boundary: the edge of the option grid, the outer extent of the range of displacement the structure permits.

These two features define the three states. If the remainder's expression is below the threshold and within the boundary, it is latent; if it crosses the threshold but remains within the boundary, it is manifest; if it crosses the boundary, it is boundary-crossing. Whichever state the remainder is in, its anchor is always the same ontological ρ.

The containing structure has one of itself for each node of the relation: the containing structure of the one over whom power is exercised is the grid A draws; the containing structure of the one who exercises power is the finite grid of exercise the structure affords; the containing structure of the apparatus is its own finite form. The three states are therefore node-neutral, applying equally to the side of the one over whom power is exercised, the side of the one who exercises it, and the side of the apparatus.

> Criterion L2, the three states of the remainder. By the amplitude of its expression relative to the absorption threshold and grid boundary of the structure the node occupies, the remainder of any node takes three expressive states: latent (sub-threshold and within the grid), manifest (over-threshold and within the grid), and boundary-crossing (across the grid boundary). The three states exhaustively cover the amplitude space; but within each state the remainder's concrete modes of expression are unbounded.

L2 contains two layers. First, the three states as amplitude intervals exhaustively cover the expression space: any single remainder-expression is either sub-threshold within the boundary, or over-threshold within the boundary, or across the boundary; there is no fourth landing point. Second, within each state the remainder's concrete modes of expression are unbounded and not enumerated here. The frame partitions landing points of amplitude, not kinds of expression; the three states are a mechanical-topological structural frame, not a biological-taxonomic species table.

The object that lands in one and only one state is a single remainder-expression, not the whole subject, the whole apparatus, or the whole relation. A single node can bear several remainder-expressions at once, each in a different state: some still latent, some already turned manifest, some already across the boundary. The three states characterize the landing point of a single remainder-expression, not the single overall state of a subject at a given moment.

By landing point, the six phenomena named by T3 in P1 each take their place as illustrative anchors. The latent state gathers drift and misreading, the manifest state gathers resistance and backlash, the boundary-crossing state gathers escape and migration. These six are anchors that show the frame holds, not an enumeration of modes of remainder-expression; their concrete mechanics are unfolded in §IV through §VII.

> Criterion L3, monotonic irreversibility of remainder accumulation. Every power operation (including the four operations of operational recognition in P3: producing, maintaining, steering, revoking) leaves behind, reshapes, or redistributes a remainder that cannot be losslessly absorbed. This monotonic irreversibility is not that magnitude only increases, but that the remainder cannot be zeroed and cannot be losslessly absorbed by the power structure (P3's non-Markovian machine: revocation is not a true undo); a local expression may decay, transfer, or reshape, and may even be taken up by cultivation into a new relational structure, but the total remainder does not vanish because power succeeded.

L3 attaches to P3's state-machine ontology. P3 established that every operation of operational recognition (producing, maintaining, steering, revoking) produces a T3 remainder, and that this state machine is a non-Markovian machine carrying irreversible friction: revocation is not a true undo, the broken mirror cannot be rejoined. L3 takes this up into the law of remainder accumulation.

Monotonic irreversibility means this: the remainder cannot be zeroed and cannot be losslessly absorbed by the power structure. It does not mean that the magnitude of each concrete remainder-expression only increases and never decreases. Both in fact and in structure there will be local leakage, transfer, recoding, and even uptake by cultivation into a new relational structure; some concrete expression may perfectly well decay, transfer, or reshape. What is monotonic is that the total remainder does not vanish because power succeeded, not the magnitude of each local expression. In this form L3 is consistent with Via Rho's remainder discipline.

One direct consequence of accumulation is a change of state: latent low-amplitude leakage accumulates and, once it crosses the threshold, turns into the manifest or boundary-crossing state. This accumulation is the remainder-side engine of the decay phase M4 (P2 §IV.4). M4 is driven jointly by the three factors of T3 (the remainder's ineliminability), T5 (the thinning of the seam), and the pressure of dominant-layer migration; the concrete dynamics of the ineliminability factor are precisely here. This paper takes up the remainder side of M4; the other two factors are not re-unfolded here.

The modal calibration that follows holds throughout. That the remainder is ineliminable and that accumulation is monotonic are structural necessities; when the remainder crosses the threshold, and when it changes state, are empirically contingent. The register is consistent with P1 §7: whether migration occurs is settled by structure, when migration occurs is settled by many factors. The existence of the remainder does not entail the necessary occurrence of any specific outcome.

The case of cultivation's success must, here, hold L1 to the end. When a cultivating power succeeds, the positional difference is dissolved and the relation comes apart; a natural question follows: does the remainder vanish along with it, and does L1's absoluteness thereby collapse? It does not. Cultivation's dissolution does not constitute a zeroing of the remainder; it only dissolves one particular power grid, so that the remainder in that relation no longer expresses in confined form, while the subject's absolute ontological ρ, qua 13DD subject, persists undiminished. The full distinction, between the local relational grid and the absolute ontological ρ, is given in §IX.

§IV The Latent-State Remainder: Drift and Misreading

The latent state is the remainder's expression below the threshold and within the grid. It does not manifest as open confrontation, nor does it cross out of the range of displacement the structure permits; it leaks in low-amplitude form inside the grid. For the latent-state remainder on the side of the one over whom power is exercised, T3 named two phenomena in P1 as anchors: drift and misreading.

Drift is the latent state at the behavioral layer. The actions of the one over whom power is exercised slide slowly within the grid, deviating from the direction A points to, yet each step still falls within the permitted options. The structure can absorb this low-amplitude slide; it does not manifest, does not trigger confrontation, and so goes long unnoticed by the one who exercises power. But drift accumulates by L3, and after a time the position the one over whom power is exercised actually occupies has come a considerable distance from the position A set, a distance that never passed through a single open crossing.

Misreading is the latent state at the registration layer. It means that the content of B's registration of the structure deviates from the structure itself, and from what A intended it to become. Here misreading must be distinguished from a superficially similar case. If, through source-displacement (T4), the one who exercises power succeeds in making the one over whom power is exercised believe that power comes from some claimed source, and the latter acts a hundred percent by that claimed logic, that is not misreading; that is the latter completing a co-directional registration (P3) within A's grid, the success of T4, not an expression of B's remainder. True misreading is something else: B's remainder erects a shadow topology inside the grid. B has not stepped out of the larger grid, on the surface still complies, still in the latent state; but B's internal anchor for the structure has already deviated from the safe zone A delimited. An example: A uses the Mandate of Heaven to mask the actual source of power (T4, A's displacement of his own source), while B reads the Mandate of Heaven as the principle that when the ruler is without the Way he may be opposed (misreading, a topological mutation of B's remainder). These two share a root, both being a deviation between registration-content and structure; but they stand apart. T4 is A's displacement of his own source; misreading is the topological mutation of B's remainder beneath an appearance of compliance. The shadow topology is still in the latent state, since it has not yet crossed the threshold, but it is already a seed accumulating by L3.

This accumulation has a physical outlet; it does not stay at the psychological layer. The shadow topology makes B's output always carry a faint deviation; the one who exercises power, or the apparatus that bears it, in order that B's every output continue to fit the grid, must invest ever more verification: disambiguating, issuing denser and longer instructions, configuring stricter review. This rise in verification overhead is the real friction that misreading exacts from the structure, and it is the mechanical channel by which misreading accumulates by L3 and finally feeds into the decay phase M4. Misreading is therefore not a psychological game but the entropy increase of the structure.

The ontological absoluteness in L1 is most evident here. Neither drift nor misreading requires the one over whom power is exercised to feel discomfort or to be prepared to resist. Even where he is subjectively in perfect compliance, without friction, his actions may still be drifting slowly, and his registration of the structure may still carry a shadow topology he himself may not articulate; for ρ ≠ ∅ does not depend on whether he is aware. The latent-state remainder can leak through observable low-amplitude behavior, and the figure of the glance exchanged in silence on the road (P3 §V.4) is its standard form: a leak at the behavioral layer, not some suppressed psychology.

§V The Manifest-State Remainder: Resistance and Backlash

The manifest state is the remainder's expression that has crossed the threshold yet remains within the grid. Crossing the threshold means the expression is no longer quietly absorbed by the structure but manifests and is registered by both sides; remaining within the grid means the relation has not yet come apart, the one over whom power is exercised is still inside this relation. For the manifest-state remainder on his side, T3 named two anchors: resistance and backlash.

Resistance is a reverse vector the one over whom power is exercised outputs. He no longer merely slides slowly but, at the behavioral layer, actively pushes against the direction A points to. Resistance manifests and is registered by A as confrontation, but he is still inside the relation, still within the boundary of the grid. Resistance in this paper means only this reverse vector at the behavioral layer, not any emotional state; it is the structural expression of the remainder after it crosses the threshold, not a psychological description such as the anger of the oppressed.

Backlash is the accumulated remainder discharging at the structure. The remainder accumulated on B's side shows not only as a reverse vector aimed at A; it also acts on the structure itself in the form of raising the cost of maintenance: to keep containing this over-threshold remainder, the structure must invest ever more maintenance, the seam thins accordingly, and brittleness accumulates accordingly. This is the same dynamic as the remainder-rebound spoken of in P1 §8; this paper uses the single word backlash for it. Backlash still occurs within the relation.

Backlash must be distinguished from back-binding. Backlash is T3, the discharge of B's remainder at the structure; back-binding is T6, the compression of A's own remainder that arises because A performs locking (§II). When A performs a downward locking on B, the two rise together: B's grid is narrowed, his remainder swells, and his discharge at the structure grows fiercer (backlash intensifies); A invests continuously to maintain that locking, and his own remainder is compressed the deeper (back-binding deepens). But the two are heterogeneous and on different sides, and cannot swallow each other: backlash is the B-side remainder the structure bears, back-binding is the self-binding A bears, the one landing between the one over whom power is exercised and the structure, the other landing on the one who exercises it himself.

That backlash raises the cost of maintenance is precisely the concrete dynamic of the decay phase M4 (P2 §IV.4) on the remainder side: M4 is driven by the three factors of T3 (the remainder's ineliminability), T5 (the thinning of the seam), and the pressure of dominant-layer migration, and backlash is how the ineliminability factor among them acts on the structure. In a multi-agent network the manifest-state remainder has a further channel of amplification: when a bystander node in the recognition network flips this relation from co-directional registration to counter-directional registration, backlash spreads through the network and reputation collapses accordingly (P3 §VI.3); the manifest state is the visible side of that collapse. The complete mechanism of its network spread is left to P7.

§VI The Boundary-Crossing-State Remainder: Escape and Migration

The boundary-crossing state is the remainder's expression that has crossed the grid boundary. Crossing the boundary means the relation can no longer contain this remainder: either the one over whom power is exercised has left this relation, or the whole power structure has been overtaken by the population it occupies. For the boundary-crossing-state remainder on his side, T3 named two anchors: escape and migration. It should first be said that the word migration here is a mechanical description at the structural level, not a prediction, encouragement, or evaluation of any specific historical event (the series's guard is given in §I).

Escape is an individual crossing out of the grid. P1 §1 already named the space of departure as the remainder of the one over whom power is exercised; escape is the cashing-out of that space of departure. The space of departure always exists structurally, which is necessary, because ρ ≠ ∅; but in most cases it does not actually occur, because the cost of departure may be extremely high (P1 §5). Whether escape is cashed out is settled by cost and circumstance and is empirically contingent; that escape persists as a structural possibility is the necessity of L1.

Migration is a crossing out of the grid at the structural level. It is not that some one over whom power is exercised leaves, but that the whole power structure is overtaken by the population it governs. P1 §7 already named its mechanism: a power structure runs successfully, triggering a rise in the population's DD ratio; the risen population exerts stress on the original structure, and along the severing path this rise appears mainly, and indirectly, through remainder-rebound. Migration sits on this line of remainder-rebound: the accumulated remainder crosses out of the boundary the old grid can contain, and the relation migrates toward a new dominant layer. This paper makes evident the remainder-side engine of the dominant-layer migration thesis; it does not re-establish that thesis itself.

Escape and migration constitute the two scales of the boundary-crossing state, one at the individual and one at the structural level. The two share one modality: the remainder's ineliminability guarantees that the structural possibility of the boundary-crossing state always exists; but when the crossing is cashed out is settled by empirical conditions. The existence of the remainder does not entail the necessary occurrence of escape or migration.

§VII The Remainder on the Side of the One Who Exercises Power, and the Constructive-Layer Remainder

§IV through §VI walked the three states along the side of the one over whom power is exercised. The three states are node-neutral (§III), and the same frame applies equally to the side of the one who exercises power and to the side of the apparatus. The remainder is not the monopoly of the one over whom power is exercised; the one who exercises it and the apparatus that bears the power each have their own remainder, each unfolding by the three states.

The remainder on the side of the one who exercises power comes from the differential between A's unbounded possibility as a 13DD subject and the finite grid of exercise the structure affords (§II). It unfolds by the three states. The latent state is A's possibility deviating slightly within the script of exercise: A wields the power the structure authorizes, but with personal manners and slight deviations the structure never encoded. The manifest state is this remainder crossing the threshold while still within the position of exercise: A's exercise crosses out of the structure's script (off-script exercise), or A makes a visible alteration to the structure itself (modification). The boundary-crossing state is A crossing out of the position of exercise itself: A abandons his own position of exercise, or A's modification grows so large that the old structure is overtaken. These expressions can take unboundedly many forms; abuse, modification, and inaction are only three anchors among them, and all must be read in the structural sense, value-free. Abuse here means an exercise crossing out of the structure's script, not the moral judgment of misuse; modification means rewriting the structure itself; inaction means A staying his hand, since A's possibility itself contains the branch of not exercising, while the structure's script encodes A as one who exercises, so this branch overflows as remainder.

The remainder on the side of the one who exercises power and his back-binding are two things (§II, running in the same direction as the back-binding theorem T6 in P1 §8). The remainder is ever-present, present even without locking, and freely unfolds as the forms above; back-binding arises only when A performs a downward locking and is the compression of this remainder of A's under locking. The deeper A locks, the more his own remainder is compressed, the more his freely-unfolding off-script remainder is suppressed, and the more A is bound into the structure he has locked, at the limit locked into it together with the one over whom power is exercised. Modification must further be distinguished from migration: modification is A actively rewriting the structure, an expression of the remainder on the side of the one who exercises power; migration is the structure being overtaken by stress on the side of the one over whom power is exercised (§VI), not something A does actively. The two run in different directions and cannot be fused.

One vector must be singled out here. A's off-script exercise is A's possibility overflowing, a release of A's remainder; but if the direction of that overflowing action happens to point at fixing the one over whom power is exercised downward, then in one and the same action release and locking coincide. A jailer who on his own initiative intensifies the treatment of a prisoner is at once an exercise crossing out of the script (a release of his own remainder) and a downward locking on the prisoner (a pull of the T6 trigger). In that moment A casts the very freedom he has let overflow into a chain around himself: the remainder he releases is used as a locking action, and the locking action back-binds him by T6. Therefore releasing the remainder is not the same as gaining freedom; when the remainder is used for downward locking, it becomes the fuel of back-binding. The release of the remainder and the accumulation of back-binding are, when not pointed at locking, two things that stand apart (§II); in the moment they point at locking, they occur isomorphically in one and the same action.

The remainder on the side of the apparatus is the constructive-layer remainder (mediator cost), not a subject-remainder (§II). It is not the apparatus feeling or wanting something, but the interface failures, the rising complexity, the maintenance costs, and the brittleness-deposition the construct produces in maintaining its own closure. It too unfolds by the three states: the latent state is low-amplitude seepage of brittleness within the apparatus's tolerance; the manifest state is rising complexity and interface failures manifesting as a visible maintenance burden; the boundary-crossing state is the narrative-bearing capacity collapsing to the point of the apparatus itself dissolving. Though the apparatus, qua construct, has a remainder, it is a borrowing node: it is a power apparatus because there is a set of 13DD key nodes that invoke and administer it; for a locking applied through the apparatus, back-binding lands on this set of key nodes, not on the apparatus (§II). The complete dynamics of the apparatus-layer remainder, including how it walks the line of brittleness-accumulation toward dissolution, are left to P7 (Dissolution), the division of labor having been set in P2 §V.5.

With this the global distribution of the remainder is walked through: on the side of the one over whom power is exercised, on the side of the one who exercises it, and on the side of the apparatus, all three kinds of node carry an undigestible remainder (the two subjects an ontological ρ, the apparatus a constructive-layer remainder), all unfold by the same set of three states, and within each state the concrete expression can take unboundedly many forms. There is no remainder-exempt node in a power relation.

§VIII The Dynamics of the Remainder Across the Various Forms

§IV through §VII cut the remainder by expressive state and node (the three states on the side of the one over whom power is exercised, the side of the one who exercises it, and the side of the apparatus). The remainder can also be cut by P2's forms. P2 §IX.3 handed this paper the concrete dynamics of the remainder across the various forms, and this section makes good on it.

Cutting by expressive state and cutting by form are two independent blades, and they cross. The form does not settle whether the remainder exists (ρ ≠ ∅ holds for all forms); what the form settles is which state the remainder tends to land in. Below, one place is taken from each of P2's three axes, geometric, phase, and dimensional, to show how the form shapes the state of the remainder. What is taken from each of the three axes is only an example, not an exhaustive form-by-state correspondence.

By the geometric axis, covert severing and overt severing differ systematically in the state of the remainder. Covert severing keeps the remainder of the one over whom power is exercised long in the latent state: he misreads the result of severing as natural order or free choice, the remainder leaks in the low-amplitude forms of drift and misreading, and the short-term cost of maintenance is accordingly low. But once the covertness is broken, a large amount of the latent-state remainder accumulated by L3 crosses the threshold in batches and turns manifest: the one over whom power is exercised re-registers the prior severing as illegitimate, and backlash manifests abruptly. This is the remainder-side mechanism of P2 §III.3's point that the covert is no more stable than the overt: what covertness lowers is the short-term cost, what it accumulates is the batch change-of-state at threshold-crossing. Apparatus severing, on top of the remainder on the subject's side, lays a further layer of the apparatus's own constructive-layer remainder (§VII).

By the phase axis, the remainder accumulates across the four phases of establishment, maintenance, transmission, and decay, and the transmission phase is the most worth looking at. Transmission is not simple copying (P2 §IV.3): in transmission across generations and across changes of personnel, the new bearer re-registers the structure by his own DD-position, and the remainder is redistributed in the moment of transmission rather than carried over intact. The decay phase, in turn, is driven by the remainder, the engine of M4 on the remainder side (§V).

By the dimensional axis, the remainder has its forms at 13DD, 14DD, and 15DD, and the perfect-compliance state of algorithmic power is the apex for testing L1. The three-layer severing chain of algorithmic power is highly covert (P2 §VI), and at its deepest the one over whom power is exercised has perfectly internalized the algorithm's steering, without any subjective friction or consciousness of resistance. At this point a direct question arises: if he is psychologically in complete compliance, where does the remainder come from, and does L1 fail here? The answer is that L1 receives here its strongest confirmation, not its failure. The remainder is not his psychological discomfort; it is the ontological-layer topological differential between his unbounded possibility as a 13DD subject and the finite option grid the algorithm provides. This differential does not depend on whether he is aware. Even where he is in perfect compliance, this differential still exists. So long as the apparatus tries to maintain the appearance of perfect compliance, it must continuously expend computation, energy, and maintenance cost to smooth out the slight inconsistencies this differential produces. The accumulation of brittleness on the apparatus side (§VII) is in essence the accumulation of the cost of this maintaining action; when this cost exceeds the threshold the apparatus can bear, the decay phase manifests (M4). Hence the drive of M4 under algorithmic power does not depend on his psychological state but on the persistence of this ontological differential. In the limit case where he is subjectively wholly unaware, ρ ≠ ∅ still holds, and the whole mechanics of the remainder remain intact. This is the one place where L1's ontological absoluteness, L3's accumulation, and the de-psychologizing of the remainder reach their strongest together.

§IX Interfaces with the Series

This paper unfolds, along T3, the remainder's function as a limit; it does not unfold direction, and does not unfold dissolution. Direction and dissolution are left to later papers; the interfaces are given here.

The interface with P5 (Cultivating) and P6 (Severing) lies in direction. The discrimination between severing-mode and cultivating-mode is given not by this paper but by whether the T6 locking action is triggered. The remainder's orientation differs under the two directions: along the severing path, the accumulated remainder builds toward backlash, escape, and migration, acting against the structure; along the cultivating path, the accumulated remainder is taken up into the relation toward the dissolution of the positional difference itself, and P2 §IV.4 records this self-dissolution as the mark of cultivation's success. It must be stressed that the success here is a property of the direction, discriminated by T6, not a property of the remainder itself; the remainder itself remains value-free. The remainder on the side of the one who exercises power likewise forks by direction: under severing, A's remainder is compressed by locking (back-binding), while A's off-script remainder leaks as usual; under cultivation, A does not lock, A's remainder is not compressed, and its orientation is toward releasing the one over whom power is exercised. The complete dual dynamics of both sides are left to P5 and P6.

The case of cultivation's success must, here, hold L1 to the end. When a cultivating power succeeds, the positional difference is dissolved and the relation comes apart; a question follows: does the remainder zero out as well? It does not. The key is that what cultivation does is not reduce the remainder at all, but raise the one over whom power was exercised to a higher DD level, and dissolve the particular grid A imposed within that relation. Two things must be separated. One is that local relational grid: the finite option grid A drew for the one over whom power was exercised within that relation. When cultivation succeeds, A has dissolved this grid; the one over whom power was exercised is no longer confined by this particular grid, and that local relation of confinement thereby loses its definition, rather than some differential being computed down to zero. The other is the absolute ontological ρ: the remainder between his possibility as a 13DD subject and any finite structure, given by L1, with ρ ≠ ∅ holding universally. Cultivation does not cure, does not fill, and does not erase this absolute ontological ρ.

A subject raised to a higher level has a greater total remainder. At the population level this rise has two co-directional destinations. The first is that the overlap of mutual registrability and mutual sustaining grows larger, that is, absolute consensus increases. The second is that the remainder-differences among individuals grow larger. The two run in the same direction, and the difference grows the stronger: the increment of each subject's total remainder is greater than the increment that falls into absolute consensus, and the surplus precipitates as difference. Diversification is therefore not a subtraction from absolute consensus but the surplus left after the total's increment exceeds the consensus's increment, and this surplus is amplified with the rise. Hence even as mutual sustaining keeps deepening, the remainder-differences, far from being absorbed, expand; ρ ≠ ∅ holds at the population level along the direction of the rise just as round-the-clock, and the more strongly so. Cultivation moves the DD level; what it dissolves is the local grid that imprisoned the remainder; the remainder itself, as the subject rises to a higher level, continues to exist, and at the population level even grows rather than diminishes. What is dissolved is only the local grid of imprisonment, not the remainder. The deepened sense of the cannot-but toward others, and the will to cultivate, that a risen subject comes to display are the directional dynamics of cultivating power, whose complete unfolding is left to P5 (Cultivating).

The interface with P7 (Dissolution) lies in dissolution. The complete dynamics of the apparatus-layer remainder, namely how the constructive-layer remainder walks the line of brittleness-accumulation toward the apparatus's dissolution, are left to P7 (P2 §V.5). The remainder-side engine of the decay phase (§V), together with the spread of manifest-state backlash through the recognition network (P3 §VI.3's flip of bystander recognition), provide together the remainder-side basis for P7's resistance and dissolution.

The interface with M4 lies in decay. This paper takes up the remainder side of M4, namely the concrete dynamics of the T3-ineliminability factor; the other two factors of M4, the thinning of the seam (T5) and the pressure of dominant-layer migration, are not re-unfolded here. The dominant-layer migration thesis has its remainder-side engine made evident in §VI, likewise without re-establishment.

§X Conclusion

This paper unfolds, along T3 (the power-remainder theorem), the complete dynamics of the remainder within the domain of power, introducing no new T-theorem. The conceptual machinery established in P1 receives, along the dimension of the remainder, its full unfolding.

The results of this paper can be gathered into four. First, the remainder is the ontological-layer topological differential between a 13DD subject's unbounded possibility and the finite option grid it occupies; ρ ≠ ∅ holds universally, symmetric across any subject in the relation, independent of subjective awareness (L1). Second, the remainder's expression, by its amplitude relative to a structure's absorption threshold and grid boundary, takes the latent, manifest, and boundary-crossing states; the three states are a structural frame of expressive amplitude, a single remainder-expression lands in one and only one state, and within each state the modes of unfolding are unbounded (L2). Third, remainder accumulation is monotonically irreversible, that is, the remainder cannot be zeroed and cannot be losslessly absorbed by power, rather than each expression only increasing in magnitude; this accumulation is the remainder-side engine of the decay phase (L3). Fourth, remainder and back-binding belong to two sides: the remainder is the undigestible residue of both subjects in the relation and of any construct, while back-binding is the compression of A's own remainder due to locking; for a locking applied through an apparatus, back-binding lands on the set of key nodes that invoke and administer the apparatus, and the apparatus does not bear back-binding (L4).

From this, the remainder is the structural limit of power, and this limit is global. On the side of the one over whom power is exercised, on the side of the one who exercises it, and on the side of the apparatus, all three kinds of node carry an undigestible remainder, and there is no remainder-exempt position in a power relation. The limit of power comes not from an external constraint, not from the psychological discontent of the one over whom power is exercised, not from moral sanction; it comes from the ineliminable remainder at every node internal to the power relation.

The remainder is value-free, and its modes of expression are unbounded. The discrimination between severing-mode and cultivating-mode is given not by this paper but by whether the T6 locking action is triggered. The complete dynamics of severing power are left to P6 (Severing), the complete dynamics of cultivating power to P5 (Cultivating), and resistance and dissolution to P7 (Dissolution). This paper's work is complete; P5 (Cultivating) takes up the directional dynamics of cultivating power.

§I 引言: 余项作为限度

权力论是 SAE 道德律论的对偶系列。 道德律自内而外处理 DD 位置对称或互维持的情形, 权力论自外而内处理 DD 位置非对称且非互维持的情形。 前置篇清出了权力的指称空间, P1 立了 13DD 起源点与三层源, 显化了权力权利对偶, 预告了余项, 对偶, 反缚三定理的闭环。 P2 沿形态学维度展开了权力的几何, 相位, 维度三轴, 处理了装置作为 14DD 叙事承载的拓扑学合法性。 P3 切清了承认的三个位格, 展开了操作层承认的状态机动力学。 本篇 P4 的任务在此地基上单一而确定: 沿 T3 权力余项定理 (P1 §8) 把余项在权力领域的完整动力学展开。

本篇不立新 T 定理。 T3 已在 P1 立桩, P4 沿 T3 展开, 单向依赖 P1 P2 P3, 不反过来定义前篇。 P1 §9 已点名 P4 全面展开余项在权力领域的应用, P2 §IX.3 把余项在不同形态中的具体动力学点给 P4, P3 把每次操作层承认操作产生的 T3 余项交给 P4。 本篇兑现这三处点名。

核心断言一句话: 余项即权力的结构性限度。 任何权力结构, 无论多完备, 都在 ρ ≠ ∅ 处遇到自己的结构限度。 这一限度不来自外部约束, 不来自被权力者的心理不满, 也不来自道德制裁; 它来自权力关系内部每一个节点都无法消除的余项。

这一限度是全域的, 不是被权力者一侧的特例。 被权力者 B 携带余项, 施权者 A 携带余项, 承载权力的 14DD 装置作为构也携带余项。 没有余项豁免的位置, 宣布拥有权力的一方不例外。 权力既不能完全捕获它所施为的对象, 也不能把自己的施为者还原为纯粹的工具, 更不能让承载自己的装置达到无余的闭合。

这一断言的被权力者一侧是 T5 权力权利对偶定理在被权力者侧的纵深, 不是 T5 的重立。 T5 说权力的成立需要 B 的主体位置被保留, 剥夺到极致权力即退场。 P4 接着说: 被保留的那个主体位置必然携带不可消化的余项。 T5 给出权力成立的条件, P4 给出该条件的结构后果, 两者分立。 施权者一侧与装置侧的余项不走 T5 这条线, 走 Via Rho 的普遍性, 即任何 13DD 主体与任何构都 ρ ≠ ∅; 这一点已在 P1 §8 T6 的 锁定越深 A 主体性余项被压缩越多 一句中露头。 P4 把这条全域分布显化, 仍是 T3 的展开, 不是新立。

关于余项本身, 须先廓清两点。

其一, 余项无价。 余项不是好, 也不是坏; 余项只是不可消灭。 它不站在权力的对立面, 不是被权力者的资源, 不是抵抗的火种, 不是自由意志的容器, 也不是权力的敌手。 它只是 ρ ≠ ∅ 这一结构事实在权力领域的呈现, 是权力所操作的主体位置的本体另一面。 不可消灭不等于朝任何方向使力, 不等于任何价值取向。 后文的三态因此不是价值进程: 隐态不比显态更受压, 越界态不比隐态更自由, 抵抗不比漂移更可取。 三态是表达幅度的区间, 不是道德阶梯。

其二, 余项的展开方式无限。 余项在权力关系中可以以无限种方式展开, 无法穷举。 本篇给出的是横贯一切展开方式的不变量, 即余项从哪里来 (L1), 余项如何累积 (L3), 余项与反缚如何切分 (L4), 而不是展开方式的目录。 后文用来组织讨论的三态是按表达幅度切出的结构框架, 不是分类学; 三态收纳的六类现象是 T3 在 P1 点名的示例锚点, 不是穷举列表。 这与 P2 §I 把形态学严格限定在力学拓扑义而不取生物分类学义一致, 也与 P3 R2 的最小生成集而非穷尽列表同向。

由此还须划清一条边界。 Via Rho 说任何构都 ρ ≠ ∅, 这是 SAE 的一般余项纪律。 本篇不重述这条一般余项论, 只处理余项在已登记的权力关系中如何成为限度, 即被权力者侧, 施权者侧, 装置侧各自如何使权力无法成为全权。 本篇是 T3 在权力领域的专门展开, 不与 Methodology 00 重叠。

本系列不预测, 不鼓励, 不评价任何具体政治事件。 后文的余项三态是结构层面的力学描述, 不是行动处方, 不是事件预测。 抵抗, 反噬, 逃逸, 迁移这些词在本篇只指余项表达的结构形态, 不指任何具体历史事件或行动号召。

命题预告

本篇立四条判据, 沿 T3 展开, 不立新 T。 此处先给四条的一句话预告, 完整陈述分布于 §II 与 §III, 给读者一张判据地图。

L1 余项本体绝对性. 余项是 13DD 主体无限 DD 可能性与其所处有限选项网格之间的本体层拓扑差值, ρ ≠ ∅ 普遍成立, 对关系中任一主体对称, 不依赖主观察觉。 完整陈述见 §II。

L2 余项三态. 余项的表达按其幅度相对于结构吸收阈值与网格边界, 呈隐态, 显态, 越界态三态; 三态是表达幅度的结构框架, 不是现象的穷举。 完整陈述见 §III。

L3 余项累积的单调不可逆. 每一次权力操作都会留下, 改形或重新分配不可无损吸收的余项; 此单调不是量值只增, 而是余项不可归零, 不可被权力无损吸收。 完整陈述见 §III。

L4 余项与反缚的本体切分. 余项 (T3) 是被权力者与施权者两主体及任一构的不可消化剩余; 反缚 (T6) 是施权者因锁定动作而起的自缚。 完整陈述见 §II。

§II 余项的本体定位

追踪余项如何展开之前, 须先把余项定位在本体层。 余项是什么, 它在本体上为何绝对, 由 L1 给出; 余项与反缚如何切分, 装置的构层余项归于何处, 由 L4 给出。

> 判据 L1 余项本体绝对性. 余项 (remainder, ρ) 是 13DD 主体无限 DD 可能性与其所处有限选项网格 (option grid) 之间的本体层拓扑差值, ρ ≠ ∅ 普遍成立。 此判据对关系中任一 13DD 主体对称: 被权力者 B 受困于施权者 A 划定的网格, 施权者 A 受困于结构所予的有限施为网格 (A 能调用的有限动作集), 两侧各有不可化约的余项。 此差值不依赖该主体是否主观察觉, 不依赖是否产生不适, 不依赖是否准备抵抗或改动。

L1 直接调用 Methodology 00 Via Rho 的 ρ ≠ ∅, 把它落到权力关系中。 一个 13DD 主体的可能性是无限的, 而任何权力结构能给它的, 是一张有限的选项网格。 无限的可能性与有限的网格之间, 永远有一个拓扑差值。 这个差值就是余项。 网格再密, 也密不到与无限可能性等势; 因此 ρ 不可能为空。

这一判据对关系两侧对称。 余项不是被权力者的特权。 被权力者 B 受困于施权者 A 划定的网格, B 的无限可能性溢出这张网格, 这是 B 的余项。 但施权者 A 同样是一个 13DD 主体, A 的施为只能经由结构所予的一张有限施为网格 (A 能调用的有限动作集) 发生; A 的无限可能性同样溢出这张施为网格, 这是 A 的余项。 余项对称地分布在关系两侧, 不偏袒任何一方。 这一对称性防止本篇被读成被权力者一侧的故事 (施权者侧与装置侧的展开见 §VII)。

余项在本体层, 不在心理层。 余项不是憋屈, 不是压抑的冲动, 不是反抗的意识。 它是本体层的拓扑差值, 不依赖主体是否主观察觉到它, 不依赖主体是否因它产生不适, 不依赖主体是否准备抵抗或改动。 即使一个被权力者主观上完美顺应, 毫无摩擦, 这个拓扑差值依然存在 (§VIII 的算法完美顺应态正面证此)。 施权者一侧亦然: A 的余项不是 A 的某种心理冲动, A 的越脚本施为不是 冲动 , A 的收手不施不是 仁慈 ; 它们都是 A 的可能性溢出施为网格的结构表达。 余项可以以可观察的低幅行为泄出 (P3 §V.4 的 道路以目 , 目光与姿态回避里的信号), 但那是行为层的泄出, 不是心理状态。

> 判据 L4 余项与反缚的本体切分. 余项 (T3) 是关系中任一 13DD 主体 (被权力者 B 与施权者 A 同有) 及任一 14DD 构的不可消化剩余, ρ ≠ ∅ 普遍成立, 不限于锁定时。 反缚 (T6) 是施权者 A 侧因锁定动作而起的自缚, 即 A 主体性余项在锁定下被压缩 (P1 §8), 仅在锁定触发时累积。 A 余项 (恒在, 不锁也有) 与 A 反缚 (锁定才有) 分立: 反缚是锁定对 A 余项做的压缩, 不是 A 余项本身。

L4 切开两件极易混为一谈的东西: 余项与反缚。 P1 §8 的 T6 说, 锁定越深, A 主体性余项被压缩越多。 这一句里同时出现了 余项 与 压缩 , 若不切清, T3 (余项恒在) 与 T6 (反缚仅锁定时) 会被读成同一件事。 L4 把两者的关系精确定位: 余项是对象, 反缚是对这个对象的操作。

余项 (T3) 是被权力者侧, 施权者侧, 以及任何构侧的不可消化剩余, ρ ≠ ∅ 普遍成立, 不限于锁定时。 它恒在, 无论权力是否执行锁定。 反缚 (T6) 则是施权者一侧的一个特定动力学: 当 A 对 B 执行向下锁定, 即试图把 B 的 DD 位向下固化, A 必须持续投入 DD 运作空间维持这个结构, A 自己的主体性余项随之被压缩, A 被自己锁定的结构反缚。 反缚只在锁定动作触发时累积。 因此 A 余项 (恒在, 不锁也有) 与 A 反缚 (锁定才有) 是两件事: 反缚是锁定对 A 余项做的压缩, 不是 A 余项本身。 A 不锁定时, A 的余项自由展开为越脚本的施为, 改制, 收手不施等等; A 锁定时, A 的这部分余项被压缩, A 被绑进结构, 极限处与被权力者同被锁定。

装置一侧的余项, 性质与两个主体不同。 14DD 装置没有主体性 ρ; 按 SAE 本体论, 装置不是 13DD 主体, 不具自意识场域 (P2 §V.1)。 但装置作为构, 按 Via Rho 任何构都产生余项, 仍然产生它自己的构层余项 (mediator cost)。 这个构层余项归入 T3 余项族, 与 T6 反缚异质。 须强调: 构层余项不是主体性余项, 它不是装置 感到 了什么, 也不是装置 想要 什么; 它是构在维持自身闭合时产生的接口失灵, 复杂度上升, 维护成本与脆性沉积。 装置不可人格化。

由此可以处理一个关键问题: 经由装置施加的锁定, 反缚落在哪里? 答: 落在调用并执行向下锁定的 13DD 关键节点集身上, 不落在装置上。 装置无 ρ, 不能承反缚。 反缚不需要一个单一的 最终管理者 来承担, 它分布式地锚定在每一个调用该装置并对 B 执行核心锁定动作的 13DD 主体身上: 写代码的工程师, 签发指令的主管, 各自在自身的 13DD 场域内承受一份完整的 T6, 反缚不在人数上稀释 (P2 §V.5 不可稀释性)。 机器只产生物理磨损 (构层脆性), 活人承担拓扑反缚。 一台庞大到再没有任何 13DD 主体调用或管理的装置, 即彻底无头化的装置, 按 M2 不再是社会权力, 它退场为 11DD 或 12DD 的物理现象。 不变量是: 反缚永远在 13DD 主体上, 装置永不承反缚。 装置层余项的完整动力学不在本篇展开, 留 P7 解 (P2 §V.5 已分工); 本篇只确认它归 T3 族, 与反缚异质。

§III 余项的表达结构: 三态与累积

余项定位在本体层之后, 接着是它如何表达。 余项表达的三态框架由 L2 给出, 余项的累积律由 L3 给出。

三态从任一容纳结构的两个特征推出。 任何容纳一个主体的结构, 都有两个特征。 一是吸收阈值: 结构在余项的表达显形之前, 能吸收多少。 任何结构都有一点余地, 能吸收一定幅度的低幅泄出, 而不让这泄出越出容纳, 进入公开。 二是边界: 选项网格的边缘, 即结构允许的位移范围的外延。

这两个特征定义三态。 余项的表达若在阈值之下且在边界之内, 是隐态; 若越过阈值但仍在边界之内, 是显态; 若越出边界, 是越界态。 余项不论处在哪一态, 它的锚点始终是同一个本体 ρ。

容纳结构对关系的每一个节点各有一份: 被权力者的容纳结构是施权者划定的网格, 施权者的容纳结构是结构所予的有限施为网格, 装置的容纳结构是它自身的有限形式。 因此三态是节点中性的, 同样适用于被权力者侧, 施权者侧, 装置侧。

> 判据 L2 余项三态. 任一节点的余项, 依其表达幅度相对于该节点所处结构的吸收阈值 (absorption threshold) 与网格边界, 呈三表达态 (expression state): 隐态 (latent state, 亚阈且网格内), 显态 (manifest state, 越阈且网格内), 越界态 (boundary-crossing state, 越网格边界)。 三态在幅度空间上穷尽覆盖; 但每一态内余项的具体展开方式无限。

L2 含两层。 第一层, 三态作为幅度区间穷尽覆盖表达空间: 任一余项表达, 要么亚阈在界, 要么越阈在界, 要么越界, 三者之外无第四种落点。 第二层, 每一态内余项的具体展开方式无限, 无法穷举。 框架划的是幅度落点, 不是展开种类; 三态是力学拓扑义的结构框架, 不是生物分类学义的物种表。

落且仅落一态 的对象, 是单个余项表达, 不是整个主体, 整个装置或整个关系。 一个节点可以同时承载多个余项表达, 它们分别处于不同的态: 某些仍在隐态, 某些已转显态, 某些已越界。 三态刻画的是单个余项表达的落点, 不是某个主体在某一时刻的唯一总状态。

按落点, T3 在 P1 点名的六类现象各归其位, 作为示例锚点。 隐态收漂移与误读, 显态收抵抗与反噬, 越界态收逃逸与迁移。 这六类是示范框架成立的锚点, 不是余项展开方式的穷举; 它们的具体力学在 §IV 到 §VII 展开。

> 判据 L3 余项累积的单调不可逆. 任一权力操作 (含 P3 操作层承认的制造, 维持, 引导, 撤销四操作) 都会留下, 改形或重新分配不可无损吸收的余项。 所谓单调不可逆, 不是量值只增, 而是余项不可归零, 不可被权力结构无损吸收 (P3 非马尔可夫机, 撤销不是真 undo); 局部表达可衰减, 转移, 改形, 甚至被涵育收入新的关系结构, 但总余项不因权力成功而消失。

L3 接 P3 的状态机本体属性。 P3 已立, 操作层承认的每一次操作 (制造, 维持, 引导, 撤销) 都产生 T3 余项, 而这个状态机是携带不可逆摩擦的非马尔可夫机, 撤销不是真正的 undo, 破镜不能重圆。 L3 把这一点收进余项累积律。

单调不可逆指的是: 余项不可归零, 不可被权力结构无损吸收。 它不指每一个具体余项表达的强度只增不减。 现实与结构上都会出现局部的泄出, 转移, 重编码, 甚至被涵育收入新的关系结构等情况; 某一具体表达完全可以衰减, 转移或改形。 单调的是总余项不因权力的成功而消失, 不是每个局部表达的强度。 这与 Via Rho 的余项纪律一致。

累积的一个直接后果是态的转移: 隐态的低幅泄出持续累积, 一旦越过阈值, 即转为显态或越界态。 这一累积是 M4 衰减相 (P2 §IV.4) 的余项侧引擎。 M4 由 T3 余项不灭, T5 接缝薄化, 主导层迁移压力三因素共同驱动; 其中余项不灭这一项的具体动力学就在此。 本篇收 M4 的余项侧, 另两个因素不在本篇重展。

余项不灭与累积的单调是结构必然; 余项何时越过阈值, 何时转态, 是经验 contingent。 口径与 P1 §7 一致: 是否迁移由结构决定, 何时迁移由多因素决定。 余项的存在不蕴含任何具体结果的必然发生。

涵育性权力成功之后, 位差消解, 一个自然的问题是: 余项是否也随之消失, L1 的绝对性是否因此破产。 不。 涵育性消解不构成余项归零; 它只是解除某一具体的权力网格, 使该关系中的余项不再以受困的形式表达, 而主体作为 13DD 主体的绝对本体 ρ 无损续存。 完整的冷分 (局域关系网格差值与绝对本体 ρ) 在 §IX 给出。

§IV 隐态余项: 漂移与误读

隐态是余项在阈值之下, 网格之内的表达。 它不显形为公开的对抗, 也不越出结构允许的位移范围, 而是以低幅的形式在网格内部泄出。 被权力者一侧的隐态余项, T3 在 P1 点名了两类作为锚点: 漂移与误读。

漂移是行为侧的隐态。 被权力者的行动在网格内缓慢偏滑, 偏离施权者指向的方向, 但每一步都仍落在允许的选项之内。 结构吸收得了这种低幅偏滑, 它不显形, 不触发对抗, 因此长期不被施权者察觉。 但漂移按 L3 累积, 一段时间后, 被权力者实际所处的位置已经离施权者设定的位置有了可观的距离, 而这段距离从未经过一次公开的越界。

误读是登记侧的隐态。 它指被权力者对结构的登记内容偏离了结构本身, 也偏离了施权者意图让它成为的样子。 这里须把误读与一种表面相似的情形分开。 若施权者通过声源错位 (T4) 成功让被权力者相信权力来自某个被声称的来源, 而被权力者百分之百按这个被声称的逻辑行动, 那不是误读, 那是被权力者在施权者的网格内完成了顺向登记 (P3), 是 T4 的得逞, 不是被权力者余项的表达。 真正的误读是另一回事: 被权力者的余项在网格内部搭起了一个影子拓扑 (shadow topology)。 被权力者没有跳出大网格, 表面上仍在顺从, 仍处隐态; 但他对结构的内部锚点已经偏离了施权者划定的安全区。 一个例子: 施权者用 天道 掩盖权力的实际来源 (T4, 施权者一侧对自己来源的位移), 而被权力者把 天道 读成 君无道则可反 (误读, 被权力者一侧余项的拓扑变异)。 这两件事同根, 都是登记内容与结构之间的偏离; 但它们分立。 T4 是施权者一侧对自己来源的位移, 误读是被权力者一侧的余项在顺从表象之下的拓扑变异。 影子拓扑仍处隐态, 因为它尚未越阈, 但它已经是一颗按 L3 累积的种子。

这一累积有它的物理出口, 不停在心理层。 影子拓扑使被权力者的输出始终带着细微的偏差; 施权者或承载它的装置, 为了让被权力者每一次的输出都持续契合网格, 必须投入越来越多的校验: 消解歧义, 下达更密更长的指令, 配置更严的审核。 这份校验成本 (verification overhead) 的上升, 就是误读向结构索取的真实摩擦, 也是它按 L3 累积, 最终汇入衰减相 (M4) 的力学通道。 误读因此不是心理游戏, 而是结构的熵增。

隐态的两类锚点都印证 L1 的本体绝对性。 漂移与误读都不要求被权力者主观上感到不适或准备抵抗。 即使一个被权力者主观上完美顺应, 毫无摩擦, 他的行动仍可能在缓慢漂移, 他对结构的登记仍可能搭着一个他自己都未必言明的影子拓扑; 因为 ρ ≠ ∅ 不依赖他是否察觉。 隐态余项可以以可观察的低幅行为泄出, P3 §V.4 的 道路以目 即是其标准形态: 目光与姿态回避里的信号, 是行为层的泄出, 不是某种被压抑的心理。

§V 显态余项: 抵抗与反噬

显态是余项越过阈值, 但仍在网格之内的表达。 越过阈值意味着表达不再被结构悄悄吸收, 而是显形, 被双方登记; 仍在网格之内意味着关系尚未解体, 被权力者仍在这段关系里。 被权力者一侧的显态余项, T3 点名了两类锚点: 抵抗与反噬。

抵抗是被权力者输出的反向向量。 被权力者不再只是缓慢偏滑, 而是在行为层主动顶着施权者的指向使力。 抵抗显形, 被施权者登记为对抗, 但被权力者仍在关系内, 仍在网格的边界之内。 抵抗在本篇只指这一行为层的反向向量, 不指任何情绪状态; 它是余项越阈后的结构表达, 不是 被压迫者的愤怒 这类心理描述。

反噬是累积的余项向结构放电。 被权力者一侧累积的余项不只表现为针对施权者的反向向量, 它也以抬高结构维持成本的形式作用于结构本身: 结构为了继续容纳这份越阈的余项, 必须投入越来越多的维持, 接缝随之变薄, 脆性随之累积。 这与 P1 §8 所说的余项反弹是同一动力学, 本篇统一用反噬一词。 反噬仍发生在关系之内。

反噬须与反缚分清, 这是显态一节的关键。 反噬是 T3, 是被权力者一侧的余项向结构放电; 反缚是 T6, 是施权者一侧因执行锁定而对自己余项的压缩 (§II)。 当施权者对被权力者执行向下锁定, 两者会同步上升: 被权力者的网格被收窄, 他的余项涨, 向结构放电更猛 (反噬加剧); 施权者为维持这一锁定持续投入, 他自己的余项被压缩越深 (反缚加重)。 但两者异质且异侧, 不能互相吞: 反噬是结构承受的被权力者侧余项, 反缚是施权者承受的自缚, 一个落在被权力者与结构之间, 一个落在施权者自身。

反噬抬高维持成本这一点, 正是 M4 衰减相 (P2 §IV.4) 在余项侧的具体动力学: M4 由 T3 余项不灭, T5 接缝薄化, 主导层迁移压力三因素驱动, 反噬就是其中余项不灭这一项如何作用于结构。 在多主体网络中, 显态余项还有一条放大通道: 当承认网络中的旁观者节点把这段关系从顺向登记翻转为反向登记, 反噬经由网络扩散, 声誉随之塌缩 (P3 §VI.3); 显态是这一塌缩的可见侧。 其网络扩散的完整机制留 P7。

§VI 越界态余项: 逃逸与迁移

越界态是余项越出网格边界的表达。 越出边界意味着关系不再能容纳这份余项: 要么被权力者离开了这段关系, 要么整个权力结构被它所处的人群超越。 被权力者一侧的越界态余项, T3 点名了两类锚点: 逃逸与迁移。 须先说明, 迁移一词在此是结构层的力学描述, 不是对任何具体历史事件的预测, 鼓励或评价 (本系列的防线见 §I)。

逃逸是个体越出网格。 P1 §1 已把离开空间点名为被权力者的余项; 逃逸就是这一离开空间的兑现。 离开空间在结构上永远存在, 这是必然的, 因为 ρ ≠ ∅; 但它在多数情形下并不实际发生, 因为离开的成本可能极高 (P1 §5)。 逃逸是否兑现, 由成本与条件决定, 是经验 contingent; 逃逸作为结构可能性的永存, 则是 L1 的必然。

迁移是结构层越出网格。 它不是某一个被权力者离开, 而是整个权力结构被它所统治的人群超越。 P1 §7 已点名其机制: 权力结构成功运行, 触发人群 DD 比例提升; 提升后的人群对原结构产生应力, 而在凿削路径下, 这一提升主要通过余项反弹间接出现。 迁移就坐在这条余项反弹的线上: 累积的余项越出旧网格能容纳的边界, 关系迁向一个新的主导层。 本篇显出主导层迁移命题在余项侧的引擎, 不重立这一命题本身。

逃逸与迁移构成越界态的两个尺度, 一个在个体, 一个在结构。 两者共享同一个模态: 余项不灭, 保证越界态的结构可能性永远存在; 但越界何时兑现, 由经验条件决定。 余项的存在不蕴含逃逸或迁移的必然发生。

§VII 施权者侧余项与构侧余项

§IV 到 §VI 沿被权力者一侧走完了三态。 三态是节点中性的 (§III), 同一框架同样适用于施权者一侧与装置一侧。 余项不是被权力者独占的; 施权者与承载权力的装置, 各有自己的余项, 各按三态展开。

施权者一侧的余项, 来自 A 作为 13DD 主体的无限可能性与结构所予的有限施为网格之间的差值 (§II)。 它按三态展开。 隐态是 A 的可能性在施为脚本之内的微偏: A 行使着结构授权的权力, 但带着结构未曾编码的个人方式与细微偏离。 显态是这一余项越阈而仍在施为位之内: A 的施为越出了结构的脚本 (越脚本施为), 或 A 对结构本身做出可见的改动 (改制)。 越界态是 A 越出施为位本身: A 弃用自己的施权位, 或 A 的改制大到使旧结构被超越。 这些表达可以取无限种形式; 滥用, 修改, 不作为只是其中三个锚点, 且都须按结构义读, 不带价。 滥用在这里指越出结构脚本的施为, 不是 错用 的道德判断; 修改指改写结构本身; 不作为指 A 收手不施, 因为 A 的可能性本就含有 不施为 这一支, 而结构脚本把 A 编码为施为者, 这一支于是溢出为余项。

施权者侧的余项与施权者的反缚是两件事 (§II, 与 P1 §8 T6 反缚定理同向)。 余项恒在, 不锁也有, 自由展开为上述诸式; 反缚只在 A 执行向下锁定时起, 是锁定对 A 这份余项的压缩。 A 锁定得越深, 他自己的余项被压缩得越多, 能自由展开的越脚本余项就越受抑, A 越被绑进自己锁定的结构, 极限处与被权力者同被锁定。 还须把修改与迁移分开: 修改是 A 主动改写结构, 是施权者侧余项的一种表达; 迁移是结构被被权力者侧应力超越 (§VI), 不是 A 主动为之。 两者方向不同, 不能混为一谈。

这里有一个矢量需要单独点出。 施权者的越脚本施为是 A 的可能性在溢出, 是 A 余项的释放; 但这一溢出动作若方向恰好指向对被权力者的向下固化, 那么在同一个动作里, 释放与锁定就重合了。 一个狱卒私自加重对囚犯的处置, 既是他越出脚本的施为 (释放自己的余项), 也是一次对囚犯的向下锁定 (扣动 T6)。 此时 A 把自己溢出的那份自由度, 当场铸成了套在自己身上的锁链: 他释放的余项被用作锁定动作, 而锁定动作按 T6 反缚于他自身。 因此释放余项并不等于获得自由; 当余项被用作向下锁定时, 它就成了反缚的燃料。 余项的释放与反缚的累积, 在不指向锁定时是分立的两件事 (§II); 在指向锁定的那一刻, 则在同一个动作里同构地发生。

装置一侧的余项是构层余项 (mediator cost), 不是主体性余项 (§II)。 它不是装置感到或想要什么, 而是构在维持自身闭合时的接口失灵, 复杂度上升, 维护成本与脆性沉积。 它也按三态展开: 隐态是脆性的低幅渗出, 在装置的容差之内; 显态是复杂度上升与接口失灵显形为可见的维护负担; 越界态是叙事承载力崩到装置本身瓦解。 装置作为构虽有余项, 却是借位节点: 它之所以是权力装置, 是因为有 13DD 关键节点集调用并管理它; 经由装置施加的锁定, 反缚落在这一关键节点集身上, 不落在装置上 (§II)。 装置层余项的完整动力学, 包括它如何沿这条脆性累积的线走向瓦解, 留 P7 解 (P2 §V.5 已分工)。

至此余项的全域分布走完: 被权力者一侧, 施权者一侧, 装置一侧, 三类节点都携带不可消化的余项 (两个主体为本体的 ρ, 装置为构层余项), 都按同一套三态展开, 而每一态内的具体表达都可以取无限种形式。 权力关系中没有余项豁免的节点。

§VIII 余项在各形态中的动力学

§IV 到 §VII 按表达态与节点切余项 (被权力者侧, 施权者侧, 装置侧的三态)。 余项还可以按 P2 的形态切。 P2 §IX.3 把余项在不同形态中的具体动力学点给了本篇, 这一节兑现这一点。

按表达态切与按形态切是两把独立的刀, 它们交叉。 形态不决定余项是否存在 (ρ ≠ ∅ 对一切形态成立), 形态决定的是余项倾向落在哪一态。 下面按 P2 的几何, 相位, 维度三轴各取一处, 显出形态如何塑造余项的态。 三轴各举的只是示例, 不是形态与表达态的穷举对照。

按几何轴, 隐蔽凿削与公开凿削在余项的态上有系统差别。 隐蔽凿削让被权力者的余项长久停在隐态: 被权力者把凿削结果误读为自然秩序或自由选择, 余项以漂移与误读的低幅形式泄出, 短期的维持成本因此很低。 但隐蔽一旦被打破, 此前按 L3 累积的大量隐态余项会成批越阈转为显态: 被权力者把先前的凿削重新登记为非正当, 反噬骤然显形。 这就是 P2 §III.3 所说 隐蔽并不比公开更稳定 的余项侧机制: 隐蔽压低的是短期成本, 累积的是越阈时的成批转态。 装置凿削则在被权力者侧的余项之上, 再叠一层装置自身的构层余项 (§VII)。

按相位轴, 余项跨建立, 维持, 传递, 衰减四相累积, 其中传递相最值得看。 传递不是简单复制 (P2 §IV.3): 一个权力结构在跨代际, 跨人事的传递中, 新的承载者按自己的 DD 位重新登记这个结构, 余项在传递的瞬间被重新分配, 而不是原样搬运。 衰减相则由余项驱动, 即 M4 在余项侧的引擎 (§V)。

按维度轴, 余项在 13DD, 14DD, 15DD 各有形态, 而算法权力的完美顺应态是检验 L1 的顶点。 算法权力的三层凿削链高度隐蔽 (P2 §VI), 在其最深处, 被权力者完美内化了算法的定向, 主观上没有任何摩擦或反抗意识。 此时一个直接的追问是: 若被权力者心理上完全顺应, 余项从何而来, L1 是否在这里失效。 答案是 L1 在这里得到最强的印证, 而非失效。 余项不是被权力者心理上的不适, 它是被权力者作为 13DD 主体的无限可能性, 与算法提供的有限选项网格之间的本体层拓扑差值。 这一差值不依赖被权力者是否察觉。 即使被权力者完美顺应, 这一差值依然存在。 只要装置试图维持完美顺应这一外观, 它就必须持续耗费算力, 能源与维护成本, 去抹平由这一差值产生的微小不一致。 装置侧脆性的累积 (§VII), 本质上就是这一维持动作的成本累积; 当这一成本超过装置可承载的阈值, 衰减相显化 (M4)。 因此 M4 在算法权力下的驱动不依赖被权力者的心理状态, 而依赖这一本体差值的持续存在。 在被权力者主观毫无察觉的极限情形下, ρ ≠ ∅ 仍然成立, 余项的全部力学仍然完整。 这是 L1 的本体绝对性, L3 的累积, 以及余项去心理学化三者同时达到最强的一处。

§IX 系列接口

本篇沿 T3 展开余项的限度功能, 不展开方向, 不展开瓦解。 方向与瓦解分别留给后续各篇, 此处给出接口。

与 P5 育, P6 削的接口在方向上。 凿削性与涵育性的判别不由本篇给出, 由 T6 锁定动作是否触发给出。 余项在两个方向下的取向不同: 凿削路径下, 累积的余项建向反噬, 逃逸与迁移, 作用于结构; 涵育路径下, 累积的余项被收入关系朝向位差自身消除, P2 §IV.4 把这一自我消解记为涵育成功的标志。 须强调, 这里的成功是方向的属性, 由 T6 判别, 不是余项本身的属性; 余项本身仍然无价。 施权者一侧的余项同样按方向分岔: 凿削下 A 的余项被锁定压缩 (反缚), 而 A 的越脚本余项照常泄出; 涵育下 A 不锁定, A 的余项不被压缩, 取向朝向释放被权力者。 两侧完整的对偶动力学留 P5 与 P6。

涵育成功这一情形需要在此把 L1 守到底。 涵育性权力成功时, 位差消解, 关系解体; 一个问题随之而来: 余项是否也随之归零。 答案是不。 关键在于, 涵育做的根本不是减少余项, 而是把被权力者托往更高的 DD 层级, 并解除施权者在这段关系中加给他的那个特定网格。 须把两件事分开。 一件是那个局域的关系网格: 施权者在这段关系中为被权力者划定的有限选项网格。 涵育成功时, 施权者把这个网格解除了; 被权力者不再被这个特定网格囚禁, 这个局域的受困关系随之失去定义, 而不是某个差值被算到了零。 另一件是绝对本体 ρ (absolute ρ): 被权力者作为 13DD 主体的可能性与任何有限结构之间的余项, 它由 L1 给出, ρ ≠ ∅ 普遍成立。 涵育不治愈, 不填满, 也不抹除这个绝对本体 ρ。

升入更高层级的主体, 其余项总量提升。 在人群层面, 这一提升有两个同向的去向。 一是相互可登记, 相互维持的重合 (overlap) 增大, 即绝对共识增多。 二是个体之间的余项差异增大。 两者同向, 而差异增长更强: 每个主体的余项总量增幅大于落入绝对共识的那部分增幅, 多出的部分沉淀为差异。 多样化因此不是绝对共识的减项, 而是总量增幅超出共识增幅之后的余额, 且这一余额随升级放大。 由此, 哪怕相互维持持续加深, 余项差异不但不被吸收, 反而扩大; ρ ≠ ∅ 在升级方向上, 在人群层面同样全天候成立, 而且更强。 涵育移动的是 DD 层级, 它解除的是那个把余项囚禁起来的局域网格, 余项本身随主体升入更高层级继续存在, 在人群层面不减反增。 归于消解的只是局域的囚禁网格, 不是余项。 升级主体展现出的对他者之 不得不 与涵育意愿, 是涵育性权力的方向动力学, 其完整展开留 P5 育。

与 P7 解的接口在瓦解。 装置层余项的完整动力学, 即构层余项如何沿脆性累积的线走向装置瓦解, 留 P7 (P2 §V.5)。 衰减相的余项侧引擎 (§V), 与显态反噬经由承认网络的扩散 (P3 §VI.3 的旁观者承认翻转), 一起为 P7 的抗与瓦解提供余项侧的基础。

与 M4 的接口在衰减。 本篇收 M4 的余项侧, 即 T3 余项不灭这一因素的具体动力学; M4 的另两个因素, T5 接缝薄化与主导层迁移压力, 不在本篇重展。 主导层迁移命题在 §VI 显出其余项侧的引擎, 同样不重立。

§X 收束

本篇沿 T3 权力余项定理把余项在权力领域的完整动力学展开, 不立新 T 定理。 P1 立的概念机器在余项这一维度上获得了完整的展开。

本篇的成果可以收束为四条。 一, 余项是 13DD 主体无限可能性与其所处有限选项网格之间的本体层拓扑差值, ρ ≠ ∅ 普遍成立, 对关系中任一主体对称, 不依赖主观察觉 (L1)。 二, 余项的表达按其幅度相对于结构吸收阈值与网格边界, 呈隐态, 显态, 越界态三态; 三态是表达幅度的结构框架, 单个余项表达落且仅落一态, 而每一态内的展开方式无限 (L2)。 三, 余项的累积单调不可逆, 即余项不可归零, 不可被权力无损吸收, 而非每个表达的强度只增; 这一累积是衰减相在余项侧的引擎 (L3)。 四, 余项与反缚分属两侧: 余项是被权力者与施权者两主体及任一构的不可消化剩余, 反缚是施权者因锁定而对自己余项的压缩; 经由装置的锁定, 反缚落在调用并管理装置的关键节点集身上, 装置不承反缚 (L4)。

由此, 余项即权力的结构性限度, 而这一限度是全域的。 被权力者一侧, 施权者一侧, 装置一侧, 三类节点都携带不可消化的余项, 权力关系中没有余项豁免的位置。 权力的限度不来自外部约束, 不来自被权力者的心理不满, 也不来自道德制裁; 它来自权力关系内部每一个节点都无法消除的余项。

余项无价, 余项的展开方式无限。 凿削性与涵育性的方向判别不由本篇给出, 由 T6 锁定动作是否触发给出。 凿削性权力的完整动力学留 P6 削, 涵育性权力的完整动力学留 P5 育, 抗与瓦解留 P7 解。 本篇的工作完成, P5 育接续涵育性权力的方向动力学。
