Abstract

The dark-sector installment of this series left two action-level open items: fixing the khronon–$C$ mixing coefficient $g$, and identifying the route by which the signed vacuum ledger $\Lambda_1+\Lambda_2=0$ enters the equations that matter sees. This paper recognizes both as facets of a single realization problem — how the two-sided dual-4DD structure is to enter a single-metric action — and measures precisely where that problem currently stands. The negative half is a theorem: in a healthy local bulk (single metric, time-orientable, positive-definite propagating sector, exact shift symmetry) a signed ledger cannot be realized as the difference of two independent canonical stress tensors, and $g$ is shown to be constructively underdetermined — the bulk does not generate it, the mixing operator does not drive it, symmetry fixes only its parity, and a bulk operator is symmetry-permitted but its coefficient is not pinned. The constructive half is a candidate: taking as geometric raw material the antipodality defect already published upstream (the two time axes are anti-aligned but not exactly $180^\circ$, the offset being first-order-linear in the frequency asymmetry), an internal top-form wedge furnishes a bounded-below, ghost-free existence witness that yields, on the non-trivial branch, a non-negative vacuum magnitude entering at $O(|\varepsilon|)$, whose zero is exactly the geometric content of the ledger axiom. The measurement is the paper's real result: observation fixes only the single combination $\mathcal N^2\lambda k=8$ — the flux normalization $\mathcal N$, the geometric tilt coefficient $k$, and the Wilson readout $\lambda$ are none of them independently derived, and their underdeterminacies are separately located as identifiable open problems. As a by-product, the HP2 hierarchy is established: intrinsic $w_{\rm int}\ge-1$ remains a hard bound within the positive-sign, ghost-free DBI baseline, while an effective phantom crossing is a provably underdetermined bulk-Wilson branch, adjudicated by a nested-hypothesis structure. All claims are tagged across four tiers — structural, posterior, motivated, open.

§1 Introduction: two open items, one realization problem

The dark-sector installment of this series unified, within a single action, dark matter (the khronon), dark energy and MOND (the $C$ field), and the cosmological constant (a 4-form sector); it closed by recording, honestly, two action-level open items. The first is the coefficient of the khronon–$C$ mixing operator $g\,\theta Q$ — the carrier of the apparent phantom crossing in the effective equation of state, whose size and sign were left unfixed. The second is the action-level route by which the signed vacuum ledger $\Lambda_1+\Lambda_2=0$, together with the observed value $\Lambda_{\rm obs}=2(\omega_2^2-\omega_1^2)/c^2$, reaches the equations matter obeys — open since the two-frame installment. This paper begins from a single recognition: these two items belong to the same realization class — how the two-sided dual-4DD structure enters a single-metric action. The strength of that statement must be qualified at once: it is the same class, not the same operator or the same coefficient. $g\theta Q$ is a derivative-order mixing and the signed ledger is a zero-derivative vacuum source; they share an obstruction to realization, but a no-go for one does not hand over the number for the other.

State the realization problem precisely. We seek a mathematical structure that lets a single-manifold, single-metric action satisfy simultaneously: (L) signed ledger — the vacuum sector's net contribution to the observed equations is proportional to $\omega_2^2-\omega_1^2$ (the difference, not the sum); (V) numerical boundary condition — the published relation $\Lambda_{\rm obs}=2(\omega_2^2-\omega_1^2)/c^2$ matches the Planck-derived value to within about $5\%$ (this $5\%$ is the mismatch between the SAE formula and the Planck-derived value, not Planck's observational uncertainty, which is far finer), the flux dictionary being revisable but subject to registration; (C) locality of $C$ — $C=\tau_1-\tau_2$ remains a local dynamical field on this spacetime, the field on which the dark-energy and MOND sectors are built; (G) ghost-freedom — both sectors share the metric, so any propagating field carrying wrong-sign kinetics is, through its gravitational coupling, a death sentence; (T) no closed causal loops — a single metric, with no bimetric structure or looped time topology; (S) exact shift — $C\to C+C_0$ is an exact symmetry of the action. Why this set is not decoration is shown by one load-bearing number: with flux normalization $f_i=\mathcal N M_P\omega_i/c$, the ratio of the vacuum-energy sum of two independent positive-sign 4-forms to the observed value is $\mathcal N^2(1+\varepsilon^2)/(8\varepsilon)$ — about $20$ under the original dictionary of the cosmological-constant installment ($\mathcal N=\sqrt2$), about $40$ for $\mathcal N=2$. Unless the ledger nearly cancels, the model is off by more than an order of magnitude at leading order.

The paper's results, stated at their true strength, are as follows. We prove that the signed dual-4DD vacuum ledger cannot be realized as the difference of two independent canonical stress tensors in a healthy single-metric bulk (the no-go group, §4); and that $g$ is provably constructively underdetermined within the locked baseline and operator classification — the bulk does not produce it, the mixing operator does not drive the background, symmetry fixes only its parity, and a bulk $\theta Q$ is symmetry-permitted but its coefficient is not pinned (§3). In the surviving gap that the no-go group's elimination points to, an internal top-form wedge supplies a bounded-below local existence witness: it naturally produces, on the non-trivial branch, a positive vacuum magnitude of order $O(|\varepsilon|)$, but its flux normalization $\mathcal N$, geometric tilt coefficient $k$, and Wilson readout $\lambda$ are constrained only by the single target relation $$\mathcal N^2\lambda k=8,$$ none of the three being independently derived (§5–§6). Accordingly, the minimal consistent architecture places the physical magnitude at a transition-surface imprint and the cross-cycle sign in an orientation ledger, while leaving the complete matching law explicitly to the next layer (§7–§8). The by-product is the nested HP2 hierarchy: intrinsic $w_{\rm int}\ge-1$ remains a hard bound, the effective crossing is a provably underdetermined bulk-Wilson branch, and the observational adjudication structure is given in §9.

What the paper does not do is listed with equal candor: it does not complete the transition-surface matching functional; it does not compute model-specific nucleation rates (only a thin-wall benchmark partition, with numbers in Appendix D); it does not touch the $a_0$ sector of the third installment (the HP1 adjudication discipline stands); it does not activate the effective-crossing branch; and it does not derive a numerical value for any one of $\mathcal N$, $\lambda$, $k$. Order of exposition: §2 sets the contract and the assumption box; §3 treats $\theta Q$; §4 proves the no-go group; §5 supplies the tilt raw material; §6 builds the wedge witness; §7 handles pairing and the transition surface; §8 synthesizes the architecture and registers revisions to earlier installments; §9 gives the nested falsifiable hypotheses; §10 closes and lists the keys to the next layer.

§2 Epistemic contract, baseline, and two ledgers

Tiers and accounting. This paper carries forward the four-tier tagging established in the zeroth installment — 〔structural〕 forced by the framework and falsifiable; 〔posterior〕 dependent on observation or parameters; 〔motivated〕 a well-motivated preference; 〔open〕 not yet adjudicated — and adds to every coefficient that appears a three-way accounting: prior-derived (given by framework structure), construction input (a modeling choice, explicitly flagged), posterior boundary (taken from observation). Any place where a "construction input" masquerades as "prior-derived" is a self-identified, checkable error of this paper.

Locked posterior inputs. Breathing periods $T_1=20$ Gyr, $T_2\approx19.5$ Gyr; frequency asymmetry $\varepsilon=(\omega_2-\omega_1)/(\omega_2+\omega_1)\approx0.0127$. Every "$O(\varepsilon)$" in this paper is an order in this parameter.

Baseline declaration. Throughout, the exact-shift baseline: a single metric; matter minimally coupled, $S_m[g_{\mu\nu}]$; $C$ entering the action only through derivatives ($Q$, $\nabla C$), its potential constant by the symmetry. The conformal machinery of the two-frame installment ($\mathscr A(C)$, $F(C)R$) migrates wholesale to a shift-breaking branch, which this paper neither uses nor refutes; that migration is registered as one of the revisions in §8.

Common assumption box. Every no-go proposition here shares one domain, stated once: four dimensions; Lorentzian signature; a single metric; a time-orientable bulk; a positive-definite propagating sector (ghost-free); a local action; exact shift symmetry for $C$. Wherever analyticity is used in addition (Theorem 3.3, the parity trichotomy of §6.4) it is flagged in place. The "escape routes outside the domain" listed after each proposition are the concrete ways of leaving this box.

Two ledgers. Two quantities run through the paper — one a definition, one a definition plus a candidate law — and must be kept apart; and two distinct indices must first be separated: the face index ($\pm$, the two faces of the dual pair within one cycle) and the cycle index ($n\to n+1$, adjacent time cycles). Collapsing them into a single $n+1$ was an error. Definition: $\rho_{\rm phys}\ge0$ is the physical vacuum energy entering the Einstein equation each cycle; the orientation ledger, written by face index, is $$\mathscr L_{n,+}=+\rho_n,\qquad \mathscr L_{n,-}=-\rho_n,\qquad \boxed{\mathscr L_{n,+}+\mathscr L_{n,-}=0},$$ the zero-sum of the two faces of the dual pair in cycle $n$ — this, and only this, is the identity of $\Lambda_1+\Lambda_2=0$; it concerns the two faces of one cycle and involves no adjacent cycle. The candidate matching law (not a definition; awaiting a complete boundary functional) governs the cycle index instead: $$\rho_{n+1}=\mathcal M(\rho_n,\Xi_{1,n},\Xi_{2,n}),$$ permitting the magnitude to drift cycle by cycle (the spiral). Thus "each side observes a positive cosmological constant" is a property of $\rho_{\rm phys}\ge0$; "dual-pair zero-sum" is a face-level ledger; "spiral drift" is the $n\to n+1$ evolution — the three live at different indices and no longer conflict (collapsing "the other-side label" and "the next-cycle label" into one $n+1$ would wrongly demand $\rho_{n+1}=\rho_n$). Throughout, the sign of a dual pair means the face-index $\pm$ (orientation label $\chi_{n,s}=s$, with no bare $\chi_n$ lacking a face index); the magnitude means $\rho_{\rm phys}$ — conflating the two is this paper's second self-forbidden error.

§3 The origin of θQ and the HP2 hierarchy

Fix the objects first. In this paper's baseline (§2) the aether is the breathing-foliation unit normal $A_\mu=-\partial_\mu\tau_b/\sqrt{-(\partial\tau_b)^2}$; write $$\theta\equiv\nabla_\mu A^\mu,\qquad Q\equiv A^\mu\partial_\mu C,$$ so that on FLRW $\theta=3H$, $Q=\dot C$. The operator $g\,\theta Q$ is the leading, shift-respecting, woven-type mixing between the khronon and $C$; the dark-sector installment identified it as the carrier by which the effective equation of state can present an apparent phantom crossing, and listed the coefficient $g$ as a free, $g\propto\varepsilon$–constrained Wilson branch. This section upgrades that EFT classification into three action-level theorems and one constructive-underdetermination proposition: where $g$ cannot come from, what constrains its functional form, and why the existing structure is insufficient to fix it.

Theorem 3.1 (no bulk source). A two-DBI bulk containing only first derivatives does not generate a $\theta Q$ operator carrying $\nabla A$. Assumptions: the baseline two-clock action $-\sum_i\mathcal T_i\sqrt{1+(\partial\tau_i)^2}$; single metric; no interface term. Proof sketch: at quadratic order the sum/difference variables give $$\mathcal T_1(\partial\tau_1)^2+\mathcal T_2(\partial\tau_2)^2=(\mathcal T_1{+}\mathcal T_2)\Big[(\partial\tau_b)^2+\tfrac14(\partial C)^2\Big]+(\mathcal T_1{-}\mathcal T_2)\,\partial\tau_b\!\cdot\!\partial C,$$ the cross term being of the pure-first-derivative $Y\equiv\partial\tau_b\!\cdot\!\partial C$ type, diagonalizable at quadratic order by a field redefinition (note: diagonalization changes the physical definition of the aether, so the conclusion here is confined to "the quadratic kinetic matrix is diagonalizable," not "the mixing is physically void"). The full DBI $-\mathcal T_1\sqrt{U+Y}-\mathcal T_2\sqrt{U-Y}$ (with $U=1+X_b+\tfrac14X_C$) retains $Y^2$ and, for unequal tensions, the odd-in-$Y$ nonlinear mixing — but throughout only first derivatives; $\theta$ contains second derivatives of $\tau_b$ and has, structurally, nowhere to sit. The $3H$ appearing in the homogeneous field equation (via $\tfrac{d}{dt}(a^3P_Q)=0$) is the Hubble friction supplied by the covariant divergence, not an action-level $g\theta Q$ operator. Escape routes outside the domain: an interface/boundary term (on which the existence part of Proposition 3.4 rests); higher-derivative bulk operators beyond minimal DBI; matter couplings that break exact shift (outside the baseline). 〔structural〕

Theorem 3.2 (the $J\!\cdot\!\nabla C$ operator has no background source). On an exact FLRW background the aether–scalar mixing operator $J^\mu\nabla_\mu C$ (with $J^\nu\equiv A^\mu\nabla_\mu A^\nu$) vanishes pointwise; its integration-by-parts relation with $\theta Q$ supplies no independent background-level mixing source. Assumptions: exact FLRW; an action containing $J^\mu\nabla_\mu C$ (as in the Skordis–Złośnik–type vector–scalar mixing; note that theory is a complete vector–scalar–metric system, and this theorem addresses only that one mixing operator, not the remaining $Q$-dependent functions). Proof sketch: the identity $$\int\!\sqrt{-g}\;\theta Q=-\int\!\sqrt{-g}\Big[J^\nu\nabla_\nu C+A^\mu A^\nu\nabla_\mu\nabla_\nu C\Big]$$ binds $\theta Q$ to its companion normal Hessian; on FLRW $J^\mu=0$, $\theta Q=3H\dot C$, the Hessian $=\ddot C$, and $\int dt\,a^3(3H\dot C+\ddot C)=[a^3\dot C]_{\rm bdry}$ is a pure boundary term. Reading off, by parts, a lone "background coefficient of $\theta Q$" is operator-basis bookkeeping, not physics; the mixing operator does not by itself drive a background-level effective crossing. Escape routes: the perturbative level and inhomogeneous backgrounds — where a parameter-dependent $J\!\cdot\!\nabla C$ mixing survives, which is exactly the source of the §9 perturbation criterion; or an independently added bulk $\theta Q$ operator at an interface — whose coefficient is then governed by Theorem 3.3 and Proposition 3.4. 〔structural〕

Theorem 3.3 (parity and analyticity). If the action is invariant under face exchange $1\leftrightarrow2$, if $\varepsilon$ is the unique exchange-odd spurion, and if $g$ is analytic near $\varepsilon=0$, then $$g(\varepsilon)=g_1\varepsilon+g_3\varepsilon^3+\cdots.$$ Assumptions: the three above, each load-bearing. Proof sketch: under exchange $\tau_b\to\tau_b$, $C\to-C$, so $A,\theta$ are even, $Q$ odd, $\theta Q$ odd; invariance forces $g(-\varepsilon)=-g(\varepsilon)$; an analytic odd function contains only odd powers. Escape routes outside the domain: dropping analyticity leaves only "$g$ is odd" — $\operatorname{sgn}\varepsilon$, $\varepsilon|\varepsilon|$, $\varepsilon\log|\varepsilon|$ and other non-analytic odd structures are then permitted and the linear leading term is not forced ($|\varepsilon|$ is even and can never by itself be $g$); dropping "unique odd spurion" lets $g$ be written as another exchange-odd object times an even function — the $\chi W$ route in the §6.4 parity trichotomy is one instance of exactly this escape, the two being two faces of the same structural fact. 〔structural〕

Proposition 3.4 (symmetry-permission and constructive underdetermination). A bulk Wilson operator $g_{\rm df}(\varepsilon)\,\theta Q$, respecting exact shift and exchange covariance, is symmetry-permitted — $g_{\rm df}$ satisfies Theorem 3.3 — but within this paper's locked baseline and operator classification its coefficient is not fixed by structure, and the underdeterminacy is constructive. Assumptions: the baseline; some construction relating the operator to the vacuum sector, with its zero-derivative (vacuum) normalization already fixed. On type: what is asserted here is a four-dimensional bulk interaction $\int_M\sqrt{-g}\,g_{\rm df}(\varepsilon)\theta Q$ — it can persistently affect late-time $w_{\rm eff}(z)$, and is the object of the §9 interacting branch; its microscopic (interface or other) origin is itself open. This is a different type of object from the codimension-one temporal junction of §7–§8 (Proposition 4.1), which produces only a boundary matching or an instantaneous impulse and cannot serve as a bulk operator persistently driving the background; the two are not conflated. Proof sketch (permission + underdetermination): an exchange-odd scalar built from the expansion and normal derivative of the phase flows reduces, in the homogeneous co-directed limit, to the $\theta Q$ type with coefficient proportional to $\varepsilon$ — so the operator is not symmetry-forbidden. Record the dimensions once: under this paper's two-clock/DBI normalization, $[\theta]=M$ and $Q/c$ is dimensionless (so $Q$ is dimensionless at $c=1$), giving $[\theta Q]=M$ and Wilson coefficient $[g_{\rm df}]=M^3$ (consistent with the candidate scale $g_{\rm df}\sim M_P^2\bar\omega\,\varepsilon$). Its size is constructively underdetermined: let the single microscopic function generating it be $\mathscr F$ with dimensionless argument $X\equiv\theta Q/\mu_\Sigma$ ($[\mu_\Sigma]=M$); the zero-derivative gate fixes $\mathscr F(0)$, but the one-parameter family $\mathscr F_\beta(X)=1+\beta X$ ($\beta$ a dimensionless Wilson coefficient) passes the same gate for any $\beta$ while giving different $\mathscr F'(0)$ — $$\mathscr F(0)\ \text{fixed}\;\nRightarrow\;\mathscr F'(0)\ \text{fixed}.$$ In EFT the coefficients of operators at different derivative order are in principle independent; a vacuum normalization cannot lock a derivative coefficient. Escape route: a complete microscopic action that locks the zero-derivative order and the derivative order in a single expansion (supplying action, measure, coefficient dimension, and variation) — which §4's bookkeeping theorem (Proposition 4.3), together with the display above, shows the natural candidate (a vacuum factor used as a calibration gate) cannot deliver; or leaving the baseline. 〔structural (permission + underdetermination); complete microscopic construction: open〕

The HP2 hierarchy is thereby established. The first layer is untouched: the baseline's intrinsic equation of state satisfies $w_{\rm int}\ge-1$ — this follows from the non-ghost condition of positive-sign DBI ($\rho+p=2XP_X\ge0$), has nothing to do with anything in this section, and is a hard bound within the positive-sign, ghost-free DBI-$C$ baseline (this strength-qualified statement avoids being read as unconditional over every conceivable SAE completion). The second layer is upgraded: the bulk Wilson operator $g_{\rm df}\theta Q$ needed for an effective crossing, formerly an "uncomputed free branch" in the dark-sector installment, is turned by this section's theorems and proposition into a "branch provably underdetermined within the existing structure" — the minimal DBI bulk does not produce it (Theorem 3.1), the $J\!\cdot\!\nabla C$ operator does not drive a background crossing (Theorem 3.2), symmetry fixes only its parity (Theorem 3.3), and symmetry permits a bulk $\theta Q$ but does not lock its coefficient (Proposition 3.4). The framework neither predicts a crossing nor forbids one; the observational adjudication structure of this hierarchy — which data adjudicate the first layer, which combination the second — is given by the nested hypotheses of §9. 〔structural (classification); observational correspondence in §9〕

§4 The no-go group for a signed ledger

This section proves the paper's central negative result: within the healthy local bulk delimited by the §2 assumption box — four-dimensional, Lorentzian, single-metric, time-orientable, positive-definite propagating sector, local action, exact shift symmetry for $C$ — the signed dual-4DD vacuum ledger $\Lambda_1+\Lambda_2=0$ cannot be realized by two co-located sectors each coupling to the shared metric through a standard stress tensor. We split the result into seven propositions (the chain is Figure 1). Each is stated in one template: proposition, assumptions, proof sketch, escape route outside the domain. Listing the escape routes explicitly is both honesty and signposting: the constructive road taken in §6–§8 is selected from these escapes, not a way around the propositions.

Proposition 4.1 (codimension mismatch). A four-dimensional effective vacuum density and a three-dimensional junction tension are objects of different type; reading an effective "interface" term written $\int d^4x\sqrt{-g}\,\mathcal L_\Sigma$ directly as a codimension-one microscopic junction produces both a dimensional and a support mismatch. Assumptions: four-dimensional spacetime; the junction a codimension-one hypersurface carrying action through the induced volume element $\int d^3\xi\sqrt{|h|}$. Proof sketch: the former has density dimension $M^4$, the latter $M^3$, so the conversion must introduce a thickness scale $\ell_\Sigma$ — a new free microscopic scale. More to the point is the support: a codimension-one source enters the Einstein equations through the Israel junction condition (Israel 1966), its stress supported on $\delta_\Sigma$; a spacelike single transition surface gives the cosmological equations an instantaneous impulse, a timelike domain wall gives a local wall and generically breaks FRW homogeneity — neither is a homogeneous $-\rho\,g_{\mu\nu}$ Friedmann source. Escape routes: keep the four-dimensional effective-density reading (but its microscopic origin is then suspended); smear a wall network into a dense foliation; or lift the dimension so the four-dimensional universe is itself a brane. All three require structure beyond the assumption box. 〔structural〕

Proposition 4.2 (sign mismatch). In a single metric, the vacuum energies of two canonical 4-forms add rather than subtract; a spacetime orientation reversal cannot flip that sign. Assumptions: single metric; canonical kinetics $-\frac{1}{2\cdot4!}\sum_{ij}K_{ij}F_i^{\mu\nu\rho\sigma}F_{j\,\mu\nu\rho\sigma}$ with positive-definite kinetic matrix $K$; both fluxes co-located in the same bulk. Proof sketch: in four dimensions $F_i=f_i\,\mathrm{vol}_4$ and the vacuum energy is $\rho=\tfrac12 f^{\mathsf T}Kf\ge0$. Orientation reversal realizes at most $f_i\mapsto-f_i$, and $\rho(-f)=\rho(f)$. Adding a cross term does not help: as long as $K$ is positive-definite, an orthogonal diagonalization still gives a sum of positive squares. To obtain a $f_2^2-f_1^2$ difference, $K$ must carry at least one negative eigenvalue, or a physical orientation-odd spurion must be introduced. Escape routes: non-positive-definite kinetics (for the special status of the top form see the remark closing this section); a physical odd spurion; a bimetric structure (excluded by this series' anti-causal-loop discipline); or moving the sign out of the bulk to a boundary or non-local structure. 〔structural〕

Proposition 4.3 (bookkeeping theorem). A boundary-term coefficient appearing in the on-shell action of a 4-form cannot be read directly as a physical factor in the stress tensor. Assumptions: the 4-form field varied well-posedly in a fixed-flux ensemble, requiring a Bousso–Polchinski boundary term (Bousso & Polchinski 2000). Proof sketch: that boundary term's on-shell value is exactly minus twice the volume $F^2$ term; its job is to remove the surface mismatch between the on-shell action and the field equations (the difference between fixed-flux and fixed-potential ensembles), not to alter the physical vacuum energy. The latter must be read through $T_{\mu\nu}=-\frac{2}{\sqrt{-g}}\frac{\delta S}{\delta g^{\mu\nu}}$, whose value remains of the $+f^2/2$ type. Escape route: none. This is a methodological theorem: any derivation that takes an on-shell action value straight for an energy must redo it through the metric variation. 〔structural〕

Proposition 4.4 (causal-type mismatch). A Brown–Teitelboim membrane used for flux discharge and a khronon leaf satisfying $K_\Sigma=\theta$ cannot be the same hypersurface. Assumptions: Lorentzian signature; the discharge membrane a timelike worldvolume (spacelike normal, Brown & Teitelboim 1988); the khronon leaf $\tau_b=\mathrm{const}$ spacelike (timelike normal $A^\mu$). Proof sketch: $K_\Sigma=\theta$ requires $n^\mu=A^\mu$; but a single normal cannot be both spacelike and timelike. Escape routes: an explicit projection factor ($A\!\cdot\!n$, etc.) — at the cost of a new independent coefficient; or replacing the discharge object with a spacelike temporal matching surface (the temporal-junction path of §7, which is realized in essence by leaving the smoothness of the Lorentzian manifold) — at the cost that it is no longer a standard, nucleable dynamical membrane. 〔structural〕

Proposition 4.5 (the quadratic wall). Orientation reversal cannot flip any positive-definite quadratic vacuum energy; a signed ledger can enter only through orientation-odd objects. Assumptions: local field theory; energy a quadratic form in the fields with positive-definite kinetic structure. Proof sketch: by direct generalization of the argument of Proposition 4.2 to a general quadratic form. In the boson vacuum sector considered here — no fundamental fermions, no torsion, up to two derivatives — the directly relevant orientation-odd candidates are enumerable: $\int_M F_4$, $\int_\Sigma B_3$ (a WZ term), a linear $\star F$ (a pseudoscalar), and $d\tau\wedge B$–type $\varepsilon$-contractions — all linear or topological, none a quadratic energy capable of giving a homogeneous vacuum difference. (Note: higher characteristic densities such as $R\wedge R$ and Euler/Pontryagin classes, though also orientation-odd, are topological/boundary terms and likewise supply no homogeneous $-\rho\,g_{\mu\nu}$ vacuum source, so they do not change this conclusion; the list here is "directly relevant," not a claim of exhaustiveness over every four-dimensional theory.) Escape route: the channel list of Proposition 4.6. 〔structural〕

Proposition 4.6 (confinement theorem). In a bulk with positive-definite propagating sector, the ledger sign cannot be carried by the positivity or negativity of any propagating-mode Hamiltonian; its carrier must live in non-propagating objects, discrete/superselection sectors, boundary, or non-local constraints. Assumptions: the full §2 assumption box; both sectors sharing the metric. Proof sketch: by Proposition 4.5 the sign must enter through an orientation-odd object; and any propagating field carrying wrong-sign kinetics overall becomes, through its gravitational coupling to the shared metric, a ghost, violating the box's positive-propagation clause. Non-propagating top-forms (zero local degrees of freedom), discrete/superselection sectors, boundary data, and non-local constraints are not so bound. Two narrowing faces must be stated precisely. First, a UV completion may contain propagating modes: a healthy propagating field can perfectly well supply an exchange-odd spurion in its discrete vacuum/superselection sector — what is excluded is not "the theory contains propagating modes" but "the ledger sign is carried by the wrong sign of a propagating-mode Hamiltonian"; the sign cannot be carried by a propagating Hamiltonian, but its UV completion may contain propagating modes. Second, a healthy, exchange-even coupling (e.g. a positive-definite kinetic mixing) can still transmit perturbations between the two sectors — what is confined is only the sign bit, not all cross-sector communication. Escape route: the four carrier classes here are the channel list; §6 and §8 take the "non-propagating top-form" and "boundary" channels respectively. 〔structural〕

Proposition 4.7 (covering obstruction). If $C$ is taken as an odd section of a non-trivial time-orientation line bundle $L_t$, and exact shift requires adding a globally non-zero constant section to $C$, then the connected time-orientation double cover is incompatible with exact shift; a global khronon also trivializes the bundle. Assumptions: connected cover (equivalently, a non-time-orientable base); $C\in\Gamma(L_t)$; the shift an additive global constant. Proof sketch: on a non-trivial $\mathbb Z_2$ line bundle there is no globally parallel non-zero constant section — if there were, it would trivialize the bundle; and the globally timelike vector field required by the khronometric structure is time-orientability. Escape routes: a trivial cover (which organizes exchange parity cleanly but does not solve the ledger); or confining the non-triviality to the transition core — precisely where the causal structure itself dissolves — which is the boundary-matching reading of §7–§8, but that is a cross-boundary matching rule, not a bulk realization on a single smooth Lorentzian manifold. 〔structural〕

Remark (the special status of a wrong-sign top-form). In four dimensions the local propagating degrees of freedom of a 3-form gauge field are $\binom{D-2}{p}=\binom{2}{3}=0$; so assigning wrong-sign kinetics to one 4-form produces no perturbative ghost — this keeps the "non-positive-definite kinetics" escape of Proposition 4.2 from being immediately fatal as it usually would be. But the cost changes location: the flux energy of the wrong-sign sector is unbounded below, and any membrane allowed to change the flux opens a per-jump equal-step downward channel with no intrinsic stopper at fixed charge and tension. So the status of a wrong-sign top-form is: formally writable, healthy microscopic completion absent. This paper does not build on it, and cites its formal bookkeeping only in the comparison of §8. 〔structural〕

Net verdict. Combining Propositions 4.1–4.7: two independent, canonical, co-located 4-form sectors, each coupling to the shared metric through a standard stress tensor, cannot realize a signed difference ledger by time-orientation reversal. Every qualifier in that verdict is deliberate. It does not cover: two marked components of a single internal multiplet co-located in a near-antiparallel way (they do not each carry an independent standard stress tensor, but are components of a single functional) — the constructive part of §6 is built on exactly this un-sealed gap; nor does it cover boundary and non-local structures — the architectural synthesis of §8 is built there. The value of a no-go group is half in what it kills, half in the survivor it points to by elimination.

§5 Tilt: the antipodality defect and geometric underdetermination

Having evicted the signed ledger from the healthy bulk in §4, the constructive road of §6 needs one piece of geometric raw material: the two sectors' internal directions are near antiparallel, not exactly antiparallel. §6 The wedge: a minimal EFT candidate and its underdetermination

Having sealed off "the difference of two independent stress tensors" in the previous section, this section gives one explicit construction in the surviving gap and measures honestly what it does and does not supply. It has two layers: Layer one is a bulk existence witness — a proof that a bounded-below, local wedge vacuum-energy functional can be written down; Layer two states how this paper's final architecture (§8) uses it — inheriting only its internal invariant as matching data on the transition surface. 6.1 The surplus problem and the marked decomposition

First, why a general pair of positive-sign fluxes is unacceptable. With flux normalization $f_i=\mathcal N M_P\omega_i/c$ (the original value of this series' cosmological-constant installment being $\mathcal N=\sqrt2$), the ratio of the sum of two independent positive-sign 4-forms to the observed value is $$\frac{\rho_{\rm sum}}{\rho_\Lambda^{\rm pub}}=\frac{\mathcal N^2(1+\varepsilon^2)}{8\varepsilon}\;,\tag{6.1}$$ about $20$ at $\mathcal N=\sqrt2$, about $40$ at $\mathcal N=2$. This is the positive-flux surplus problem: unless the ledger nearly cancels somehow, the model is off by more than an order of magnitude at leading order. 〔structural, coefficient dictionary-dependent〕

The object of this section is therefore not two independent 4-forms but two marked components of a single rank-four internal top-form multiplet. For the exterior derivative and the vacuum energy to be mathematically clean, this paper locks the internal bundle to a fixed, trivial, flat-connection Euclidean bundle $E=M\times V_{\rm int}$ ($V_{\rm int}\cong\mathbb R^4$): then $B\in\Omega^3(M)\otimes V_{\rm int}$, $F=dB$ is legitimate (under a flat connection the covariant exterior derivative is the ordinary $d$), and the internal directions $e_1,e_2\in V_{\rm int}$ in the flux decomposition $\Phi_1=f_1e_1$, $\Phi_2=f_2e_2$ are global internal background sections, the marked projection an explicit construction input. This lock is deliberate: a general non-trivial internal bundle would require a connection $\mathcal D$, internal curvature, and the corresponding gauge covariance, substantially widening this paper's jurisdiction and introducing near the degeneracy point a transverse gauge dynamics not treated here; a trivial flat bundle avoids these, at the cost of recording "why the internal bundle is trivial and flat" as a construction input. This internal fiber is not newly invented: the block time axes of Four-Forces VII are objects in the internal configuration space (the stage of their $S^3$ packing), not spacetime directions; the two cosmological sectors each inherit one internal time axis, antiparallel at the symmetric point, and the frequency asymmetry offsets them from exact antiparallel by the defect mechanism of §5. Flag at once the construction's first open item: at the strict antiparallel point $e_2=-e_1$ the decomposition of the total flux $\Phi=(f_1-f_2)e_1$ degenerates, and the total field alone cannot reconstruct $f_1f_2$ and the wedge — the marked decomposition itself (two labeled components, a face index, or a rank-one projection) is a construction input, its microscopic origin listed in §10. 〔open〕

6.2 Conventions, action, and variation

Take signature $(-,+,+,+)$; dual scalars $\Phi_i=\star F_i$; and, under a metric variation at fixed covariant $F_i$, $\delta\Phi_i=\tfrac12\Phi_i\,g_{\mu\nu}\delta g^{\mu\nu}$. Layer one's action is $$S_F=+\int d^4x\,\sqrt{-g}\;\mathcal U_\lambda(\Phi_1,\Phi_2),\qquad \mathcal U_\lambda=\tfrac12\big|\Phi_1+\Phi_2\big|^2+\lambda\big|\Phi_1\wedge\Phi_2\big|,\quad\lambda\ge0,\tag{6.2}$$ the norm and wedge taken from the internal bundle's Euclidean structure. The positive sign is forced: the canonical single 4-form kinetic term $-\tfrac1{48}F^2=+\tfrac12\Phi^2$, and (6.2) must agree with it in the $\lambda=0$, single-component limit. $\mathcal U_\lambda$ is quadratically homogeneous in the fluxes; the Euler relation $\sum_i\Phi_i\,\partial\mathcal U/\partial\Phi_i=2\mathcal U$ together with the metric variation of $\delta\Phi_i$ gives $$T_{\mu\nu}=-\,\mathcal U_\lambda\,g_{\mu\nu},\qquad \rho=\mathcal U_\lambda\ge0.\tag{6.3}$$ This is a genuine $w=-1$ vacuum source, placed into the bulk density and obtained through the complete metric variation — provided $e_i$ are metric-independent internal boundary data. Three qualifications on (6.3) must be stated together. First, bounded below, not strictly positive-definite: along the equal-amplitude strictly-antiparallel direction $\Phi_2=-\Phi_1$ any common amplitude gives zero energy — a zero-energy flat flux direction, not a propagating ghost, but a microscopic-completion problem to be kept for §10. Second, what this paper calls "the witness's health" means only bounded-below and free of local propagating ghosts; it does not mean the microscopic completion, spectral stability, and membrane sector are closed, so throughout it is called "a bounded-below, ghost-free existence witness" with no stronger claim. Third, a hard cutoff at the degenerate core: as the system approaches the strict-antiparallel zero of (6.4), with $e_1$ and $e_2$ tending to collinear, $\mathcal U_\lambda$ itself contains no term protecting the positivity of the internal transverse (non-collinear) perturbation-mode kinetics; so this construction's range as a bulk existence witness is cut off above $\varphi\sim O(\varepsilon)>0$, and the behavior at the degenerate core ($\rho\to0$) must be given an infrared cutoff by higher-derivative terms or by the transition-surface completion mechanism (§10) — this cutoff being itself listed as a sub-item of $D_{\rm int}$, not claimed as solved. 〔structural (construction exists); health as a qualified definition; degenerate-core cutoff open〕

本系列的暗扇区篇留下两个作用量级开放项:khronon–$C$ 混合系数 $g$ 的固定,与带符号真空账本 $\Lambda_1+\Lambda_2=0$ 进入观测方程的来路。本文辨认二者属于同一个实现问题——dual-4DD 双侧结构如何进入单度规作用量——并给出其现状的精确度量。否定结果是定理:在健康局域 bulk(单度规、时间可定向、传播扇区正定、精确平移对称)中,带符号账本不能由两个独立正则应力张量之差实现;$g$ 被证明构造性欠定——bulk 不产它、该混合算符不驱动背景穿越、对称性只锁其奇性、对称性允许一个 bulk $\theta Q$ 但锁不住系数。建设性候选:以上游四力篇 VII 已发表的一阶 packing 向量解给出的反平行亏损角(双侧时间轴相反但不严格 180°,偏离角一阶线性于频率不对称)为几何原料,一个内部 top-form wedge 提供有下界、无局域传播鬼的存在性见证,在非零分支中给出 $O(|\varepsilon|)$ 起始、恒非负的真空量值;该总见证泛函的零点是两标记分量等幅严格反平行($\varepsilon=0$ 时量值归零),而账本公理 $\Lambda_1+\Lambda_2=0$ 在此架构中只由同一循环内 dual pair 两面的零和($\mathscr L_{n,+}+\mathscr L_{n,-}=0$,face 指标)承载;量值的逐循环继承/漂移由独立的 cycle 映射候选 $\rho_{n+1}=\mathcal M(\rho_n,\Xi_{1,n},\Xi_{2,n})$ 承担,不属于该恒等式;最小一致架构把量值置于 Crunch–Bang 过渡面烙印,把 dual-face 符号置于 face 取向账本。欠定性度量是本文的真正成果:观测只固定 $\mathcal N^2\lambda k=8$ 这一个不变量组合——通量归一 $\mathcal N$、几何倾角系数 $k$ 与 Wilson 读出 $\lambda$ 三者无一被独立导出,其欠定性分别定位于可指认的开放问题;此匹配关系是目前最简洁的数值目标,但仍是 consilience(𝒩 是字典、λ 是 Wilson 系数,非纯几何),未达先验强制。附带产出:HP2 分层坐实——正号非鬼 DBI baseline 内 $w_{\rm int}\ge-1$ 为硬边界,有效穿越是被证明欠定的 bulk Wilson 分支,其观测判决按嵌套假说结构给出。本文有三重结果:否定结果、建设性候选、欠定性度量;全文主张按结构性/后验/动机性/开放四层标注。

§1 引言:两个开放项,一个实现问题

本系列的暗扇区篇以一个作用量统一了暗物质(khronon)、暗能量与 MOND($C$ 场)以及宇宙学常数(4-form 扇区),并在篇末如实留下两个作用量级的开放项:其一,khronon–$C$ 混合算符 $g\,\theta Q$ 的系数——它承载有效状态方程的表观 phantom 穿越,而其大小与符号未被固定;其二,带符号真空账本 $\Lambda_1+\Lambda_2=0$ 与观测宇宙学常数 $\Lambda_{\rm obs}=2(\omega_2^2-\omega_1^2)/c^2$ 进入物质所见方程的作用量级来路——自双帧篇以来即列为开放。本文的出发点是一个辨认:这两个开放项属于同一个实现类——dual-4DD 的双侧结构如何进入单度规作用量。须立即限定这句话的强度:是同一个实现类,不是同一个算符或同一个系数;$g\theta Q$ 是导数阶混合,带符号账本是零导数真空源,二者共享实现障碍,但其中一方的 no-go 不自动给出另一方的数值。

把实现问题摆成精确形式。所求是一个数学结构,使单流形、单度规上的作用量同时满足:(L) 带符号账本——真空扇区对观测方程的净贡献正比于 $\omega_2^2-\omega_1^2$(差,不是和);(V) 数值边界条件——发表式 $\Lambda_{\rm obs}=2(\omega_2^2-\omega_1^2)/c^2$ 与 Planck-derived $\Lambda$ 值之间约 5% 的数值偏差(此 5% 是既有 SAE 公式与 Planck 导出值之间的匹配误差,非 Planck 观测本身的不确定度),通量字典可修订但须登记;(C) $C$ 场局域性——$C=\tau_1-\tau_2$ 仍是本时空上的局域动力学场,暗能量与 MOND 扇区建立在它上;(G) 无鬼——两扇区共享度规,任何传播场的错号动能经引力耦合即为死刑;(T) 无因果闭环——单度规,不引入双度规或环状时间拓扑;(S) 精确平移——$C\to C+C_0$ 为作用量的精确对称。这组约束为什么不是装饰,由一个承重数说明:设通量归一 $f_i=\mathcal N M_P\omega_i/c$,两支独立正号 4-form 的真空能和项与观测值之比为 $\mathcal N^2(1+\varepsilon^2)/(8\varepsilon)$——在本系列宇宙学常数篇的原始字典($\mathcal N=\sqrt2$)下约 20 倍,$\mathcal N=2$ 下约 40 倍。账本若不近于相消,模型在零阶即错一个数量级以上。

本文的结果按其真实强度陈述如下。我们证明:dual-4DD 的带符号真空账本,不能由健康单度规 bulk 中两个独立正则应力张量之差来实现(§4 的 no-go 群);$g$ 在本文锁定的 baseline 与算符分类中被证明构造性欠定——bulk 不产它、该混合算符不驱动背景穿越、对称性只锁其奇性、对称性允许一个 bulk $\theta Q$ 但锁不住系数(§3)。在被 no-go 群排除法指明的幸存缝隙中,一个内部 top-form wedge 提供了有下界的局域存在性见证:它在非零分支中自然产生 $O(|\varepsilon|)$ 的正真空量值,但其通量归一 $\mathcal N$、几何倾角系数 $k$ 与 Wilson 读出 $\lambda$ 仅受 $$\mathcal N^2\lambda k=8$$ 这一条目标关系约束,三者无一被独立导出(§5–§6)。由此,最小一致架构把物理量值置于过渡面烙印、把 dual-face 符号置于 face 取向账本,而把完整的匹配律明确留作下一层问题(§7–§8)。附带产出是 HP2 的嵌套分层:正号非鬼 DBI baseline 内 $w_{\rm int}\ge-1$ 为硬边界,有效穿越是被证明欠定的 bulk Wilson 分支,其观测判决结构见 §9。

本文不做的事同样明列:不完成过渡面匹配泛函;不计算模型特定的核化率(仅给薄壁 benchmark 分区,数值入附录 D);不触动本系列第三篇的 $a_0$ 扇区(HP1 的裁决纪律照旧);不激活有效穿越分支;不推导 $\mathcal N$、$\lambda$、$k$ 中任何一个的数值。行文次序:§2 立契约与假设盒;§3 处理 $\theta Q$;§4 证 no-go 群;§5 给倾角原料;§6 建 wedge 见证;§7 处理配对与过渡面;§8 综合架构并登记对前篇表述的修订;§9 给嵌套可证伪假说;§10 收束并列下一层钥匙。

§2 认识论契约、baseline 与两本账

分层与分账。 本文沿用本系列第零篇立下的四层标注——〔结构性〕由框架强制、可证伪;〔后验〕依赖观测或参数;〔动机性〕有充分动机的偏好;〔开放〕尚未判决——并对每一个出现的系数追加三分账:先验导出(由框架结构给出)、构造输入(建模选择,显式标注)、后验边界(取自观测)。凡"构造输入"冒充"先验导出"之处,均为本文自设的可指认错误。

锁定的后验输入。 呼吸周期 $T_1=20$ Gyr、$T_2\approx19.5$ Gyr;频率不对称 $\varepsilon=(\omega_2-\omega_1)/(\omega_2+\omega_1)\approx0.0127$。本文一切"$O(\varepsilon)$"均指对此参数的阶。

Baseline 声明。 本文全程采用精确平移 baseline:单度规;物质极小耦合 $S_m[g_{\mu\nu}]$;$C$ 只经导数进入作用量($Q$、$\nabla C$),势为常数由对称强制。双帧篇的共形机器($\mathscr A(C)$、$F(C)R$)整体迁往破缺平移的分支,本文不使用亦不否证之;此迁移作为对前篇表述的修订之一,登记于 §8。

公共假设盒。 本文全部 no-go 命题共享同一定义域,在此一次写明:四维;Lorentz 签名;单度规;时间可定向的 bulk;传播扇区正定(无鬼);作用量局域;$C$ 的精确平移对称。凡额外用到解析性之处(定理 3.3、§6.4 的宇称三岔口),逐处标注。每条命题之后列出的"定义域外的逃生口",即离开此盒的具体方式。

两本账。 以下两个量贯穿全文,其一为定义,其一为定义加候选律,须分立;并须先把两个不同的指标分清——face 指标($\pm$,同一循环内 dual pair 的两面)与 cycle 指标($n\to n+1$,相邻时间循环),此前混为一个 $n+1$ 是错误。定义:$\rho_{\rm phys}\ge0$ 为每一循环进入 Einstein 方程的物理真空能;取向账本按 face 指标写为 $$\mathscr L_{n,+}=+\rho_n,\qquad \mathscr L_{n,-}=-\rho_n,\qquad \boxed{\mathscr L_{n,+}+\mathscr L_{n,-}=0},$$ 这表示第 $n$ 循环之 dual pair 内两面的零和账本——这才是 $\Lambda_1+\Lambda_2=0$ 的身份,它属于同一循环的两面,不涉及相邻循环。候选匹配律(非定义,待完整边界泛函导出)另管 cycle 指标: $$\rho_{n+1}=\mathcal M(\rho_n,\Xi_{1,n},\Xi_{2,n}),$$ 允许量值逐循环漂移(螺旋)。由此,"两侧各自观测到正的宇宙学常数"是 $\rho_{\rm phys}\ge0$ 的性质;"dual pair 零和"是 face 级的零和账本;"螺旋漂移"是 $n\to n+1$ 的跨循环演化——三者分属不同指标,不再互相抵触(此前把"对侧编号"与"下一循环编号"压成一个 $n+1$,会错误地要求 $\rho_{n+1}=\rho_n$)。全文凡言 dual pair 的符号,指 face 指标的 $\pm$(取向标签 $\chi_{n,s}=s$,无不带 face 指标的裸 $\chi_n$);凡言量值,指 $\rho_{\rm phys}$——二者混用是本文自禁的第二类错误。

§3 θQ 的起源与 HP2 的分层

先立对象。在本文的 baseline 中(§2),aether 是呼吸叶状的单位法向 $A_\mu=-\partial_\mu\tau_b/\sqrt{-(\partial\tau_b)^2}$;记 $$\theta\equiv\nabla_\mu A^\mu,\qquad Q\equiv A^\mu\partial_\mu C,$$ FLRW 上 $\theta=3H$、$Q=\dot C$。算符 $g\,\theta Q$ 是 khronon 与 $C$ 之间领头的、尊重精确平移对称的编织型混合;本系列暗扇区篇已指出,它是有效状态方程得以呈现表观 phantom 穿越的载体,并把系数 $g$ 列为自由的、受 $g\propto\varepsilon$ 约束的 Wilson 分支。本节把那一 EFT 分类升级为三条作用量级定理与一条构造性欠定命题:$g$ 从何处不能来、其函数形式被什么约束、以及为什么现有结构不足以固定它。

定理 3.1(bulk 无源)。 仅含一阶导数的双 DBI bulk 不产生含 $\nabla A$ 的 $\theta Q$ 算符。假设: baseline 双钟作用量 $-\sum_i\mathcal T_i\sqrt{1+(\partial\tau_i)^2}$;单度规;无界面项。证明梗概: 二次展开下,和差变量给出 $$\mathcal T_1(\partial\tau_1)^2+\mathcal T_2(\partial\tau_2)^2=(\mathcal T_1{+}\mathcal T_2)\Big[(\partial\tau_b)^2+\tfrac14(\partial C)^2\Big]+(\mathcal T_1{-}\mathcal T_2)\,\partial\tau_b\!\cdot\!\partial C,$$ 叉项是纯一阶导的 $Y\equiv\partial\tau_b\!\cdot\!\partial C$ 型,二次层可经场重定义对角化(须注意:对角化改变 aether 的物理定义,故此处结论限于"二次动能矩阵可对角化",不称"混合物理上无效")。完整 DBI 为 $-\mathcal T_1\sqrt{U+Y}-\mathcal T_2\sqrt{U-Y}$($U=1+X_b+\tfrac14X_C$),保留 $Y^2$ 及张力不等时的 $Y$ 奇次非线性混合——但通篇仍是一阶导数;$\theta$ 含 $\tau_b$ 的二阶导数,在结构上无处栖身。齐次场方程中出现的 $3H$(经 $\tfrac{d}{dt}(a^3P_Q)=0$)是协变散度给出的 Hubble 摩擦,不是作用量级的 $g\theta Q$ 算符。定义域外的逃生口: 独立加入高阶导数 bulk Wilson 算符 $g_{\rm df}\theta Q$(命题 3.4 的对称性允许性建立于此;其微观来源可以是厚界面有效化或其他 UV 结构,但真正 codimension-one 边界项不能持续驱动晚期 bulk 背景);或破坏精确平移对称的物质耦合(在 baseline 之外)。〔结构性〕

定理 3.2($J\!\cdot\!\nabla C$ 算符无背景源)。 在精确 FLRW 背景上,aether–标量混合算符 $J^\mu\nabla_\mu C$($J^\nu\equiv A^\mu\nabla_\mu A^\nu$)点值为零;其与 $\theta Q$ 的分部积分关系不提供独立的背景层混合源。假设: 精确 FLRW;作用量含 $J^\mu\nabla_\mu C$ 项(如 Skordis–Złośnik 型相对论 MOND 理论中的矢量–标量混合;须注意该理论是完整的矢量–标量–度规系统,本定理只针对其中这一混合算符,不涉及其余 $Q$-依赖函数)。证明梗概: 恒等式 $$\int\!\sqrt{-g}\;\theta Q=-\int\!\sqrt{-g}\Big[J^\nu\nabla_\nu C+A^\mu A^\nu\nabla_\mu\nabla_\nu C\Big]$$ 把 $\theta Q$ 与其伴随的法向 Hessian 绑定;FLRW 上 $J^\mu=0$、$\theta Q=3H\dot C$、Hessian $=\ddot C$,而 $\int dt\,a^3(3H\dot C+\ddot C)=[a^3\dot C]_{\rm bdry}$ 是纯边界项。故经分部积分单独读出一个"$\theta Q$ 的背景系数"是算符基底的簿记,不是物理;该混合算符自身不驱动背景层的有效穿越。逃生口: 扰动层与非均匀背景——彼处保留依赖参数的 $J\!\cdot\!\nabla C$ 混合,这正是 §9 扰动判据的来源;或显式加入一个独立的 bulk $\theta Q$ 算符——其系数随即受定理 3.3 与命题 3.4 支配。〔结构性〕

定理 3.3(宇称与解析性)。 设作用量在双面交换 $1\leftrightarrow2$ 下不变、$\varepsilon$ 是唯一的交换奇 spurion、且 $g$ 在 $\varepsilon=0$ 附近解析,则 $$g(\varepsilon)=g_1\varepsilon+g_3\varepsilon^3+\cdots.$$ 假设: 如上三条,逐条为要。证明梗概: 交换下 $\tau_b\to\tau_b$、$C\to-C$,故 $A,\theta$ 偶、$Q$ 奇、$\theta Q$ 奇;作用量不变强制 $g(-\varepsilon)=-g(\varepsilon)$;解析的奇函数只含奇次幂。定义域外的逃生口: 撤去解析性,只余"$g$ 为奇函数"——$\operatorname{sgn}\varepsilon$、$\varepsilon|\varepsilon|$、$\varepsilon\log|\varepsilon|$ 等非解析奇结构被允许,线性首项不被推出($|\varepsilon|$ 为偶,任何情形下不可单独为 $g$);撤去"唯一奇 spurion",则 $g$ 可写作另一交换奇对象与偶函数之积——§6.4 宇称三岔口中的 $\chi W$ 路正是此逃生口的一个实例,两处是同一结构事实的两面。〔结构性〕

命题 3.4(对称性允许性与构造性欠定)。 一个尊重精确平移与交换协变的 bulk Wilson 算符 $g_{\rm df}(\varepsilon)\,\theta Q$ 被对称性允许——$g_{\rm df}$ 满足定理 3.3——但在本文锁定的 baseline 与算符分类中,其系数未被结构固定,且欠定是构造性的。假设: baseline;某个把该算符与真空扇区联系起来的构造,其零导数(真空)归一已定。关于类型的声明: 这里断言的是一个四维 bulk 相互作用 $\int_M\sqrt{-g}\,g_{\rm df}(\varepsilon)\theta Q$——它可持续影响晚期 $w_{\rm eff}(z)$,是 §9 相互作用分支的对象;其微观(界面或其他)起源本身开放。这与 §7–§8 的 codimension-one temporal junction 是不同类型的对象(命题 4.1),后者只产生边界匹配或瞬时冲量,不能充当持续驱动背景演化的 bulk 算符;本文不把二者混为一谈。证明梗概(允许性 + 欠定): 交换奇的、由相位流膨胀与法向导数组成的标量在均匀共向极限约化为 $\theta Q$ 型,其系数对 $\varepsilon$ 为奇函数(在唯一奇 spurion 且解析时首项为 $O(\varepsilon)$)——故该算符不被对称性禁止。须一次记明量纲:按本文双钟/DBI 归一,$[\theta]=M$、$Q/c$ 无量纲(取 $c=1$ 则 $Q$ 无量纲),故 $[\theta Q]=M$,而 Wilson 系数 $[g_{\rm df}]=M^3$(与候选尺度 $g_{\rm df}\sim M_P^2\bar\omega\,\varepsilon$ 一致)。其大小构造性欠定:设生成它的单一微观函数为 $\mathscr F$、以 $X\equiv\theta Q/\mu_\Sigma$ 为自变量($[\mu_\Sigma]=M$,故 $X$ 无量纲),零导数闸固定 $\mathscr F(0)$;则单参数族 $\mathscr F_\beta(X)=1+\beta X$($\beta$ 为无量纲 Wilson 系数)对任意 $\beta$ 通过同一闸而给出不同的 $\mathscr F'(0)$—— $$\mathscr F(0)\ \text{已定}\;\nRightarrow\;\mathscr F'(0)\ \text{已定}.$$ EFT 中不同导数阶算符的系数原则上独立;真空归一锁不住导数系数。逃生口: 一个把零导数阶与二导数阶在同一次展开中锁死的完整微观作用量(给出作用量、测度、系数量纲与变分)——本文 §4 的簿记定理(命题 4.3)与上式共同说明,以真空因子为校准闸的自然候选不能胜任;或离开 baseline。〔结构性(允许性 + 欠定);完整微观构造:开放〕

HP2 的分层由此坐实。 第一层不动:baseline 的内禀状态方程满足 $w_{\rm int}\ge-1$——它来自正号 DBI 的非鬼性($\rho+p=2XP_X\ge0$),与本节的一切无关,是正号非鬼 DBI-$C$ baseline 内的硬边界(此强度陈述避免被读成对一切可能的 SAE 完成无条件)。第二层升级:有效穿越所需的 bulk Wilson 算符 $g_{\rm df}\theta Q$,此前在暗扇区篇中是"未曾计算的自由分支";本节的定理与命题把它变成"被证明在现有结构内欠定的分支"——最小 DBI bulk 不产它(定理 3.1),$J\!\cdot\!\nabla C$ 算符不驱动背景穿越(定理 3.2),对称性只锁其奇性(定理 3.3),对称性允许一个 bulk $\theta Q$ 但锁不住其系数(命题 3.4)。框架既不预言穿越,也不禁止穿越;这一分层的观测判决结构——什么数据裁第一层、什么组合裁第二层——见 §9 的嵌套假说。〔结构性(分类);观测对位见 §9〕

§4 带符号账本的 no-go 群

本节证明本文的中心否定性结果:在 §2 假设盒所限定的健康局域 bulk 中——四维、Lorentz 签名、单度规、时间可定向、传播扇区正定、作用量局域、$C$ 场精确平移对称——dual-4DD 的带符号真空账本 $\Lambda_1+\Lambda_2=0$ 不能由两个共处的、各自以标准应力张量耦合同一度规的扇区实现。我们把这一结果拆成七条命题(链路见图 1)。每条命题都按同一模板陈述:命题、假设、证明梗概、定义域外的逃生口。逃生口的显式列出既是诚实,也是路标:本文 §6 至 §8 所走的建设性道路,正是从这些逃生口中选出的,而非绕过这些命题。

命题 4.1(余维错配)。 四维有效真空密度与三维 junction 张力是不同类型的对象;把一个写作 $\int d^4x\sqrt{-g}\,\mathcal L_\Sigma$ 的有效"界面"项直接读作 codimension-one 微观 junction,会同时产生量纲错配与支撑错配。假设: 四维时空;junction 为 codimension-one 超曲面,以诱导度规体元 $\int d^3\xi\sqrt{|h|}$ 携带作用量。证明梗概: 前者的密度量纲为 $M^4$,后者为 $M^3$,转换必引入一个厚度尺度 $\ell_\Sigma$——一个新的自由微观尺度。更要害的是支撑:codimension-one 源进入 Einstein 方程的方式是 Israel 跳变条件(Israel 1966),其应力支撑在 $\delta_\Sigma$ 上;类空的一次过渡面给宇宙学方程一个瞬时冲量,类时的 domain wall 给局域墙并一般地破坏 FRW 均匀性——两者都不是齐次的 $-\rho\,g_{\mu\nu}$ 型 Friedmann 源。逃生口: 保持四维有效密度读法(但其微观起源随之悬置);把墙网络 smear 成稠密叶状;或升维使四维宇宙本身成为 brane。三者都需要本文假设盒之外的额外结构。〔结构性〕

命题 4.2(符号错配)。 同一度规中两支正则 4-form 的真空能相加而不相减;时空取向反转不能翻转这一符号。假设: 单度规;正则动能 $-\frac{1}{2\cdot4!}\sum_{ij}K_{ij}F_i^{\mu\nu\rho\sigma}F_{j\,\mu\nu\rho\sigma}$,kinetic matrix $K$ 正定;两支通量共处于同一 bulk。证明梗概: 四维中 $F_i=f_i\,\mathrm{vol}_4$,真空能 $\rho=\tfrac12 f^{\mathsf T}Kf\ge0$。取向反转至多实现 $f_i\mapsto-f_i$,而 $\rho(-f)=\rho(f)$。加入 cross-term 亦无济于事:只要 $K$ 正定,正交对角化后仍是正平方之和。欲得 $f_2^2-f_1^2$ 型的差,$K$ 须至少含一个负本征值,或另引一个物理的取向奇 spurion。逃生口: 非正定 kinetic(对 top-form 的特殊地位见本节末注记);物理奇 spurion;双度规(已被本系列的反因果闭环纪律排除);把符号移出 bulk、交给边界或非局域结构。〔结构性〕

命题 4.3(簿记定理)。 4-form 的 on-shell 作用量中出现的边界项系数不能直接读作应力张量中的物理因子。假设: 4-form 场在固定通量系综下作良定变分,须补 Bousso–Polchinski 型边界项(Bousso & Polchinski 2000)。证明梗概: 该边界项的 on-shell 值恰为体积 $F^2$ 项的负两倍;它的职能是消除 on-shell 作用量与场方程之间的表面矛盾(固定通量与固定势两种系综之差),而非改动物理真空能。后者必须经 $T_{\mu\nu}=-\frac{2}{\sqrt{-g}}\frac{\delta S}{\delta g^{\mu\nu}}$ 读出,其值仍为 $+f^2/2$ 型。逃生口: 无。这是一条方法论定理:任何把 on-shell 作用量数值径直当作能量的推导,须回到度规变分重做。〔结构性〕

命题 4.4(因果型错配)。 用于通量放电的 Brown–Teitelboim 膜与满足 $K_\Sigma=\theta$ 的 khronon 叶不能是同一张超曲面。假设: Lorentz 签名;放电膜为类时世界体(法向类空,Brown & Teitelboim 1988);khronon 叶 $\tau_b=\mathrm{const}$ 为类空(法向 $A^\mu$ 类时)。证明梗概: $K_\Sigma=\theta$ 要求 $n^\mu=A^\mu$;但同一法向不能既类空又类时。逃生口: 引入显式投影因子($A\!\cdot\!n$ 等)——代价是新增独立系数;或把放电对象改为类空的时间向匹配面(§7 的 temporal junction 路径,该逃生口本质上是通过离开 Lorentz 流形的光滑性要求而实现的)——代价是它不再是标准的、可核化的动力学膜。〔结构性〕

命题 4.5(二次墙)。 取向反转不能翻转任何正定二次型真空能;带符号账本只能经取向奇对象进场。假设: 局域场论;能量为场的二次型且 kinetic 结构正定。证明梗概: 由命题 4.2 的论证对一般二次型直接推广。在本文所考虑的、无基本费米子、无挠率、至两导数的玻色真空扇区中,直接相关的取向奇候选可数:$\int_M F_4$、$\int_\Sigma B_3$(WZ 项)、线性 $\star F$(赝标量)、$d\tau\wedge B$ 型 $\varepsilon$-缩并——它们全部是线性或拓扑的,没有一个是能给出齐次真空差的二次能量。(注:更高阶的特征密度如 $R\wedge R$、Euler/Pontryagin 类虽亦取向奇,却是拓扑/边界项,同样不提供齐次 $-\rho\,g_{\mu\nu}$ 型真空源,故不改变本命题结论;此处的清单是"直接相关",不宣称是一切四维理论的穷尽集。)逃生口: 即命题 4.6 的通道清单。〔结构性〕

命题 4.6(限域定理)。 在传播扇区正定的 bulk 中,账本符号不能由任何传播模 Hamiltonian 的正负承担;其载体必须住在不传播对象、离散/超选择扇区、边界或非局域约束中。假设: §2 假设盒全套;两扇区共享同一度规。证明梗概: 由命题 4.5,符号须经取向奇对象进场;而任何传播场若整体携带错号动能,即经共享度规的引力耦合成为鬼,违反假设盒的正定传播条款。非传播的 top-form(零局域自由度)、离散/超选择扇区、边界数据与非局域约束不受此限。须精确陈述本命题的两个收窄面。其一,UV 完成可含传播模:一个健康的传播场完全可以在其离散真空/超选择扇区中提供交换奇 spurion——被排除的不是"理论含传播模",而是"由传播模 Hamiltonian 的错号来承担账本符号"这一特定机制;符号本身不能由传播 Hamiltonian 的正负承担,但其 UV 完成可以含传播模。其二,健康的、交换偶的耦合(如正定 kinetic mixing)仍可在两扇区间传递扰动——被限域的只是符号这一比特,不是一切跨扇区通信。逃生口: 本命题给出的四类载体即通道清单;本文 §6 与 §8 分别走"非传播 top-form"与"边界"两条。〔结构性〕

命题 4.7(覆盖障碍)。 若 $C$ 被取作非平凡时间取向线丛 $L_t$ 的奇截面,且精确平移对称要求对 $C$ 加一个全局非零常数截面,则连通的时间取向双覆盖与精确平移对称不相容;全局 khronon 的存在亦平凡化该丛。假设: 连通覆盖(等价于基流形非时间可定向);$C\in\Gamma(L_t)$;平移为加性全局常数。证明梗概: 非平凡 $\mathbb Z_2$ 线丛上不存在全局平行的非零常数截面——若存在,它就把丛平凡化了;而 khronometric 结构所要求的全局类时矢量场,本身就是时间可定向性。逃生口: 平凡覆盖(它能干净地组织交换宇称,但不解账本);或把非平凡性限制在过渡核心——即因果结构本身解体之处——这正是 §7 至 §8 的边界匹配读法,但它是跨边界的匹配规则,不是单个光滑 Lorentz 流形上的 bulk 实现。〔结构性〕

注记(错号 top-form 的特殊地位)。 四维中 3-form 规范场的局域传播自由度为 $\binom{D-2}{p}=\binom{2}{3}=0$;因此给某一支 4-form 配错号动能不产生微扰鬼——这一点使命题 4.2 的"非正定 kinetic"逃生口不像通常那样立即致命。但代价换了位置:错号扇区的通量能量无下界,任何允许改变通量的膜都开启一条逐跳等步长的下行通道,而固定荷与固定张力之下没有内生的止动器。故错号 top-form 的地位是:形式上可写,健康的微观完成缺位。本文不以它为地基,只在 §8 的比较中引用其形式簿记。〔结构性〕

净判。 综合命题 4.1–4.7:两个独立、正则、共处、各自以标准应力张量耦合同一度规的 4-form 扇区,不能借时间取向反转实现有符号差账。 这句判词的每一个限定词都是刻意的。它不覆盖:单一内部 multiplet 的两个标记分量以近反平行方式共处(它们不各自携带独立的标准应力张量,而是同一泛函的分量)——§6 的建设性部分正建立在这条未被封死的缝隙上;也不覆盖边界与非局域结构——§8 的架构综合建立在那里。一个 no-go 群的价值,一半在它杀死的东西,一半在它借排除法所指明的幸存者。

§5 倾角:反平行亏损角与几何欠定

带符号账本被 §4 逐出健康 bulk 之后,本文的建设性道路(§6)需要一个几何原料:两个扇区的内部方向近反平行而非严格反平行。本节说明这一原料在上游四力篇 VII 中已有已发表的一阶 packing 向量解,给出其可继承的强度,再诚实地度量宇宙学情形下它的系数处于何种欠定状态。

四力系列篇 VII 的向量解与亏损角。 该篇定理 2.2 处理六个 4DD block(三对反向轴对)在 $S^3\subset\mathbb R^4$ 上的最近合法 packing:对称参考构型 $\hat t_a^\pm=\pm e_a$ 是严格反平行的;对内频率不对称 $\delta$ 使两成员的锥半角变为 $\tfrac\pi4(1\pm\delta)$,令对称构型违反 cross-axis 无重叠条件;"余项恰好发展"原则选取合法域中离参考点最近的解,凸优化唯一给出 $$\hat t_a^\pm\propto\pm e_a-\frac{\varepsilon_{\rm pack}}2\sum_{b\neq a}e_b,\qquad \varepsilon_{\rm pack}=\frac\pi2\delta.$$ 本文从该向量解继承对内偏离角(此处须精确说明强度:上游定理给出的是一阶线性结果 $\theta=\tfrac{\pi}{3\sqrt2}\alpha+O(\alpha^2)$,以及 $S_3$ 对称 ansatz 下线性化所得的展示向量;故以下反正切式是那组一阶展示向量正规化后的几何角,而非完整非线性 packing 解的精确角): $$\varphi_{\rm pair}=\sqrt2\,\varepsilon_{\rm pack}+O(\varepsilon_{\rm pack}^2)\qquad\big(\text{正规化一阶代表向量的几何角}=2\arctan\tfrac{\varepsilon_{\rm pack}}{\sqrt2}\big).$$ 即:双面的时间轴相反、但偏离严格 180° 一个正比于频率不对称的角,一阶线性,系数由 packing 几何(线性化解)钉定。 同一组一阶向量给出三个不同的角,须分立命名(图 2):单个 block 离轴角 $\theta_{\rm off}=\varepsilon_{\rm pack}/\sqrt2+O(\varepsilon_{\rm pack}^2)$;对内反平行亏损角 $\varphi_{\rm pair}=\sqrt2\,\varepsilon_{\rm pack}+O(\varepsilon_{\rm pack}^2)$;cross-axis 角距增量 $\Delta\theta_{\rm cross}=\varepsilon_{\rm pack}+O(\varepsilon_{\rm pack}^2)$(其一阶值恰与无重叠阈移一致)。四力篇 VII 文本中以"cross-axis 倾斜角"命名的量 $\sqrt2\,\varepsilon$,几何身份是其中第二者(亏损角)。既有数值公式($c_1=\pi/(3\sqrt2)$ 及其对 $R_1$ 的读出)使用的正是该量的大小;本次修订改变的是几何名称,不改变既有数值读出;正式的术语勘误由四力篇 VII 自身的版本升级承载,本文仅引其一阶向量解并作此注。此处"唯一"须完整理解为在 $S_3$ 对称 ansatz 的线性化 closest-packing 问题中唯一,不是不带限定地断言整个非线性 packing 问题唯一。〔一阶结构:结构性(继承上游);勘误为登记性〕

有向角严格奇性(本文新增的独立一步)。 上述一阶继承之外,若另由交换唯一性(§3 定理 3.3 同型的论证:$\widetilde\varphi$ 是唯一交换奇 spurion $\varepsilon$ 的解析奇函数)证明有向倾角严格为奇,则可进一步写 $\widetilde\varphi=k\varepsilon+O(\varepsilon^3)$(奇次幂,无 $\varepsilon^2$)。须强调:这不能从上游"线性化解"自动取得,是本文新增的一条独立论证;偶次项的消去依赖交换对称,而非 packing 几何。

有向倾角与几何亏损角的分立。 后文的宇称记账要求先把两个量分开。几何亏损角 $\varphi$ 是两内部方向对反平行的非负夹角,交换下不变号;真正交换奇的是一个有向倾角 $\widetilde\varphi$,满足 $$\widetilde\varphi(-\varepsilon)=-\widetilde\varphi(\varepsilon),\qquad \widetilde\varphi=k\varepsilon+k_3\varepsilon^3+\cdots\ (\text{解析、交换协变时}),\qquad \varphi=|\widetilde\varphi|.$$ 全文凡言物理能量与几何夹角,取偶的 $\varphi$;凡言交换奇的母量,取 $\widetilde\varphi$。三个阶须分清(消解 §5 一阶响应与 §6 匹配展开之间的表观阶差): $$\widetilde\varphi=O(\varepsilon)\ (\text{奇}),\qquad |\widetilde\varphi|=O(|\varepsilon|)\ (\text{偶}),\qquad \widetilde\varphi^{\,2}=O(\varepsilon^2)\ (\text{偶}).$$ 物理夹角与物理能量走 $|\widetilde\varphi|$ 或 $\widetilde\varphi^2$ 这两支(偶),交换奇的母量走 $\widetilde\varphi$(奇)。

§6 wedge:最小 EFT 候选与欠定性

上一节把"两个独立应力张量之差"封死之后,本节给出幸存缝隙中的一个显式构造,并诚实地度量它给出了什么、没给出什么。本节分两层:层一是一个 bulk 存在性见证(existence witness)——证明一个有下界的、局域的 wedge 真空能泛函可以被写下;层二说明本文最终架构(§8)如何使用它——只继承其内部不变量作为过渡面的匹配数据。二者不混称:层一证明"能写",层二才是本文所采用的位置。

6.1 超额问题与标记分解

先说清一般的双正号通量为何不可接受。设通量归一 $f_i=\mathcal N M_P\omega_i/c$(本系列宇宙学常数篇的原始取值对应 $\mathcal N=\sqrt2$),则两支独立正号 4-form 的和项与观测值之比为 $$\frac{\rho_{\rm sum}}{\rho_\Lambda^{\rm pub}}=\frac{\mathcal N^2(1+\varepsilon^2)}{8\varepsilon}\;,\tag{6.1}$$ 在 $\mathcal N=\sqrt2$ 下约 20 倍,$\mathcal N=2$ 下约 40 倍。这就是正通量和项超额问题:账本若不以某种方式近于相消,模型在零阶就错一个数量级以上。〔结构性,系数依赖字典〕

本节的对象因此不是两支独立的 4-form,而是一支秩四内部 top-form multiplet 的两个标记分量。为使外微分与真空能定义在数学上干净,本文把内部丛锁定为固定、平凡、带平直联络的欧氏丛 $E=M\times V_{\rm int}$($V_{\rm int}\cong\mathbb R^4$):于是 $B\in\Omega^3(M)\otimes V_{\rm int}$、$F=dB$ 合法(平直联络下协变外微分即普通 $d$),通量分解 $\Phi_1=f_1e_1$、$\Phi_2=f_2e_2$ 中的 $e_1,e_2\in V_{\rm int}$ 是全局内部背景截面,标记投影是明确的构造输入。这一锁定是刻意的:一般非平凡内部丛需引入联络 $\mathcal D$、内部曲率与相应的规范协变性,会显著扩大本文辖区,且在近退化点引入本文不拟处理的横向规范动力学;平凡平直丛回避这些,代价是把"内部丛为何平凡平直"记作一项构造输入。这一内部纤维不是新造:四力系列篇 VII 的 block 时间轴正是内部构型空间(其 $S^3$ packing 的舞台)中的对象,而非时空方向;两个宇宙学扇区各继承一个内部时间轴,在严格对称点上反平行,频率不对称按 §5 的亏损角机制使其偏离精确反平行。须立即标出本构造的第一个开放项:在严格反平行点 $e_2=-e_1$ 上,总通量 $\Phi=(f_1-f_2)e_1$ 的分解退化,单凭总场无法重建 $f_1f_2$ 与 wedge——标记分解本身(两个带标签的分量、face 指标、或 rank-one 投影)是构造输入,其微观来源列入 §10。〔开放〕

6.2 约定、作用量与变分

取签名 $(-,+,+,+)$;对偶标量 $\Phi_i=\star F_i$;固定协变 $F_i$ 作度规变分时 $\delta\Phi_i=\tfrac12\Phi_i\,g_{\mu\nu}\delta g^{\mu\nu}$。层一的作用量为 $$S_F=+\int d^4x\,\sqrt{-g}\;\mathcal U_\lambda(\Phi_1,\Phi_2),\qquad \mathcal U_\lambda=\tfrac12\big|\Phi_1+\Phi_2\big|^2+\lambda\big|\Phi_1\wedge\Phi_2\big|,\quad\lambda\ge0,\tag{6.2}$$ 其中范数与楔积取自内部丛的欧氏结构。正号是强制的:正则单支 4-form 动能 $-\tfrac1{48}F^2=+\tfrac12\Phi^2$,式 (6.2) 在 $\lambda=0$、单分量极限下须与之一致。$\mathcal U_\lambda$ 对通量二次齐次,Euler 关系 $\sum_i\Phi_i\,\partial\mathcal U/\partial\Phi_i=2\mathcal U$ 与 $\delta\Phi_i$ 的度规变分合起来给出 $$T_{\mu\nu}=-\,\mathcal U_\lambda\,g_{\mu\nu},\qquad \rho=\mathcal U_\lambda\ge0.\tag{6.3}$$ 这是一个真正放进 bulk 密度、经完整度规变分得到的 $w=-1$ 型真空源——条件是 $e_i$ 为度规无关的内部边界数据。须同时写明式 (6.3) 的三个限定。其一,有下界而非严格正定:在等幅严格反平行方向 $\Phi_2=-\Phi_1$ 上,任意共同幅度都给零能——这是一条零能平坦通量方向,不是传播鬼,但属于 §10 应保留的微观完成问题。其二,本文所称"见证的健康"仅指有下界与无局域传播鬼;它不代表微观完成、谱稳定性与膜扇区已经闭合,故全文以"有下界、无局域传播鬼的存在性见证"称之,不作更强宣称。其三,退化内核处须硬性截断:当系统逼近式 (6.4) 的严格反平行零点、$e_1$ 与 $e_2$ 趋于共线时,$\mathcal U_\lambda$ 自身不含保护内部横向(非共线)扰动模动能正定性的项;因此本构造作为 bulk existence witness 的有效范围截断在 $\varphi\sim O(\varepsilon)>0$ 之上,退化内核($\rho\to0$)处的行为须由高阶导数项或过渡面完成机制(§10)提供动力学红外切断——这一截断本身列入 $D_{\rm int}$ 的子项,而非声称已解决。〔结构性(构造存在);健康性为限定义;退化内核截断为开放〕

6.3 三个对象的分立

以下三个量在本文中承担不同职能,任何一处混用都会把条件性结果冒充为无条件结果,故在此彻底分开。

wedge 量值(与其奇母量) $\rho_{\rm wedge}=\lambda f_1f_2|\sin\widetilde\varphi|\ge0$;其交换奇母量为 $\lambda f_1f_2\sin\widetilde\varphi$(奇,$O(\varepsilon)$),物理量值取其模(偶,$O(|\varepsilon|)$)。零点集是{$e_1\parallel e_2$、$e_1\parallel-e_2$、任一通量为零};它不要求等幅。

总物理真空能 $\rho_{\rm tot}=\mathcal U_\lambda$。在 $f_i>0$、$\lambda\ge0$ 下,在本 existence witness 内, $$\rho_{\rm tot}=0\iff \Phi_2=-\Phi_1\iff\text{等幅且严格反平行}.\tag{6.4}$$ 须精确界定式 (6.4) 的职能:它给出该账本公理的零点几何部分——见证泛函的量值零点当且仅当两标记通量等幅、内部方向严格反平行(钟对语言里即等频且无亏损角);它不是 $\Lambda_1+\Lambda_2=0$ 全部公理的身份。有符号的双面相消(dual pair 零和,$\mathscr L_{n,+}+\mathscr L_{n,-}=0$)须另由 face 级匹配律承担(§2、§8),不由式 (6.4) 给出。〔结构性,witness 内〕

取向账本 $\mathscr L_{n,\pm}=\pm\rho_n$。dual pair 两面的符号由 face 指标 $\pm$ 承担,零和 $\mathscr L_{n,+}+\mathscr L_{n,-}=0$ 是同一循环内的身份(§2);它住在过渡面(§8),不在本节。跨循环量值演化 $\rho_{n+1}=\mathcal M(\rho_n,\Xi_{1,n},\Xi_{2,n})$ 是候选匹配律,尚待完整边界泛函导出。本节的一切收获都关于量值,不关于符号。

6.4 分支条件与收获

现在陈述层一给出的东西——并把分支条件写在最前面。当前对称性容许 $\lambda=0$;若 $\lambda=0$,总能量自 $O(\varepsilon^2)$ 起。因此以下收获全部限定于最小非零 wedge 分支($\lambda>0$,$k\neq0$),框架允许但尚未强制这一分支——这正是后文 $D_{\rm int}$ 的另一面,一枚硬币的两面必须一起呈给读者。

在该分支内:物理量值以 $O(|\varepsilon|)$ 起始——交换奇母量 $\lambda f_1f_2\sin\widetilde\varphi$ 为 $O(\varepsilon)$,取模后的物理真空能为交换偶的 $O(|\varepsilon|)$;它非负,两侧在各自标签下皆见正值;总能量零点(witness 内)由式 (6.4) 给出。〔在最小非零 wedge 分支内结构性〕

线性阶的宇称代价须一并写明。平滑的、交换不变的作用量对有向 wedge $W$ 只能含偶函数,自 $W^2=O(\varepsilon^2)$ 起;线性阶只有两条路——取 $|W|$(本文所走:代价是对称点处的 cusp,其物理隔离条件为 $\varepsilon$ 是逐循环的边界数据而非可越零的 bulk 动力学量),或引入另一个交换奇对象 $\chi$ 写 $\chi W$(平滑,但 $\chi$ 就是取向 spurion 迁入内部纤维)。两条路的共同教训是:离散取向比特并未被 wedge 消灭,只是获得了一个更健康的居所。〔结构性〕

6.5 匹配不变量与两本未闭账

将 §5 的有向一阶响应 $\widetilde\varphi=k\varepsilon+O(\varepsilon^3)$ 代入,wedge 主项为 $\rho_{\rm wedge}=\lambda\mathcal N^2 k\,\varepsilon\,M_P^2\bar\omega^2/c^2+O(\varepsilon^3)$;对照观测式 $\rho_\Lambda^{\rm pub}=8\,\varepsilon\,M_P^2\bar\omega^2/c^2$,匹配条件为 $$\boxed{\;\mathcal N^2\lambda k=8\;}\tag{6.5}$$ ——一条观测约束,压在三本账上:通量归一 $\mathcal N$、几何倾角系数 $k$、wedge 读出系数 $\lambda$,三者无一被独立导出。以 $F=f_1+f_2$、$D=f_2-f_1$ 写出交换协变的展开: $$\rho_{\rm wedge}^{\rm phys}=\frac{2}{\mathcal N^2}\,|FD|+O\!\Big(\frac{|D|^3}{F}\Big);\tag{6.6}$$ 本文其余部分固定在 $D>0$ 的观测分支上,彼时可去绝对值写 $\rho_{\rm wedge}=\frac{2}{\mathcal N^2}FD+O(D^3/F)$。三次修正 $-\frac{6+k^2}{3\mathcal N^2}\frac{D^3}{F}$ 仅在最小角度 ansatz($\varphi=kD/F$ 精确、无三次响应 $k_3$)下成立,$\mathcal N=2$ 时化为 $-\frac{6+k^2}{12}\frac{D^3}{F}$;$k_3$ 本文未挣得,细节入附录 D。

三本账的现状逐一如实(观测匹配等值线见图 3)。$k$:几何欠定($D_{\rm geo}$)——§5 已示多个自然的宇宙约束集给出不同的一阶系数(候选表:$1$、$\pi/\sqrt2$、$\pi\sqrt3/2$),匹配所需之 $2$(取 $\mathcal N=2$ 时)不从"一对钟 + 三维空间"自动跳出。$\lambda$:构造性欠定($D_{\rm int}$)——一参数族 $\mathcal U_\lambda$ 对任意 $\lambda\ge0$ 同时满足单度规、规范不变、精确平移、交换偶、有下界、无传播鬼:没有对称性或恒等式能在当前输入下区分 $\lambda=0.9$、$1$、$2$。曾被寄望的 Cauchy–Schwarz 钉法亦不成立:该恒等式规范的是 $|W|^2$ 而非作用量中的线性 $|W|$;且一个取向并不唯一固定复结构——相容复结构的选择空间是 $SO(4)/U(2)\cong\mathbb{CP}^1$。$\mathcal N$:未导出——它是钟频与通量之间的字典,其微观来源(§10)决定式 (6.5) 是否可写成 $\lambda k=2$。

在最小 $\mathcal U_\lambda$ ansatz、$\mathcal N$ 固定、且无同阶独立算符三个条件下,次阶相对修正 $\propto(4+k^2)\mathcal N^2\varepsilon/16$ 只依赖 $k$,原则上可破式 (6.5) 的简并;当前既有公式与观测之间约 5% 的数值偏差带内不可分辨(此 5% 是本系列公式与 Planck 导出的宇宙学常数之间的匹配误差,非 Planck 本身的观测不确定度,后者远细于此)。一般 EFT 中该判别还须先证明不存在同阶独立算符。此处所用 $SO(4)/U(2)\cong\mathbb{CP}^1$(相容复结构的选择空间)为标准事实,证明见附录 C 注。

本小节的收束句刻意直白:此匹配关系是目前最简洁的数值目标,但仍是 consilience——𝒩 是字典、λ 是 Wilson 系数,三本账中两本并非纯几何,故未达先验强制。本文的成果不是导出了那个 2,而是精确证明了观测只固定 $\mathcal N^2\lambda k$ 这一个不变量组合,并把三个因子各自的欠定性(几何的、构造的、字典的)分别定位。

6.6 层二:边界完成候选

本文最终架构(§8)不把式 (6.2) 当作 bulk 的最终作用量——那会把两个标记分量重新放回每一次呼吸的 bulk。为杜绝对象漂移,先立一张对象字典。定义钟对导出的过渡面边界内部向量 $$\Xi_{i,n}\equiv\mathcal N\,\frac{M_P\,\omega_{i,n}}{c}\,e_{i,n}\quad(i=1,2),$$ 它不是独立的应力张量,也不是第 $n$ 循环 bulk 中的两支 4-form——它是由两钟及其内部轴在过渡面上给出的边界源数据。据此,层二只继承层一泛函的内部不变量,作为 Crunch–Bang 过渡面上的匹配数据: $$\tfrac12 f_{n+1}^2=\mathcal W_\lambda\big(\Xi_{1,n},\,\Xi_{2,n}\big),\qquad \mathcal W_\lambda(\Xi_1,\Xi_2)\equiv\tfrac12|\Xi_1+\Xi_2|^2+\lambda|\Xi_1\wedge_{\rm int}\Xi_2|,\tag{6.7}$$ 即上一循环倾斜内部对(经边界向量 $\Xi_{i,n}$ 表达)的 wedge 量值,烙印下一循环唯一物理 4-form 通量 $f_{n+1}$ 的大小。全篇对象各归其位:$(\tau_i,\omega_i,e_i)$ 是两钟及其内部轴;$\Xi_i$ 是过渡面上的钟源边界数据;$f_n$ 是第 $n$ 循环唯一的物理 bulk 通量;$\chi_{n,s}=s\ (s\in\{+1,-1\})$ 是第 $n$ 循环内的 face 取向标签。产生式 (6.7) 的完整边界泛函 $\mathcal B_\Sigma$——其约束面映射、辛保持与唯一性——仍是开放问题(§10)。层一证明了"这样的量可以健康地存在",层二指明了"本文把它放在哪里";前者是定理,后者是候选架构,二者不混称。〔层一:结构性(构造存在);层二:动机性架构,构件状态逐一标注于 §8〕

§7 配对、temporal junction 与薄壁 benchmark

带符号账本若有动力学,其载体是改变通量的膜。本节处理三件事:配对膜的跳变代数(精确)、配对方式由何种几何强制(条件定理)、以及 bulk 核化的定性分区(benchmark)。本节自我限定:不完成模型特定的核化率、行列式与全局化;只给薄壁 benchmark 分区。

本节的地位声明(先立)。 以下跳变代数与薄壁 benchmark 诊断的是一个被本文拒作最终地基的形式 signed-bulk 比较分支——即两支通量共处、以差量耦合引力的分支——而非 §8 采用的逐循环单正号通量加边界烙印架构。之所以仍详述,是因为它精确地界定了"若走 bulk 放电会遇到什么",从而反衬边界读法的必要性;但其数值结论不是 boundary-completion candidate 的动力学预言。

反对称$(q,-q)$$0$$-2q$$-qF$(精确)$O(1)$,无 $\varepsilon$ 压低

反对称跳保持平均通量、直接消耗差量:取 $q_*=D/2$ 时一跳恰将当前正差量清零——它是回转/放电的自然候选。但同一代数立即暴露其无止动器:$F$ 在反对称跳下不变,故固定 $q$ 时每跳 $\Delta\widetilde\rho_{\rm diff}=-qF$ 为常数,过零后下一跳不会变慢——runaway 不是引擎,至多是没有止动器的引擎零件;止动须另有来源(有限荷库、核化只许发生于特定面、或泡内塌缩截断)。对称跳则保持差量、漂移平均频率:$\Delta(\omega_1+\omega_2)=2qc/(\mathcal N M_P)$ 而 $\omega_2-\omega_1$ 不动,故 $\varepsilon$ 逐跳微漂——这是螺旋式跨循环参数漂移的自然载体。内部几何为这一分工提供支撑:两内部方向近反平行时 $|e_1+e_2|=2\sin(\varphi/2)\approx\varphi=O(\varepsilon)$ 而 $|e_1-e_2|=2\cos(\varphi/2)\approx2$;若膜荷是内部矢量 $q\,(e_1\pm e_2)$,对称荷天然 $\varepsilon$-小、反对称荷天然 $O(1)$——"螺旋小步、放电大步"不是外加的分派,而是内部几何的模长。〔跳变代数:结构性;分工的几何支撑:结构性,条件于 §6 的内部构造〕

命题 7.1(不动点强制对称配对)。 若过渡面 $W$ 是交换对合 $\sigma$ 的不动点集,且膜的 WZ 作用量 $q_1\!\int_W\!B_1+q_2\!\int_W\!B_2$ 在交换下不变,则 $q_1=q_2$。假设: $\sigma$ 为 $t\mapsto-t$ 型反射;$W=\{t=0\}$ 被逐点固定;交换实现 $B_1\leftrightarrow B_2$。证明梗概: 记 $\Omega_M=dt\wedge\Omega_W$、法向 $n\propto dt$;反射下 $\Omega_M\mapsto-\Omega_M$、$n\mapsto-n$,诱导取向 $\Omega_W=\iota_n\Omega_M$ 满足 $\iota_{-n}(-\Omega_M)=\iota_n\Omega_M$——不动面的内禀取向不翻转;故 $\int_Wi^B_1\mapsto\int_Wi^B_2$ 无负号,交换不变强制 $q_1=q_2$。定义域外的逃生口: 类时 bulk 膜——若另加"交换反转其世界体内禀取向"这一几何条件(它不由"两侧时间箭头相反"单独保证,须明写为假设),则强制反对称 $(q,-q)$;$\pm t_0$ 处的镜像膜对——交换对称只保证每个事件有映像伙伴、荷谱对称、瞬子作用量相等,不保证同一历史中共同核化(共同核化须 $\sigma$-不变态、边界条件强制、或把两面定义为同一非局域复合 junction 之一)。〔结构性,条件明标〕

边界配对映射(P0 级类型说明)。 命题 7.1 的 WZ 项以 $B_1,B_2$ 写出,但在最终单 bulk-flux 架构中(§8)只有一支物理通量 $f_n$,$B_1,B_2$ 并非该架构的 bulk 场;故须给一条有类型的边界映射,而非直接把 §7 作用于 $(f_1,f_2)$ 的跳变代数搬入最终架构。设过渡面上的配对作用量 $$S_\Sigma^{\rm pair}=\int_\Sigma\big(q_1\,b_{1,\Sigma}+q_2\,b_{2,\Sigma}\big),$$ 其中 $b_{i,\Sigma}$ 是过渡面上与两个边界内部方向 $e_{i,n}$ 相伴的三维势(其是否为某 bulk 3-form 的拉回、抑或纯边界自由度,属 $\mathcal B_\Sigma$ 的开放内容)。此映射须回答四问方能支撑螺旋:$b_{i,\Sigma}$ 究竟是什么;是否为 bulk 场的拉回;单 bulk-flux 架构中为何仍存在两个边界 charge channel(答案候选:两个 channel 对应边界内部对 $\Xi_{1,n},\Xi_{2,n}$ 的两个方向,而非两支 bulk 通量);以及 $q_1=q_2$ 如何经该映射推出 $\bar\omega$ 的漂移。在这条映射被写全之前,最诚实的处理是:不动点交换强制等荷($q_1=q_2$,命题 7.1)保留为结构结论,而"等荷导致 $\bar\omega$ 螺旋漂移"降为待完整匹配律验证的候选。〔等荷:结构性;等荷→漂移:候选(映射未闭)〕

由此得到本文最诚实的净图,三行分立:对称 $(q,+q)$ 配对——等荷由不动点交换强制(命题 7.1),到 $\omega_i$ 漂移(螺旋)的边界映射未闭,故为候选;反对称 $(q,-q)$ 放电——住类时 bulk 膜或另一种非固定过渡缺陷,住所未定;turnaround 动力学——不能由循环末端的固定面直接触发,开放。 循环末端的过渡面至多烙印下一循环的初始通量、令下一循环携带微异参数;它不是本循环回转的动态触发器。〔三行表:第一行等荷结构性、其漂移候选;第二、三行开放〕

Temporal junction 的正名。 类空面上的 WZ 积分 $q\int_WB$ 作为有向三维边界积分保持为实,对 $B$ 变分确给出时间前后的通量跳变;有问题的是张力项——标准类时膜的 $-\sigma\int\sqrt{-\det h}$ 在类空诱导度规下变为虚数,弦论中的类空膜须经双重 Wick 旋转等方式另行定义(Gutperle & Strominger 2002)。故本文不借"S-brane 核化"之名,而称此对象为 temporal junction(时间向匹配面):一个实的边界生成泛函,在该时间面上给正则动量以跳变;它不是标准的因果膜世界体,不自动携带 Coleman 反弹或膜张力的通常概率解释。这一正名对 §8 的架构反而有利:边界表述本就无须假装它是 bulk 中可核化的实体。〔方法论〕

薄壁 benchmark 的定性分区(仅诊断被拒的 signed-bulk 分支)。 重申本节开头的地位声明:以下 benchmark 只诊断被本文拒作最终地基的形式 signed-bulk 分支,不是 boundary-completion candidate 的动力学预言;相应地,"若 bulk 核化冻结则本循环 $\Lambda$ 恒定"这类陈述在最终架构中是多余的——最终架构本就没有这个 bulk 膜通道,一次呼吸内 $\Lambda$ 恒定直接来自单支通量不被 bulk 膜改变。若反对称膜确实存在于该比较分支,其核化在张力–荷平面上分为三区:低张力区无指数压低或每呼吸事件过多(过活跃);一条极窄的"每呼吸约一次"引擎带;其上为冻结(薄壁引力效应参照 Coleman & De Luccia 1980)。三个定性结论可陈述而不依赖细节:其一,引擎带所要求的膜能标落在暗能量标度附近是量纲必然($\sigma^{1/3}=O(\Delta V^{1/4})$,一旦跃迁能差取观测暗能量量级),不是本框架的独立巧合;其二,任何显著高于该标度的膜张力使 bulk 核化极端冻结——在通常高能 EFT 直觉下,冻结是稳健默认(条件性陈述:无参数先验测度时"generic"不是数学定理);其三,即便每呼吸一次局域泡,也不自动等于全宇宙齐次回转——泡的扩张、逾渗与核化率的时间依赖均属全局化问题,开放。具体数值($\sigma$ 阈、带宽、引力阻断标度)连同其全部假设列于附录 D;其中任何一个都不是结构常数。〔定性三分:结构性(公式层);冻结默认:条件性;全局化:开放〕

§10 结论与下一层钥匙

本文对 dual-4DD 实现问题的回答,按其真实强度收束为三重结果。否定结果是定理: 带符号真空账本不能住进健康单度规 bulk 中两个独立正则应力张量之差;khronon–$C$ 混合系数在锁定的 baseline 与算符分类中构造性欠定。建设性候选: 内部倾角(上游已发表 packing 向量解给出的一阶反平行亏损角)加 wedge 读出,给出一个有下界、无局域传播鬼的存在性见证,在非零分支中量值以 $O(|\varepsilon|)$ 起始、恒非负;总见证泛函的零点是两标记分量等幅严格反平行($\varepsilon=0$ 时量值归零),而账本公理 $\Lambda_1+\Lambda_2=0$ 只由同一循环内 dual pair 两面的零和($\mathscr L_{n,+}+\mathscr L_{n,-}=0$)承载;量值逐循环继承/漂移是独立的 cycle 映射候选 $\rho_{n+1}=\mathcal M(\rho_n,\Xi_{1,n},\Xi_{2,n})$,不属于该恒等式;最小一致架构把量值放在过渡面烙印、把 dual-face 符号放在 face 取向账本。欠定性度量是本文的真正成果: 我们没有导出那个因子 2——我们精确证明了观测只固定 $\mathcal N^2\lambda k$ 这一个不变量组合,并把三个因子各自的欠定性——字典的($\mathcal N$)、几何的($k$)、构造的($\lambda$)——分别定位到可指认的开放问题上。一个框架的诚实,不在宣称推导了多少,而在能否把"由结构给出的"与"仍依赖归一、响应与读出的"划出可核查的边界。

下一层的钥匙,列出而不假装已经拧开:$k$ 的唯一化(宇宙钟对 packing 约束集的判定);$\lambda$ 的钉法(含"何种结构强制非零 wedge 分支");$\mathcal N$ 的微观来源(它决定中央关系是 $\lambda k=2$ 还是一般式);倾角三次响应 $k_3$(一切三次层结论的前提);标记分解与内部动能矩阵的结构(交叉项系数为何为一);$|W|$ cusp 的平滑或 spurion 完成;内部轴与 wedge 扇区的线性扰动稳定性;完整匹配泛函 $\mathcal B_\Sigma$(含 cycle 匹配候选 $\rho_{n+1}=\mathcal M(\rho_n,\Xi_{1,n},\Xi_{2,n})$ 的推导、边界配对映射 $S_\Sigma^{\rm pair}$ 的闭合、约束面映射、辛保持与唯一性);核化率与回转的全局化;对称过渡面与反对称放电是否需要两个不同的缺陷;以及四力系列篇 VII 的版本升级。这些不是本文的疏漏,而是本文划出的边界。
