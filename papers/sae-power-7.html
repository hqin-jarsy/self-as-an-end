Abstract

From the Prequel through Paper 6, the whole of this series argues at one time-layer: the present structural layer. This paper is the final one, and for the first time the series enters the event layer to complete the full structural account of the dissolution of power. Dissolution is the loss, by a registered power relation, of the capacity to reproduce its original asymmetric topology in its original form; that loss must be given by the relation's own maintenance and registration dynamics. There are two species. Dissolution-by-completion is the demand-side terminal closure that follows when a cultivating power has finished developing the one under power, the positional difference dissolving of itself. Dissolution-by-breakdown is the failure-mode terminal closure of a severing structure's maintenance and registration dynamics. This paper redefines resistance as load-bearing development and cuts it apart from dissolution: the remainder's force of development is entailed jointly by L1, L2 and P6, it is a must, not a want, and resistance is not a necessary condition of dissolution. The paper builds a maintenance-gap and terminal-coordinate model, reading dissolution on two readable faces, the ledger-face and the registration-face, and proves the convergence identity by which the one-under-power's backlash and the power-holder's reflexive-binding are, on the event-layer ledger, the demand component and the supply component of one and the same maintenance gap: power is dissolved by the development it itself drives, whether by structure, when by many factors. The event-layer cascade runs along three conduits: recognition network, compensation chain, and apparatus-substrate. Salvage and remnant-structure take up what follows the event: salvage yields the inventory of a re-formation space, not the result of any re-formation. The whole closes at the 15DD voting-position: at one and the same position, read against others' rights it is power, read against others' power it is a right. The three theorems close here; power theory concludes, and the rights-theory series takes over from the T5 seam. This paper neither predicts, encourages, nor evaluates any concrete political event: structural conditions are the object of argument, the occurrence of events is not the object of prophecy.

§I Introduction

From the Prequel through Paper 6 this series has completed the structural machine of power at one and the same time-layer: origin, morphology, recognition, limit, development, severing, all held at the present structural layer. The account is booked in the present; its negativity needs no resister and awaits no day of reckoning (P6 §VI.6). This paper is the series' final one, and also its single change of layer: it enters the event layer. Collapse occurs, the compensation chain unlatches, recognition reversal propagates, an exogenous load strikes, the substrate runs dry — these are events. P6 has set in place the structural ground of every one of these event lines; the work of this paper is the event dynamics themselves. The task runs along P1 §7, the dominant-layer migration proposition, and §8, the three-theorem closure: to complete the full structural account of the dissolution of power, with the full structural account of breakdown (P1 §9) contained as its main body. In the same register as the whole series, this paper borrows no thermodynamic irreversibility apparatus, no philosophy of history, and no moral judgment.

The change of layer is the series' single greatest opening for misreading, so the clarifications come first, and they are more numerous than in any of the first six papers.

Clarification one, the place of dissolution. Dissolution is not failure, not victory, not exit, not physical collapse. Dissolution is a genus: the loss, by a registered power relation, of the capacity to reproduce its original asymmetric topology in its original form. Under the genus stand two species, dissolution-by-completion and dissolution-by-breakdown; one is development run to its end, the other is a failure of maintenance, and neither carries value. The end of power, in this series, is not a day of moral reckoning.

Clarification two, the place of resistance. Resistance is not revolt, not a class of action, not a posture. Resistance is load-bearing development: the one under power, unfolding its own remainder under pressure, is developing. Pressure is a universal of all power; cultivation, too, is pressure. Revolt is only one manifest possibility of resistance under particular structural conditions; there can be resistance within compliance, resistance within silence, and the criterion is given in §III, not on the posture.

Clarification three, the framing. The central case of this paper is the one under power who does not exit but develops within the relation. Presence is the framing, not a constituent of resistance: a one-under-power who develops while exiting is still resisting, only not inside this paper's main line. The escape and migration of the L2 boundary-crossing state were already registered by P4; this paper draws them as a boundary and not as the main line.

Clarification four, the place of value. The two species hold equal ontological standing (P5 C2): dissolution-by-completion is not the success branch, dissolution-by-breakdown is not the failure branch, collapse is not punishment, and salvage is not retribution. The value lies in the direction, not in the remainder (P4), and not in the terminus either: what can be judged is the structure's disposition (judged by T6, P6), not the structure's outcome. To read the terminus as a day of reckoning is to read this paper back into philosophy of history, which is the first thing the task excludes.

Clarification five, the event-layer defense. The paper holds four things throughout. First, modal discipline: every criterion takes the conditional-structural form — if the event occurs, its structural form, order of transmission, and registration state must be thus; or, the event's mechanism of occurrence is thus, with threshold-reachability given by structure. No criterion takes a predictive form; no sentence can be rewritten as "such-and-such a structure will, under such-and-such a condition, produce such-and-such an event." Whether is given by structure, when by many factors (P1 §7) — this construction runs through the whole. Second, two-way non-prescription: the statement of a dissolution mechanism is neither a manual for subversion nor a manual for stabilization; the ledger is equally readable by the locker and by the reverser, and is therefore equally unreadable as either one's operating manual. Third, hygiene of time and anchor: events take generic time, when the threshold is crossed, not calendar time; anchors take a structural type, not a named instance, kept very short, and not entering the main line of argument. Fourth, the division of layers: P6 holds the present structural layer, this paper holds the event layer; structural conditions are the object of argument, the occurrence of events is not the object of prophecy; this paper does not write back onto P6, whose negativity is a structural fact dependent on no event, and which this paper does not rewrite into a prophecy of events.

§II The genus and species of dissolution

§II.1 The genus definition and the source clause

Dissolution is the loss, by a registered power relation, of the capacity to reproduce its original asymmetric topology in its original form. Every component of the definition bears load. Registered: the object of dissolution is a power relation that stands by way of T2 registration; without registration there is nothing to dissolve. Original asymmetric topology: what is dissolved is that particular positional-difference structure of A over B, not order or organization in general. In its original form: a topology may deform, may give way, may be succeeded by a new topology, and none of these is a counterexample to dissolution — dissolution asks only whether the original form can still reproduce. Capacity to reproduce: a power relation is not a state once registered and valid forever, but a loop that must reproduce at every beat, the maintaining action being non-omittable (P2, P6); dissolution is the loss of this reproducing loop's capacity, not a single beat's malfunction.

The genus definition needs a source clause, without which it loses its boundary: any termination would enter dissolution. The loss of capacity must be given by the relation's own maintenance and registration dynamics, and there are only three paths: demand-side completion, a cultivating power's development run to its end, the positional difference no longer having a demand side; ledger-face failure, the failure of the maintenance ledger, given in §IV; registration-face failure, the field's no longer registering the relation under its original topology, given in §IV. A unilateral act of registration is not among the three: A may withdraw (P1 §6; P6 §VI.2, the second level), B may exit (P4 L2, the boundary-crossing state's departure), and the relation thereby terminates — but this is not dissolution, it is going off-register. The difference between off-register and dissolution is not in the outcome, since the loop terminates either way, but in the source: off-register is a unilateral act of registration, dissolution is a dynamical terminal closure. Hence a consequence-note: at dissolution the maintaining loop must terminate, but termination of the loop is not sufficient for dissolution.

§II.2 The two species

Under the genus stand two species, divided by terminal account. Dissolution-by-completion (P5 C3) is the demand-side terminal closure: a cultivating power develops the one under power until the positional difference no longer holds, the local grid is released, the relation runs to the dissolution of the positional difference, the account booked on the demand side, the demand completed. Its dynamics are already established by P5 (C2 third paragraph, C3); this paper only sets its genus-membership and its terminal-index position (§IX), and does not reopen it. Dissolution-by-breakdown is the failure-mode terminal closure of maintenance and registration dynamics: a severing structure's maintenance demand crosses its castable supply, or the field no longer registers the relation under its original topology, the account booked on failure. Dissolution-by-breakdown is this paper's main body.

P5 §VIII's cut is preserved verbatim under the genus: dissolution-by-completion is not dissolution-by-breakdown, the two run in opposite directions. The precise form of that sentence is now an inter-species distinction: same genus, different species, different account. The setting-up of the genus has altered no published criterion; it merely gives each of the two already-registered terminations a genus-membership.

The two arrival paths of dissolution-by-breakdown are registered here in advance, their coordinates in §IV and their convergence in §V. The development path: the one under power outgrows its original script within the relation, the old locking can no longer register it, power is dissolved by development. The hold-out path: the structure refuses to upgrade and maintenance fails first (P6 §V rigidification, S5 threshold). The two paths belong to the same species, breakdown, in no order of priority, and a real trajectory is always a mixture.

§II.3 The mirror of off-register and framing

Off-register and this paper's framing are mirror-symmetric. The framing keeps in view the relation whose dynamics still run, the one under power who does not exit but develops within the relation (§I clarification three); what it draws away is the relation terminated by a unilateral act of registration, A's withdrawal and B's exit. The mirror is no disparagement of off-register: withdrawal and exit are real terminations, only they do not belong to the genus of dissolution. The apparatus after a withdrawal may be recalled by a new 13DD hand, and the one under power after an exit may enter a new relation; these are new registrations, T2 re-instituted, neither the dissolution of the original relation nor its continuation.

§II.4 Topological death and physical persistence

Dissolution is a topological event, not a physical one. P2 §IV.5's cut is in place at the genus definition: topological power-death and the physical persistence of the apparatus are strictly separated. Bureaucratic buildings, written rules, algorithmic systems, position-sequences may all continue to exist physically and even to run after dissolution, and this does not constitute non-dissolution; conversely, the physical dismantling of an apparatus does not constitute dissolution either, so long as the original topology can still reproduce through a new apparatus. The criterion is the reproduction of the topology, not the persistence of the material. The full operationalization of this cut is in §VIII: a persisting apparatus is a remnant-structure, raising the dead requires T2 re-instituted, and an apparatus with no 13DD call withdraws by M2 into an 11- or 12DD physical phenomenon. The genus level sets one sentence only: what is dissolved is the relation, not the thing.

§II.5 The genus is flat

The two species hold equal ontological standing, the promise of the P5 C2 exit bifurcation extended to the genus: dissolution-by-completion is not the success branch, dissolution-by-breakdown is not the failure branch. This is not rhetorical restraint but a structural fact: there is no place for value in the genus definition, and the loss of reproductive capacity does not distinguish the dignified from the undignified. The value that can be judged is elsewhere, in the structure's disposition (judged by T6), not in the structure's outcome. The breakdown of a severing structure is not punishment, and the completion-dissolution of a cultivating power is not reward. Every event mechanism that follows in this paper runs under this flat genus: the ledger does not adjudicate reward or punishment; it only registers which maintaining loop cannot pay.

§III Resistance

§III.1 Load-bearing development

Resistance is load-bearing development: the one under power, unfolding its own remainder under pressure, is developing. The definition begins by subtraction. Resistance has no posture as a requirement: it does not require the intention to oppose, does not require anger or refusal, does not require recognizing A as an adversary. Resistance has no object as a requirement: it points at no one, and the development is not aimed at A. Resistance has no success as a requirement: it may be pressed back, it may go unseen, and it is still resistance. From which, revolt is only one manifest possibility of resistance under particular structural conditions, its conditions given in §VI's network conduit; there can be resistance within compliance, so long as B's own law becomes more registrable in the course of complying rather than being eaten by the original script; there can be resistance within silence, so long as the silence is not zero-resistance forward conduction but the holding of a position within the relation. The criterion is not on the type of behavior but on the increment (§III.4).

Pressure is a universal of all power. Under T1, any power that stands alters, limits, and orients the available positions and paths of the one under power; cultivation, too, is pressure, the temporary scaffold being likewise a structural field pushed along by development (P5 C3). Pressure itself carries no value: the value is in the direction, that is, whether the pressure is accompanied by the removal of the interrupt-right and a downward lock (judged by T6), not in the existence of pressure.

§III.2 The source of the force

The remainder's force of development is entailed jointly by L1, L2 and P6, not by L1 alone: L1 gives a static fact, L2 and P6 give dynamical facts, and in motion cannot be derived from present alone. L1 establishes the remainder as an ontological differential, ineliminable, not dependent on awareness (P4): this is there is a remainder, a static statement; from it alone, the most one can do is refute the remainder is eliminable (a differential that can be gathered to zero and held at no cost is not an ineliminable differential, contradicting L1), but refuting eliminability establishes only that the remainder cannot be pressed out of existence, which is a force of resisting compression, not yet a force of development — a remainder at rest yet incompressible would satisfy L1. The force of development must be supplied by dynamical facts, and two published machines each give one: L2, expression cannot be permanently zeroed, the lowest-amplitude leakage persists under even the deepest suppression (P3 §V.4, P4 L2), and persistent low-amplitude leakage is persistent unfolding; P6, the maintaining action is non-omittable, maintenance costs precisely because overflow does not stop (P6 §III), and overflow that does not stop is unfolding that does not stop. These two are evidence for the judgment the force is in motion, not the ontological source of the force. The ontological source remains on B's side, in L1's differential and L2's leakage; P6's "maintenance costs" is a mechanical consequence of B's not-ceasing overflow, cited only because the consequence in turn corroborates the not-ceasing overflow, not because A's maintenance bill is taken as a constituent part of B's force of development. Hence even if at some beat A withdraws and no longer locks, P6's corroboration pausing, L1 and L2 still independently give unfolding-that-does-not-stop, and the force does not vanish on that account. The three together are what make it a must to develop: ineliminable (L1), and not permanently zeroable (L2), and maintenance-costly (P6). Hence the absoluteness of L1, by way of L2 and P6, is necessarily present in the form of a force: the remainder must develop.

This force is a must, not a want. It has no direction: P6's original sentence holds, the remainder exerts force in no direction, the force is present without aim. It has no telos: a must to develop is not a developing-toward-something, development has no terminal station. It carries no value of good or bad, right or wrong: a must to develop simply is dynamics, not merit, not virtue, and not threat. Its per-beat interface reading is exactly P6's already-registered overflow pressure. (What ordinary language calls "vitality," should it appear in this text, can only be reduced to this directionless, telos-free force of development of the remainder, and cannot serve as an explanans; this reduction is of a piece with §IX's drawing of the conduction-norm out of its colloquial name, in the opposite direction, there a pejorative is neutralized, here a colloquial name is given mechanical content.)

§III.3 Pressure and compression

Pressure is not compression. Compression is the reading when structural pressure exceeds the remainder's force of development: at that interface, that beat, the unfolding is pressed back. The reading is instantaneous; turning the reading into a state requires work. The persistence of compression requires the locking action to maintain that inequality beat by beat, and maintaining it beat by beat is exactly the account that T6 bills (P6): every beat casts adjustable degrees of freedom into the pressing action. Under cultivation the inequality is not persistently maintained, the scaffold gives way by design, the temporary in temporary scaffold being the design (P5 C3); under severing the inequality is persistently maintained by the locking action, and compression thereby turns from a reading into a regime feature, its account having an entry on each side: A's side is T6's casting, B's side is the compression of expressive space, what is compressed being expressive space and not the remainder itself, L1 preserved. One mechanics, two regimes, the difference lying entirely in who pays that per-beat account.

§III.4 The increment closure

The development that resistance requires is an increment in B's subject-position or in the registrability of its own law, the interrupt-right standing included; it is not an increment in the output-efficiency of the locked role. This closure excludes a class of misreading: B under pressure becomes better at playing the assigned role, more efficient at adapting to the lock, smoother at running the return-map — this is an improvement in performance, not an increment in position, and is not resistance. Likewise, a movement that installs a deeper lock is judged otherwise by T6 and is by definition not resistance: a movement that, in the name of overthrowing the locker, installs a deeper return-map on the populace is, under this criterion, severing, not resistance. Resistance is judged by increment, not by banner.

§III.5 Resistance is not a necessary condition of dissolution

P6 §VI.6's cut remains in force: the three lines of negativity, the sediment layer, the narrowing corridor, the draining reserve, all need no resister; a severing structure's account runs toward settlement with or without resistance. What resistance changes is pace and route: load-bearing development raises the calibration of the demand flux (§IV), making the account run faster or run by another road, but it supplies no negativity, the negativity being the structure's own. This is at once a defense: this paper neither pins the hope of dissolution on resistance nor books the responsibility for dissolution on resistance; the account carries its own settlement condition, the settlement condition built into the structure itself.

§III.6 The demand-side climb

Development has a shape. B's load-bearing development climbs along P1's three layered sources: at the mechanical source, development is an increase in available actions and avoidance paths; at the meaning source, the forming of one's own readings and narrative; at the recognition source, the rise in the registrability of one's own law in the presence of the field. The climb raises the calibration of the demand flux (§IV): the same lock, pressing a B that is growing on all three layers, grows costlier layer by layer. One guardrail sentence, bound to §III.2's no-direction clause, without which the climb would be read as intention: the climb is the observer's projective decomposition of development, not B's route of ascent. No-direction does not mean no-structure; a directionless unfolding occurring in a source-space that has a layered structure has its projection fall, of course, onto the layers, and where the projection falls is not where B is heading; the layers are the observer's coordinates, not B's goal, the shape being the shape of the projection, not the shape of intent. This three-layer ladder has two reading-directions: the climb here is the demand direction, §VI's mediator regression is the supply direction, one and the same ladder, on one side B's development raising the calibration of maintenance layer by layer, on the other A's maintaining mediators losing their subsidy layer by layer; the ladder's forming-direction is established by P1 and is registered here without elaboration.

§IV Maintenance gap and terminal coordinates

§IV.1 The maintenance account

Every standing lock has, at every beat, two fluxes. The demand flux is the overflow that must be held down: calibrated by B's state of development (§III.6, the climb), calibrated by network position (the recognition network's subsidies and readings, §VI), calibrated by the maintaining mediator (the three layered mediators have different unit prices, §VI); an exogenous load enters the demand side as an impulse term (§VII). The supply flux is the castable degrees of freedom remaining at the load-bearing node: P6's casting machine is read here as the supply side, what has been cast is no longer available, rigidification being the pre-spending of supply (P6 §V). The difference of the two fluxes is the maintenance gap. The gap is the instantaneous live-account reading of P6's corridor on each trajectory: the corridor is structural-layer geometry, the gap is the per-beat reading of that same geometry at the event layer, this paper uses the reading and does not write back onto the geometry. The asymptote does not appear anywhere: every event in this paper occurs at a reachable place, ρ appears only at the inexhaustibility step of the argument and serves as no quantity.

Dissolution must be read on three mutually independent coordinates.

§IV.3 The ledger-face: two columns and the disjunction

The ledger-face inherits P6's two frontiers, unaltered, read as two columns. The extraction column: extractable gain tends to zero, the extraction-death frontier; the lock still stands in name but can no longer bite anything extractable, the relation dies on the revenue side. The maintenance column: demand flux crosses up through supply flux, the event-layer name of the C2 death-crossing, the collapse frontier; maintenance fails first, the relation dies on the expenditure side. P6 §VI.1's disjunction lives unaltered within the ledger-face: at the end of the narrowing corridor is whichever of the two columns arrives first. From structural layer to event layer the ledger-face undergoes only one conversion: reading the frontier from structural-layer geometry into an event-layer column, the event being the beat at which a column is crossed. The placement of the authority-display form must be fixed here in advance, lest it be confused with §IV.5's coupling sentence: the death of authority-display (signal-mis-meaning) is booked on the ledger-face extraction column, and its quality-source (the subject's identifiability), though supplied by the field, has its identifiability-exhaustion as a consumption of the extraction column's object-quality (P6 final-version register), not as a registration-face re-registration failure; when §IV.5 says authority-display couples the two faces most tightly, it means the same form is exposed to both faces at once, not the same account booked on two faces.

§IV.4 The registration-face: R3 load-bearing

The registration-face is an independent axis. Re-registration failure: the field no longer registers the relation under its original topology, that positional-difference reading of A-as-A and B-as-B no longer reproduced in the recognition network and in the two parties' registration. The load-bearing citation is R3: the operational layer may prepare the conditions for T2 but cannot create the ontological event of T2; registration is an ontological-layer act and cannot be bought. Hence the irreversibility of registration-face failure rests on no account: once the field no longer reads this pair of relations thus, no supply can buy back the original topology; suppression can press down expression, it cannot press back a reading.

§IV.5 Mutual entailment, orthogonality, the exemplar

The two faces mutually entail, and the simultaneity here is logical, not temporal. Bidirectional logical sufficiency means: the failure of either face is enough to make J1's loss of reproductive capacity hold, independent of whether the two are simultaneous on a clock, neither requiring nor presupposing that the two faces fail at the same physical time. Where the registration-face fails, re-registration's non-repurchasability makes the maintenance demand impossible to price: no casting can buy a registration that no longer exists, the gap at that point is necessarily unpayable, and the ledger-face fails with it. Where the ledger-face fails, the topology has nothing to reproduce from, and the presence or absence of registration becomes irrelevant: how the field reads a relation that cannot maintain itself no longer constitutes that relation's persistence. The two directions are not symmetric, one bringing about the other face's failure too, the other rendering the other face irrelevant, but each is sufficient for J1's loss of reproductive capacity, and this is the bidirectional sufficiency. Two different firsts must be kept apart and not run together: the arriving-first is the question of which of the ledger-face's two internal columns (extraction and maintenance) is crossed first, a temporal measure, holding within the ledger-face; bidirectional sufficiency is the question between the two readable faces, ledger-face and registration-face, a logical sufficiency, not on that pair of column coordinates. The disjunction is thereby not two deaths but one death, by which readable face it first breaks through.

The arrival path is the third coordinate, orthogonal to the faces. The development path typically shows itself first on the registration-face: B outgrows its script, the field re-reads B, the lock can no longer register. The hold-out path typically shows itself first on the ledger-face: maintenance fails first. Typical is not necessary: a development trajectory can equally end on the ledger-face, a grown B making the lock costlier beat by beat until the crossing; a hold-out terminus equally renders registration irrelevant. The path does not lock the face, and this is the geometric premise of §V's polar parameterization.

Signal-mis-meaning is the ready exemplar of the two faces' coupling (P6 §VI.1). The extraction column of the authority-display form is signal-currency, and signal-currency itself depends on the field's reading: how the bystander reads that display decides whether that display earns anything. The ledger-face and the registration-face couple most tightly here, and this is also the geometric reason §VI lists the recognition-network conduit as the first of the cascade.

§V The convergence identity

§V.1 Same ledger, not the same thing

Dissolution is fed by two branches, this being P6 §VII's handoff: the one-under-power's backlash propagating through the recognition network, the power-holder's reflexive-binding crushing from within. Under that identity, juxtaposition is impossible on the ledger: to speak of the gap is to speak of both components at once, and there is no gap with only one side booked.

§V.2 Polarity and mixture

The two arrival paths of breakdown present, on the maintenance account, as two polar drivers of one and the same death-crossing. The development path is demand-side ascent dominant: B's climb raises the calibration beat by beat, the crossing reached by demand catching up to supply. The hold-out path is supply-side descent dominant: casting pre-spends supply beat by beat, the crossing reached by supply falling through demand. Polarity is parameterization, not classification: a real trajectory is always a mixture, a grown B and a rigidified A usually on one and the same trajectory. On which readable face the terminus falls is given by §IV's coordinates; the path does not lock the face.

§V.3 The genetic lock

Convergence does not stop at accounting; it goes as deep as genesis. By C1, development is a structural driver (P5): any successfully running social power structure triggers a dominant-layer migration pressure in the populace, severing and cultivating alike being development drivers, differing in rate and mode of maintenance, not in whether they drive. That is, the demand-side development term is not external: it is the product of the power structure's own successful running, this being C1's whether, while by which concrete channel it drives is the many-factored how, which this paper neither elaborates nor predicts. The development that drives dissolution here must be distinguished from §III's resistance: the development here is C1's populace-level dominant-layer development, accumulated from all the successfully running power structures across the whole populace (cultivation included, and the already-exited and already-migrated B included); resistance is the in-presence load-bearing development defined in §III, only the one branch of that populace-level development which B contributes by not exiting but staying in the pressure field. The former is strictly larger than the latter, so the account accumulates even without any single B's resistance (§III.5), in parallel with and not in contradiction to power is dissolved by development; the development in the two sentences is not one quantity. And by the T6 note (P6): under dominant-layer migration pressure, when upgrading is refused, reflexive-binding turns from optional trigger to necessary trigger. The two published criteria together give this paper's load-bearing sentence: power is dissolved by the development it itself drives. The modality of this sentence must be fixed on the spot: whether, given by structure, this being the structural necessity given by C1 and the T6 note; when, given by many factors, this paper making no temporal prophecy and rendering no judgment on any concrete structure.

The event-layer settlement of the gap does not occur at one place; it presents along three conduits. The three conduits are not three dissolutions but one and the same settlement presenting at three sites of the structure; the subscripts serve downstream addressing.

§VI.1 J5.1 The recognition-network conduit

The maintaining mediators form a subsidy series along P1's three layered sources. The higher the recognition mediator's layer, the more it can shoulder, by network registration, A's visible maintaining action: at the reputation mediator, R4's zero-resistance forward conduction is the bystander maintaining on A's behalf, A scarcely needing to act beat by beat; the narrative mediator is in the middle, M2's borrowed-position legitimacy letting apparatus and writ stand guard for A; the mechanical mediator has no subsidy, A casting directly beat by beat. This is a structural tendency, not a quantitative ranking. What recognition reversal withdraws is the mediator subsidy: the account presents as the maintenance unit-price rising continuously along the regression series, the higher mediator failing, maintenance sliding toward a lower layer, and at each slide more cast per beat. This is the supply-direction reading of P1 §5's three-layer continuous transition and P1 §3's multi-layer superposition in the dissolution direction, and is the same ladder as §III.6's demand-direction climb in two reading-directions.

A single-point reversal is double-booked: one forward subsidy withdrawn, one reverse reading to be managed added; one mechanism, booked once, and wherever later text speaks of the costliness of reversal, it refers to this single entry. Erasure as a network-side countermeasure is itself an added lock: at the limit, R3's withdrawal is the limit of locking, suppressing a reversal necessarily raising the casting rate, the account of the countermeasure booked under the account it counters. The authority-display form, its output endpoint falling entirely on the recognition network (P6), is the shortest entry for reversal: the same display, read forward as authority, read in reverse as a weakness signal, the two-sidedness of signal-currency leaving that form unscreened against this conduit's cascade. One hygiene point throughout: the C-network's reversal is only a change in the object of registration, what it once registered being authority, what it later registers being the cost of locking or the unfitness; this is a topological change, not a moral event.

§VI.2 J5.2 The compensation-chain conduit

The maintenance of a complex severing form is divided along a compensation chain (P6 §VI.4). This paper gives the latch an event-layer definition: a latch is a node that translates an upstream lock into downstream pressure and thereby obtains compensation sufficient to maintain its own subject-position; the chain is strung from latches. To unlatch is for compensation no longer to maintain that translating action: not that the node's attitude toward the upstream changed, but that the node's available subjecthood-output and the translating task it bears have fallen out of fit, the translating action itself no longer feasible. After the bottom-of-chain inequality fails, extractable gain falling below the lowest locker's compensation demand (P6 §VI.4), the unlatching propagates from the bottom up: compensation depends upward while maintenance executes downward, one link's unlatching being one delegated supply going off-register, the prior link's account growing costlier with it. Direction and cascade are given by the geometry of the account, this being a structural sentence; when the breakpoint is reached is given by many factors, on which this paper renders no judgment. P6 holds the gradient, this paper holds the event: how the gradient manufactures the bottom of the chain into the least-stable point is established by P6; this paper writes only the beat at which a latch breaks and its upward propagation.

§VII Exogenous load

An exogenous load establishes no criterion in this paper: that fact is itself the statement of its standing. The load is a trigger, not a cause; a trigger does not merit a criterion, and it occupies only an impulse term on J3's demand side.

What this paper takes up is only the strike: how a car with no suspension comes apart when it hits an external stone. The coming-apart occurs not at the point of impact but at the point of lowest recombinative capacity: the load is a recombination test, and the rigidified geometry (P6 §V) has already given the distribution of the test's answers, the most deeply cast load-bearing node having the lowest recombinative capacity, the structural conditions of the breakthrough point given by P6, not by the load. Negativity is not increased by one: this paper books no dissolution on the stone, and books no account on luck for any structure.

§IX Closure and seam

§IX.1 The three-theorem closure

The closure previewed in P1 §8 is discharged here, in the same direction as P1 §8. T3 gives the demand term: the remainder gives the structural root of resistance, drift, escape, misreading, backlash, migration and the like as expressions, but the remainder itself is not equal to any one of those expressions (P4 L1, L2); this demand term is inexhaustible and global, so within an undissolved local power grid, so long as some grid still requires maintenance in its original form, the demand flux does not go to zero, what dissolution-by-completion completes being that local positional difference's demand on the maintaining loop, not the absolute remainder, nor the termination of the subject's development. T6 gives the supply term: reflexive-binding accumulates only while locking and is non-dilutable, so the supply flux is, under a severing regime, monotonically pre-spent. T5 gives the seam: the degree to which a position is preserved is the condition of power's standing, and is also where all its readable faces lie. The maintenance-gap model (J3) is jointly entailed by T3 and T6: with an inexhaustible demand and a supply that will be pre-spent, there is a gap and its event layer. The three theorems bind at dissolution; the closure is not the juxtaposition of three papers but the three signatures on one account.

§IX.2 The four termini

The terminus of a power relation is indexed fourfold by T5. Demand-side completion, that is dissolution-by-completion; the failure of both faces, that is dissolution-by-breakdown; going off-register, that is the boundary, A's withdrawal or B's exit; the institutionalized double-reading, that is the seam-exit, the position neither dying nor going off-register but fixed on the double-reading of power and right. The first two are dissolution. The four termini are not equally reachable from different starting forms: a locked relation reaching the seam must first pass through the release branch, the double-reading requiring the position to bear the interrupt-right standing, of which locking is precisely the removal. The four termini are an index, not a menu, and are not to be read as four equally reachable exits.

§IX.3 The voting-position

This paper's so-called 15DD voting right is, first, a structural position, the voting-position: the institutionalized position at which a subject retains the interrupt-right standing within a group pressure field. Whether and how an actual institution approaches this structural position is not within this paper's evaluation; the anchor is a structural type, not an institutional instance.

At this position, the T5 duality is institutionalized into the double-reading of one and the same thing. At one and the same position, read against others' right it is power: my one vote takes part in changing the common outcome. Read against others' power it is a right: the majority of others cannot fold my position into an uninterrupted forward conduction. One and the same vote: against the common outcome it is power, against group pressure it is a right. The position is fixed on the double-reading and persists with no lock required: what maintains it is not casting but mutual registration itself, this being the institutionalized form of 15DD mutual registration (P1 §5). The no-lock here must be distinguished from §III.3's T6 billing, with which it does not conflict: what T6 bills is unidirectional locking, A unilaterally pressing B into the return-map, requiring A to cast beat by beat; what the voting-position maintains is mutual registration, no party unidirectionally pressing down the other, any party's attempt at unidirectional locking being pushed back by the other's interrupt-right standing. No-lock is not no-maintenance but no unidirectional-locking casting; the cost of maintenance is distributed by the symmetric structure itself, borne by no party alone, and this is precisely where 15DD mutual registration differs from 13DD unidirectional locking.

The resistance and dissolution at the anchor are read by this paper's criteria. Public-opinion pressure is the group C-network's pressure on the individual B, a universal, not necessarily ill; its severing form is the interpretation-locking of S1's fourth level: not forbidding B to vote, but fixing B's interpretive return-map of its own reasons for voting. Resistance is the one-under-pressure developing its own readings under this pressure, with no posture of opposition required, the increment in the registrability of the reading, J2's closure applying. What is dissolved is the zero-resistance forward-conduction group norm (R4; colloquial language might call it blind following, a term this paper does not use hereafter): its dissolution consists in the change in the distribution of conductive resistance, not in mobilization; a mobilization that installs a new lock is judged otherwise by T6, that being a new severing, not the dissolution here.

The voting-position is only one symmetric coincidence of the T5 seam, not the end of history, not a moral victory; this paper evaluates no institution's merit, gives no institutional recommendation, and neither predicts, encourages, nor evaluates any concrete political event.

前置篇至 P6, 本系列的全部论证持同一个时层: 此刻结构层。本篇为终篇, 系列首次进入事件层, 完成权力之解的完整结构论。解是已登记权力关系的原非对称拓扑失去以原形再生产的能力, 其丧失须由关系自身的维持与登记动力学给出; 属下两种, 消解是涵育性权力发展走完后需求侧的完成式终止, 瓦解是凿削性结构维持与登记动力学的失守式终止。本篇重定抗为承压的发展, 与瓦解拆开: 余项发展之力由 L1, L2 与 P6 合起来蕴含, 是一个不得不, 不是一个想要, 抗不是瓦解的必要条件。本篇立维持缺口与终局坐标系, 在账本面与登记面两个可读面上读瓦解, 证 B 侧反噬与 A 侧反缚在事件层账本上的合流恒等式: 权力被它自己驱动的发展所瓦解, 是否由结构定, 何时由多因素定。事件层级联沿承认网络, 补偿链, 装置构层三道展开; 清算与遗构接住瓦解之后, 清算给出再形成空间的库存而不给出再形成的结果。全篇终于 15DD 投票位: 同一位置相对他人的权利读出来是权力, 相对他人的权力读出来是权利, 三定理在此闭环, 权力论收束, 权利论系列自 T5 接缝接走。本篇不预测不鼓励不评价任何具体政治事件: 结构条件是论证对象, 事件发生不是预言对象。

§I 引言

前置篇至 P6, 本系列在同一个时层上完成了权力的结构机器: 起源, 形态, 承认, 限度, 发展, 凿削 (severing), 全部持此刻结构层。账此刻就记着, 否定性不需要反抗者, 不等任何清算日 (P6 §VI.6)。本篇是系列终篇, 也是系列唯一一次换层: 进入事件层。崩塌发生, 补偿链解扣 (unlatch), 承认翻转扩散, 外生载荷撞击, 基质枯竭见底, 这些是事件; P6 已把每一条事件线的结构根据立好, 本篇的工作是事件动力学本身。任务沿 P1 §7 主导层迁移命题与 §8 三定理闭环展开: 完成权力之解的完整结构论, 瓦解的完整结构论 (P1 §9) 作为主体被包含。与全系列同一口径, 本篇不借热力学不可逆工具, 不借历史哲学, 不借道德判断。

换层是本系列误读敞口最大的一步, 故廓清前置, 且多于前六篇任何一篇。

廓清一, 解的位置。解不是失败, 不是胜利, 不是退出, 不是物理崩塌。解是属: 已登记权力关系的原非对称拓扑失去以原形再生产的能力。属下两个种, 消解与瓦解, 一个是发展走完, 一个是失守, 都不带价。权力的终点在本系列里不是道德结算日。

廓清二, 抗的位置。抗不是反抗, 不是一类行动, 不是一种姿态。抗是承压的发展: B 在压力下展开自身余项 (remainder) 即是发展。压力是一切权力的共相, 涵育 (cultivating) 也是压力。反抗只是抗在特定结构条件下的显态可能之一; 顺从之中可以有抗, 沉默之中可以有抗, 判据在 §III 给出, 不在姿态上。

廓清三, 取景。本篇中心案例是不执行退出而在关系内发展的 B。在场是取景, 不是抗的构成要件: 一个边退出边发展的 B 仍在抗, 只是不在本篇主线内。L2 越界态的逃逸与迁移已由 P4 登记, 本篇划为边界, 不作主线。

廓清四, 价的位置。两种解同等本体地位 (P5 C2): 消解不是成功支, 瓦解不是失败支, 崩塌不是惩罚, 清算不是报应。价在方向不在余项 (P4), 也不在终局: 可判的是结构的取向 (T6 判, P6), 不是结构的结局。把终局读成结算日, 是把本篇读回历史哲学, 那是任务句排除的第一项。

廓清五, 事件层防线。本篇全程守四件。其一, 模态纪律: 每一条判据都取条件结构式, 若事件发生, 其结构形态, 传导次序与登记状态必为如此, 或, 事件的发生机制如此, 阈值可达性由结构给出; 没有一条判据取预言式, 没有任何一句可改写为某结构将在某条件下发生某事件; 是否由结构定, 何时由多因素定 (P1 §7), 此句式贯穿全篇。其二, 双向非处方: 瓦解机制的陈述既不是颠覆手册, 也不是维稳手册; 账本对锁定者与翻转者同样可读, 故同样不可读作任何一方的操作指南。其三, 时间与锚点卫生: 事件取类属时间, 当阈值被穿时, 而非日历时间; 锚点取结构类型, 不取具名个例, 极短, 不进论证主链。其四, 层的分工: P6 持此刻结构层, 本篇持事件层; 结构条件是论证对象, 事件发生不是预言对象; 本篇不回写 P6, P6 的否定性是结构事实, 不依赖任何事件, 本篇不把它改写成事件预言。

§II 解的属与种

§II.1 属定义与来源条款

解是已登记权力关系的原非对称拓扑失去以原形再生产的能力。定义里每个成分都承重。已登记: 解的对象是经 T2 登记而成立的权力关系, 没有登记就没有可解之物。原非对称拓扑: 被解的是那个特定的 A 对 B 的位置差结构, 不是泛指的秩序或组织。以原形: 拓扑可以变形, 可以让位, 可以被新拓扑接替, 这些都不是解的反例, 解只问原形能否继续再生产。再生产能力: 权力关系不是一次登记永久有效的状态, 是每一拍都须再生产的回路, 维持动作不可省 (P2, P6); 解是这个再生产回路的能力丧失, 不是某一拍的故障。

属定义须有来源条款, 否则失界: 任何终止都将入解。解的能力丧失须由关系自身的维持与登记动力学给出, 路只有三条: 需求侧完成, 涵育性权力发展走完, 位差不再有需求侧; 账本面失守, 维持账本的失守, §IV 给出; 登记面失守, 场不再按原拓扑登记该关系, §IV 给出。单方的登记动作不在三条之内: A 可以退场 (P1 §6; P6 §VI.2 第二层), B 可以退出 (P4 L2 越界态离场), 关系就此终止, 但这不是解, 是离册。离册与解的区别不在结果, 回路都终止了, 而在来源: 离册是单方登记动作, 解是动力学结局。由此一个后果注: 解处维持回路必终止, 但回路终止不充分于解。

§II.2 两个种

属下两个种, 按终止科目分。消解 (P5 C3) 是需求侧的完成式终止: 涵育性权力把 B 发展到位差不再成立, 局域网格解除, 关系朝位差消解走完, 科目记在需求侧, 需求完成了。其动力学 P5 已立 (C2 第三段, C3), 本篇只立其属籍与终局索引位 (§IX), 不重开。瓦解是维持与登记动力学的失守式终止: 凿削性结构的维持需求越过其可铸供给, 或场不再按原拓扑登记该关系, 科目记在失守上。瓦解为本篇主体。

P5 §VIII 的切分在属下原样保住: 消解不是瓦解, 两者方向相反。这句话的精确形式现在是种间区分: 同属, 不同种, 不同科目。属的设立没有改动任何已发布判据, 它只是给两个已登记的终止各自一个属籍。

瓦解的两条到达路径在此先行登记, 坐标在 §IV, 合流在 §V。发展路径: B 在关系内长过原脚本, 旧锁定无法继续登记 B, 权力被发展所瓦解。死扛路径: 结构拒绝升级而维持先垮 (P6 §V 僵化, S5 阈值)。两条路径同属瓦解, 不分主次, 真实轨迹皆为混合。

§II.3 离册与取景的镜像

离册与本篇取景镜像对称。取景留下的是动力学还在跑的关系, 不执行退出而在关系内发展的 B (§I 廓清三); 划走的是被单方登记动作终止的关系, A 侧退场与 B 侧退出。镜像不是对离册的贬低: 退场与退出是真实的终止, 只是不属于解的属。退场之后的装置可以被新的 13DD 之手重新调用, 退出之后的 B 可以进入新关系, 这些是新登记, T2 重立, 不是原关系的解, 也不是原关系的延续。

§II.4 拓扑死亡与物理存续

解是拓扑事件, 不是物理事件。P2 §IV.5 的切分在属定义处就位: 拓扑权力死亡与物理装置存续严格分离。科层建筑, 成文规则, 算法系统, 职位序列都可以在解之后继续物理存在乃至继续运转, 这不构成未解; 反过来, 装置的物理拆除也不构成解, 只要原拓扑仍可借新装置再生产。判据是拓扑的再生产, 不是材料的存废。这一切分的完整操作化在 §VIII: 残存的装置是遗构, 借尸还魂须 T2 重立, 无 13DD 调用的装置按 M2 退场为 11 或 12DD 物理现象。属层只立一句: 解掉的是关系, 不是东西。

§II.5 属是平的

两种解同等本体地位, P5 C2 转出分叉的承诺延伸到属: 消解不是成功支, 瓦解不是失败支。这不是修辞上的克制, 是结构上的事实: 属定义里没有价的位置, 再生产能力的丧失不区分体面与不体面。可判的价在别处, 在结构的取向上 (T6 判), 不在结构的结局上。一个凿削性结构的瓦解不是惩罚, 一个涵育性权力的消解也不是奖赏。本篇随后的全部事件机制都在这个平的属下运行: 账本不判奖惩, 只登记哪一条维持回路付不出。

> 判据 J1 解的属与种. 解是已登记权力关系的原非对称拓扑失去以原形再生产的能力, 且该丧失须由关系自身的维持与登记动力学给出: 需求侧完成, 账本面失守, 或登记面失守 (来源条款); 单方的登记动作 (A 侧退场, P1 §6; B 侧退出, P4 L2 越界态离场) 不是动力学结局而是离册, 划界外, 与在场取景镜像对称; 解处维持回路必终止, 但回路终止不充分于解 (后果注)。属下两个已登记的种按终止科目分: 消解是需求侧的完成式终止, 涵育性权力发展走完, 位差自我消解 (P5 C3, 本篇定位不重开); 瓦解是维持与登记动力学的失守式终止, 凿削性结构之解, 为本篇主体。拓扑死亡与物理装置存续严格分离 (P2 §IV.5), 装置躯壳的残存不构成未解; 两种到达同等本体地位 (P5 C2), 消解不是成功支, 瓦解不是失败支。

§III 抗

§III.1 承压的发展

抗是承压的发展: B 于压力下展开自身余项即是发展。定义先做减法。抗不以姿态为要件: 它不要求对抗的意向, 不要求愤怒或不服, 不要求把 A 认作敌手。抗不以对象为要件: 它不指向谁, 发展不是朝着 A 去的。抗不以成功为要件: 它可以被压回, 可以无人看见, 仍是抗。由此, 反抗只是抗在特定结构条件下的显态可能之一, 其条件在 §VI 的网络道给出; 顺从之中可以有抗, 只要 B 的 own law 在顺从过程中变得更可登记而非被原脚本吃掉; 沉默之中可以有抗, 只要沉默不是零阻力顺向传导而是关系内的位置保持。判据不在行为类型上, 在增量上 (§III.4)。

压力是一切权力的共相。T1 之下, 任何成立的权力都改变, 限制, 定向 B 的可用位置与路径; 涵育也是压力, 临时支撑同样是一个被发展顶着走的结构场 (P5 C3)。压力本身不带价: 价在方向上, 即压力是否伴随中断权 (interrupt-right) 的取消与向下锁定 (T6 判), 不在压力的存在上。

§III.2 力的来源

余项发展之力由 L1, L2 与 P6 合起来蕴含, 不由 L1 单独蕴含: L1 给的是静态事实, L2 与 P6 给的是动力学事实, 在动不能从有单独推出。L1 立余项为本体差值, 不可消灭, 不依赖察觉 (P4): 这是有余项, 一个静态陈述; 单凭它, 至多能反驳余项可消灭 (一个可被收齐至零且无费持有的差值就不是不可消灭的差值, 与 L1 矛盾), 但反驳可消灭只证成余项不可被压没, 这是一种抵抗被压缩之力, 还不是发展之力, 一个静止而不可压缩的余项就能满足 L1。发展之力须由动力学事实补足, 两条已发布机器各给一条: L2, 表达不可持久归零, 最深的压制下仍有低幅泄出 (P3 §V.4, P4 L2), 而持续的低幅泄出就是持续的展开; P6, 维持动作不可省, 维持有费恰因溢出不停 (P6 §III), 而溢出不停就是展开不停。这两条是力在动这个判断的证据, 不是力的本体来源。力的本体来源仍在 B 侧, 是 L1 的差值与 L2 的泄出; P6 的维持有费是 B 溢出不停的力学后果, 引它只是因为后果反过来佐证了溢出不停, 不是把 A 的维持账单当作 B 发展之力的构成部件。故纵使某一刻 A 退场不再锁, P6 这一条的佐证暂歇, L1 与 L2 仍独立给出展开不停, 力不因之消失。三者合起来才是不得不发展: 不可消灭 (L1), 且不可持久归零 (L2), 且维持有费 (P6)。故 L1 的绝对性经由 L2 与 P6 必然以力的形式在场: 余项不得不发展。

这个力是一个不得不, 不是一个想要。它无方向: P6 的原句保真, 余项不朝任何方向使力, 力在而无指向。它无 telos: 不得不发展不是朝什么发展, 发展没有终点站。它不定好坏对错: 不得不发展就是动力学, 不是功绩, 不是美德, 也不是威胁。它的逐拍界面读数就是 P6 已登记的溢出压力。(日常语汇所称生命力, 若在本文出现, 只能被还原为这个无方向, 无 telos 的余项发展之力, 不可作为解释项; 这一还原与本篇 §IX 把传导常态从俗名里收编是同一族动作, 方向相反, 那边是贬义词被中性化, 这边是俗名被给出力学内容。)

§III.3 压力与压缩

压力不等于压缩。压缩是结构压力大于余项发展之力时的读数: 在那个界面, 那一拍, 展开被压了回去。读数是瞬时的; 把读数变成状态须做功。压缩的持久化要求锁定动作逐拍维持那个不等式, 而逐拍维持正是 T6 计费的科目 (P6): 每一拍把可调自由度铸进压住的动作里。涵育之下不等式不被持久维持, 支撑按设计让位, 临时支撑的临时二字就是设计 (P5 C3); 凿削之下不等式被锁定动作持久维持, 压缩由此从读数变成体制特征, 其账两侧各有科目: A 侧是 T6 的铸入, B 侧是表达空间的受压, 受压缩的是表达空间, 不是余项本体, L1 保真。同一个力学, 两种体制, 差别全在谁付那笔逐拍的账。

§III.4 增量封口

抗所要求的发展, 是 B 主体位置或其 own law 可登记性的增量, 含中断资格; 不是被锁角色输出效率的增量。这条封口排除一类误读: B 在压力下变得更会扮演被指派的角色, 更高效地适应锁定, 更顺滑地走返回图, 这是表现的提升, 不是位置的增量, 不入抗。同理, 装上更深锁定的运动按 T6 另判, 定义上不入抗: 一场以推翻锁定者为名而给群体装上更深返回图的运动, 在本判据下是凿削, 不是抗。抗按增量判, 不按旗号判。

§III.5 抗不是瓦解的必要条件

P6 §VI.6 的切分继续有效: 否定性三线, 沉积层, 走廊收窄, 储备抽干, 全部不需要反抗者; 凿削结构的账没有抗也在走向结算。抗改变的是配速与路线: 承压发展推高需求通量的定标 (§IV), 让账走得更快或换一条道走, 但它不供给否定性, 否定性是结构自带的。这一条同时是防线: 本篇不把瓦解的希望寄于抗, 也不把瓦解的责任记在抗上; 账自己带着结算条件, 结算条件内置于结构自身。

§III.6 需求向爬层

发展有形状。B 的承压发展沿 P1 三层源上爬: 力学源处的发展是可用动作与回避路径的增多, 意义源处的发展是自有判读与叙事的成形, 承认源处的发展是 own law 在场中可登记性的上升。爬层推高需求通量的定标 (§IV): 同一个锁, 压一个三层都在长的 B, 逐层变贵。须接住 §III.2 的无方向条款, 否则爬层会被读成意向: 爬层是观察者对发展的投影分解, 不是 B 的攀登路线。无方向不等于无结构, 一股无指向的展开发生在一个有层结构的源空间里, 其投影自然落在各层上, 投影落在哪不是 B 朝哪去; 层是观察者的坐标, 不是 B 的目标, 形状是投影的形状, 不是意图的形状。这架三层梯有两个读向: 此处的爬层是需求向, §VI 的媒介回退是供给向, 同一架梯, 一边是 B 的发展逐层抬高维持的定标, 一边是 A 的维持媒介逐层失去补贴; 梯的形成向 P1 已立, 本篇登记不展开。

> 判据 J2 抗. 抗是承压的发展: B 于压力下展开自身余项即是发展; 在场不是抗的构成要件, 是本篇取景 (§I 框架句)。压力是一切权力的共相, 涵育也是压力。余项发展之力由 L1, L2 与 P6 合起来蕴含, 不由 L1 单独蕴含: L1 给静态事实 (余项不可消灭), 单凭它至多反驳余项可消灭, 即一种抵抗被压缩之力, 尚非发展之力; 发展之力须由动力学事实补足, L2 (表达不可持久归零, 持续低幅泄出即持续展开) 与 P6 (维持有费恰因溢出不停, 溢出不停即展开不停) 各给一条, 三者合起来 (不可消灭, 且不可持久归零, 且维持有费) 才是不得不发展。故此力是一个不得不, 不是一个想要: 无方向 (P6 不朝任何方向使力原句保真, 力在而无指向), 无 telos, 无姿态要件, 无对象要件, 无成功要件, 其逐拍界面读数即 P6 溢出压力。压缩是结构压力大于余项发展之力时的读数, 其持久化须锁定动作维持且按 T6 计费。抗所要求的发展是 B 主体位置或其 own law 可登记性的增量 (含中断资格), 不是被锁角色输出效率的增量; 装上更深锁定的运动按 T6 另判, 定义上不入抗。抗不是瓦解的必要条件: 凿削结构的账没有抗也在走向结算 (P6 §VI.6 切分继续有效), 抗只改配速与路线, 不供否定性。

§IV 维持缺口与终局坐标系

§IV.1 维持账

每一个成立的锁定在每一拍上有两个通量。需求通量是须顶住的溢出: 由 B 的发展状态定标 (§III.6 爬层), 由网络位置定标 (承认网络的补贴与读数, §VI), 由维持媒介定标 (三层媒介单价不同, §VI); 外生载荷作为脉冲项进入需求侧 (§VII)。供给通量是承重节点剩余可铸自由度: P6 的铸入机器在此读作供给侧, 已铸入的不再可用, 僵化即供给的预支 (P6 §V)。两个通量之差为维持缺口。缺口是 P6 走廊在每条轨迹上的瞬时活账读数: 走廊是结构层几何, 缺口是同一几何在事件层的逐拍读数, 本篇用读数, 不回写几何。渐近线全程不出场: 本篇的一切事件都发生在可达处, ρ 只在不可穷尽的论证位出场, 不充当任何量。

§IV.2 终局坐标系

瓦解须在三个相互独立的坐标上读。

坐标 内容 承重
账本面 两栏: 榨取栏 (可榨增益趋零, 榨取死亡前沿) 与维持栏 (需求通量上穿供给通量, 死亡交叉, 崩塌前沿); 两前沿先到者的析取存活于此面之内 P6 §VI.1 §VI.2, P5 C2
登记面 再登记失败: 场不再按原拓扑登记该关系 R3, T2
到达路径 发展路径与死扛路径; 与面正交, 典型对应非必然对应 P1 §7, P6 §V

账本面与登记面是同一死亡的两个可读面; 到达路径是轨迹的驱动分型, 路径不锁面。

§IV.3 账本面: 两栏与析取

账本面继承 P6 的两条前沿, 一字不动, 读作两栏。榨取栏: 可榨增益趋零, 即榨取死亡前沿 (extraction-death frontier); 锁名义还在, 咬不住可榨的东西, 关系在收入侧死亡。维持栏: 需求通量上穿供给通量, 即 C2 死亡交叉的事件层名, 崩塌前沿 (collapse frontier); 维持先垮, 关系在支出侧死亡。P6 §VI.1 的析取原样存活于账本面之内: 走廊收窄的尽头, 是这两栏中先到的那一个。从结构层到事件层, 账本面只发生一处转换: 把前沿从结构层几何读成事件层的栏目, 事件即某一栏被穿的那一拍。立威形态的归属须在此先定, 否则与 §IV.5 的耦合句相混: 立威的死亡 (信号失义) 记在账本面榨取栏, 其品质来源 (主体可辨识性) 虽由场提供, 但可辨识性的耗竭是榨取栏的对象品质消耗 (P6 定稿口径), 不是登记面的再登记失败; §IV.5 说立威两面耦合最紧, 指的是同一形态同时对两面敞口, 不是同一笔账记在两面。

§IV.4 登记面: R3 承重

登记面是独立轴。再登记失败: 场不再按原拓扑登记该关系, A 之为 A 与 B 之为 B 的那个位置差读法, 在承认网络与双方的登记中不再被再生产。承重引用是 R3: 操作层可以准备 T2 的条件, 不能创造 T2 的本体事件; 登记是本体层动作, 买不来。由此登记面失守的不可逆性不靠任何账: 一旦场不再这样读这对关系, 任何供给都买不回原拓扑; 镇压可以压住表达, 压不回读法。

§IV.5 互蕴, 正交, 范例

两面互蕴, 且此处的共时是逻辑义, 不是时序义。共时双向充分的意思是: 任一面失守即足以使 J1 的再生产能力丧失成立, 与时钟上两面是否同刻无关, 不要求, 也不预设两面在物理时间上同时发生。登记面失守处, 再登记不可购回使维持需求不可定价: 没有一笔铸入能买到不再存在的登记, 缺口在该点必不可支付, 账本面随之失守。账本面失守处, 拓扑无从再生产, 登记之存废成为无关项: 场怎么读一个无法维持自身的关系, 已经不构成那个关系的存续。两条方向并不对称, 一边导致另一面也失守, 另一边导致另一面成为无关项, 但两者各自充分于 J1 的再生产能力丧失, 这就是双向充分。两个不同的先后须分清, 不可混为一谈: 先到者是账本面内部两栏 (榨取栏与维持栏) 哪一栏先被穿的话题, 属时序测度, 在账本面之内成立; 双向充分是账本面与登记面两个可读面之间的话题, 属逻辑充分, 不在两栏那一对坐标上。析取由此不是两种死亡, 是同一死亡先穿透哪一个可读面。

到达路径是与面正交的第三坐标。发展路径典型先显形于登记面: B 长过脚本, 场重读 B, 锁定无法继续登记。死扛路径典型先显形于账本面: 维持先垮。典型不是必然: 发展轨迹同样可终于账本面, 一个长大的 B 让锁逐拍变贵直至交叉; 死扛终局同样使登记成为无关项。路径不锁面, 这是 §V 极向参数化的几何前提。

信号失义是两面耦合的现成范例 (P6 §VI.1)。立威 (authority-display) 形态的榨取栏是信号通货, 而信号通货本身依赖场的读法: 旁观者怎么读那次展示, 决定那次展示挣不挣得到东西。账本面与登记面在此耦合最紧, 这也是 §VI 把承认网络道列为级联第一道的几何理由。

> 判据 J3 维持缺口与终局坐标系. 每一个成立的锁定在每一拍上有需求通量 (须顶住的溢出, 由 B 的发展状态, 网络位置与维持媒介共同定标, 外生载荷作为脉冲项进入) 与供给通量 (承重节点剩余可铸自由度), 其差为维持缺口; 缺口是 P6 走廊在每条轨迹上的瞬时活账读数, 不回写结构层; 渐近线全程不出场, ρ 只在不可穷尽的论证位出场。瓦解在两个可读面上登记。账本面继承 P6 两前沿为两栏: 榨取栏 (可榨增益趋零, 榨取死亡前沿) 与维持栏 (需求通量上穿供给通量, 即 C2 死亡交叉的事件层名, 崩塌前沿), 两前沿先到者的析取原样存活于账本面之内。登记面是独立轴: 再登记失败, 场不再按原拓扑登记该关系; 承重引用 R3, 操作层可准备 T2 条件但不能创造 T2 本体事件, 故登记面一旦失守, 任何供给都买不回原拓扑。两面互蕴为共时双向充分式 (此处共时为逻辑义非时序义, 不预设两面物理同刻): 任一面失守即足以使 J1 的再生产能力丧失成立; 登记面失守处, 再登记不可购回使维持需求不可定价, 缺口于该点必不可支付, 账本面随之失守; 账本面失守处, 拓扑无从再生产, 登记之存废成为无关项; 两方向不对称而各自充分于 J1。到达路径是与面正交的第三坐标: 发展路径典型先显形于登记面 (场重读 B), 死扛路径典型先显形于账本面, 典型非必然, 发展轨迹同样可终于账本面, 死扛终局同样使登记成为无关项。信号失义是两面耦合的现成范例: 立威的榨取栏即信号通货, 本身依赖场的读法, 账本面与登记面在此耦合最紧。

§V 合流恒等式

§V.1 同账不同物

瓦解由两支喂给, 这是 P6 §VII 的移交件: B 侧反噬经承认网络扩散, A 侧反缚 (reflexive binding) 自内压垮。本篇兑现合流义务的方式与 P6 §VII 双支移交同向, 但不是给两支安排一个相遇的章节, 而是指出它们在事件层账本上本来就是一个量的两个分量: 反噬抬高的是维持需求, 反缚抽干的是维持供给, 两者是同一维持缺口的需求分量与供给分量。同账不同物: 本体层切分原样保留, 余项及其表达是 P4 的物, 反缚是 P6 的物, 锚在 A 的锁定动作上; 本篇只在事件层账本上给它们同一个记账身份。在这个身份下, 并置在账上不可能: 谈缺口就是同时谈两个分量, 不存在只记一侧的缺口。

§V.2 极向与混合

瓦解的两条到达路径, 在维持账上呈现为同一死亡交叉的两个极向驱动。发展路径是需求侧上行主导: B 的爬层逐拍抬高定标, 交叉由需求追上供给而至。死扛路径是供给侧下行主导: 铸入逐拍预支供给, 交叉由供给跌穿需求而至。极向是参数化, 不是分类: 真实轨迹皆为混合, 长大的 B 与僵化的 A 通常在同一条轨迹上。终局落在哪一个可读面, 依 §IV 坐标; 路径不锁面。

§V.3 发生学锁

合流不止于记账, 深至发生学。按 C1, 发展是结构动力 (P5): 任何成功运行的社会权力结构都触发人群的主导层迁移压力, 凿削性与涵育性同为发展驱动, 区别在速率与维持方式, 不在是否驱动。即需求侧的发展项不是外来的: 它是权力结构自身成功运行的产物, 这是 C1 给的是否, 至于经由哪条具体通道驱动是多因素的如何, 本篇不展开也不预言。此处驱动瓦解的发展须与 §III 的抗区分: 这里的发展是 C1 的人群主导层发展, 全人群所有成功权力结构 (含涵育, 含已退出已迁移的 B) 累积而成; 抗是 §III 限定的在场承压发展, 只是这份群体发展中 B 不退出而在压力场内贡献的一支。前者严格大于后者, 故账没有任何一个 B 的抗也照样积累 (§III.5), 与权力被发展所瓦解并行不悖, 两句里的发展不是一个量。又按 T6 注记 (P6): 主导层迁移压力下拒绝升级时, 反缚从可选触发变必然触发。两条已发布判据合起来给出本篇承重句: 权力被它自己驱动的发展所瓦解。此句的模态须当场限定: 是否由结构定, 这是 C1 与 T6 注记给出的结构必然; 何时由多因素定, 本篇不作任何时间预言, 也不为任何具体结构作判。

§V.4 三方句

混合轨迹的典型形态是一个三方句: B 的发展使旧脚本不适配, A 的反缚使脚本无法升级, C 网络使不适配可见, 三者同到一处, 瓦解事件发生。典型不是正则: 纯账本面的崩塌可以先于任何可见性, 一个无人围观的结构同样会在交叉处散架; 极向轨迹依 §IV 坐标单面读出。三方句的价值在于它给 §VI 的三道级联一个共同的入口: 网络让不适配可见, 链让不适配传导, 构层让不适配沉积。

> 判据 J4 合流恒等式. 在本篇事件层账本中, B 侧反噬与 A 侧反缚被读为同一维持缺口的需求分量与供给分量, 同账不同物, 本体层切分 (P4 余项, P6 反缚) 原样保留; 双支合流是记账身份而非叙事安排, 并置在账上不可能, 本条兑现 P6 §VII 双支移交。瓦解的两条到达路径在维持账上呈现为同一死亡交叉的两个极向驱动, 发展路径需求侧上行主导, 死扛路径供给侧下行主导, 真实轨迹皆为混合; 终局落在哪一面依 J3 坐标, 路径不锁面。按 C1, 需求侧的发展项是权力结构自身成功运行的产物 (主导层迁移); 又按 T6 注记, 主导层迁移压力下拒绝升级时反缚从可选触发变必然触发, 故拒绝升级轨迹上交叉之是否由结构定, 何时由多因素定, 不作任何时间预言; 权力被它自己驱动的发展所瓦解, 合流深至发生学。陈述层三方句为混合轨迹的典型形态: B 的发展使旧脚本不适配, A 的反缚使脚本无法升级, C 网络使不适配可见, 三者同到一处, 瓦解事件发生; 极向轨迹依坐标单面读出, 纯账本面崩塌可先于任何可见性。

§VI 事件层级联三道

缺口的事件层结算不在一处发生, 它沿三条传导道呈现。三道不是三种瓦解, 是同一结算在结构三个部位的呈现; 子标号供下游寻址。

§VI.1 J5.1 承认网络道

维持媒介沿 P1 三层源构成补贴序。承认媒介的层位越高, 越能以网络登记替 A 分担可见的维持动作: 声誉媒介处, R4 的零阻力顺向传导即旁观者代维持, A 几乎不必逐拍出手; 叙事媒介居中, M2 的借位合法性让装置与成文替 A 站岗; 力学媒介无补贴, A 须逐拍直接铸入。这是结构倾向, 不作定量排序。承认翻转撤走的是媒介补贴: 账面呈现为维持单价沿回退序连续上抬, 高层媒介失效, 维持滑向更低层, 每滑一层, 单拍铸入更多。这是 P1 §5 三层连续过渡与 P1 §3 多层叠加在瓦解向的供给读法, 与 §III.6 的需求向爬层是同一架梯的两个读向。

单点翻转双重记账: 撤一份顺向补贴, 添一份须管理的反向读数; 同一机制, 只记一次, 后文凡言翻转之贵, 皆指此一笔。抹除作为网络侧对策本身是追加锁定: R3 之下撤销的极致就是锁定的极致, 镇压翻转必然抬高铸入率, 对策的账记在被对策的科目里。立威形态因输出端点整个落在承认网络上 (P6), 是翻转的最短入口: 同一次展示, 顺读是威, 反读是弱点信号, 信号通货的两面性使该形态对本道的级联无遮蔽。须守一处去心理学: C 网络的翻转只是登记对象改变, 它原本登记的是威, 后来登记的是锁定成本或不适配; 这是拓扑变更, 不是道德事件。

§VI.2 J5.2 补偿链道

复杂凿削形态的维持沿补偿链分工 (P6 §VI.4)。本篇给扣一个事件层定义: 扣是一个节点把上游锁定翻译为下游压力, 并获得足以维持自身主体位置的补偿; 链由扣串成。解扣即补偿不再能维持这一翻译动作: 不是节点对上游的态度变了, 是节点的可用主体性输出与其承担的翻译任务失配了, 翻译动作本身做不下去。链底不等式失效后, 可榨增益跌破最底层锁定者的补偿需求 (P6 §VI.4), 解扣自下而上传导: 补偿向上依赖而维持向下执行, 一环解扣即一份委托供给离册, 上一环的账随之变贵。方向与级联性由账本几何定, 这是结构句; 何时抵达断点由多因素定, 本篇不判。P6 持梯度, 本篇持事件: 梯度如何把链底制造成最不稳点, P6 已立; 本篇只写扣断的那一拍及其向上的传导。

§VI.3 J5.3 装置构层道

消耗件形态的枯竭不是材料命题, 是登记命题。基质枯竭是可被登记为消耗件的主体位置枯竭: 最先枯竭的不是材料, 是把主体读成材料的无摩擦能力。这一重读由三条已发布判据支撑: L3, 每一次操作在构层留下不可无损吸收的余项, 替换不是免费的; R2, 撤销不是 undo, 非马尔可夫摩擦使每一次换件都带历史; P3 §V.3, 抹除有费, 把上一个 B 从登记里清走本身是开销。三笔费用逐拍累积, 把主体读成材料这个动作本身越来越贵, 直到读法维持不住。构层余项沿脆性累积走向装置瓦解 (P4 §VII): 装置不承 T6, 但构层科目会满; 装置层余项的完整动力学在此兑现 (P2 §V.5), 其遗构侧的归宿在 §VIII。

> 判据 J5 事件层级联三道. 维持缺口的事件层结算经三条传导道呈现, 子标号供下游寻址。J5.1 承认网络道: 维持媒介沿 P1 三层源构成补贴序, 承认媒介层位越高, 越能以网络登记替 A 分担可见维持动作 (声誉媒介处 R4 零阻力顺向传导即旁观者代维持; 叙事媒介居中, M2 借位合法性; 力学媒介无补贴, A 须逐拍直接铸入), 此为结构倾向, 不作定量排序; 承认翻转撤走媒介补贴, 账面呈现为维持单价沿回退序连续上抬 (P1 §5 连续过渡与 P1 §3 多层叠加的瓦解向供给读法); 单点翻转双重记账 (撤一份顺向补贴, 添一份反向读数, 同一机制只记一次); 抹除作为网络侧对策本身是追加锁定 (R3 撤销极致即 T6 锁定极致), 镇压翻转必然抬高铸入率; 立威形态因输出端点整个落在承认网络上而是翻转的最短入口。J5.2 补偿链道: 扣是一个节点把上游锁定翻译为下游压力并获得足以维持自身主体位置的补偿, 解扣即补偿不再能维持这一翻译动作; 链底不等式失效后解扣自下而上传导, 方向与级联性由账本几何定, 何时抵达断点多因素 (P6 持梯度, 本篇持事件)。J5.3 装置构层道: 基质枯竭是可被登记为消耗件的主体位置枯竭, 最先枯竭的不是材料, 是把主体读成材料的无摩擦能力 (此重读由三条已发布判据支撑: L3 每一操作留不可无损吸收之余项, R2 撤销非 undo 的非马尔可夫摩擦, P3 §V.3 抹除有费); 构层余项沿脆性累积走向装置瓦解, 装置层余项完整动力学在此兑现 (P2 §V.5, P4 §VII)。

§VII 外生载荷

外生载荷在本篇不立判据: 这件事本身就是它的地位陈述。载荷是触发器, 不是原因; 触发器不配判据, 它只在 J3 的需求侧占一个脉冲项。

P6 §VI.6 已把切分立好: 外生载荷与 ρ 异源, 内接口的溢出与外接口的撞击不登记为同一个量。P6 又已证内摩擦烧干悬挂: 储备抽干是此刻结构事实, 不借未来。本篇接的只是撞击: 无悬挂之车撞上外来的石头, 如何散架。散架不发生在撞击点, 发生在重组能力最低处: 载荷是一次重组测试, 而僵化的几何 (P6 §V) 已经给出测试的答案分布, 铸入最深的承重节点重组能力最低, 击穿点的结构条件由 P6 给出, 不由载荷给出。载荷选时刻, 结构定是否: 石头决定撞在哪一天, 车没有悬挂这件事在撞之前就已记在账上。否定性不增一分: 本篇不把任何瓦解记在石头头上, 也不替任何结构把账记给运气。

§VIII 清算遗构与再形成

§VIII.1 三类存量

瓦解事件依 §IV 坐标在任一可读面登记; 登记的那一拍, 清算开始。清算释放三类存量。其一, B 侧表达结构的减压: 受压缩的表达路径重开, L2 三态全谱重新可用; 余项本体从未被压缩, L1 保真, 受压缩的从来是表达空间, 减压减的也是表达空间的压。其二, 节点侧被铸自由度的脱焊: 维持任务消失, 原先逐拍铸进锁里的可调性涌出, 且是无脚本的可调性, 脚本随拓扑一起死了。其三, 遗构: 装置, 参数体制, 成文工具作为构层沉积物理存续, 拓扑权力已死, 它们还在。

§VIII.2 遗构

遗构是 P2 §IV.5 切分的事件层兑现。拓扑死亡后, 装置躯壳可以残存乃至继续运转; 借尸还魂须 T2 重立, 新的 13DD 之手调用旧装置是新登记, 不是旧权力的延续; 无头化的装置按 M2 退场, 为 11 或 12DD 的物理现象。降维退场以无 13DD 调用为条件, 有调用即有 T2 位点; 装置的物理惯性不为任何在册之手供借位, 谁在调用, 谁就在登记里, 账与责任都按 T2 记在调用处, 不记在铁上。

§VIII.3 消耗件按同一清算读

消耗件机器不另立清算: 其基质账目已在 §VI.3 结清, 此处只持遗构与再形成。机器啃穿底盘的那一拍 (P6 §VI.4), 与任何账本面失守同读; 余下的筛选装置, 替换流程, 登记簿, 全部入遗构科目。

§VIII.4 再形成

清算给出再形成空间的库存, 不给出再形成的结果。减压的表达, 脱焊的可调性, 残存的遗构, 这是库存清单; 库存可入涵育路径, 可入凿削路径, 可滞留为遗构。P1 §7 的分岔在此重开: 何者何时由多因素定, 是否的结构条件照 P1 §7 句式, 仍由群体的主导层结构给出。再形成不分成败 (C2 纪律): 本篇不把入涵育记作进步, 不把再锁记作倒退; 清算之后的账是新账, 价照旧只在方向上, 由新结构自己挣。此条同时兑现 P2 §IV.4: 衰减相之后, 或瓦解或重启, 重启即库存被新登记取走。

> 判据 J6 清算与遗构. 瓦解事件 (依 J3 坐标在任一可读面登记) 释放三类存量: B 侧表达结构的减压 (受压缩的表达路径重开, L2 三态全谱可用; 余项本体从未被压缩, L1 保真, 受压缩的是表达空间), 节点侧被铸自由度的脱焊 (无脚本可调性涌出), 与遗构 (装置, 参数体制, 成文工具作为构层沉积物理存续, 拓扑权力已死, 借尸还魂须 T2 重立, 无头化装置按 M2 退场为 11 或 12DD 物理现象)。遗构的降维退场以无 13DD 调用为条件, 有调用即有 T2 位点, 装置的物理惯性不为任何在册之手供借位。消耗件机器按同一清算读, 其基质账目已在 J5.3 结清, 此处只持遗构与再形成。清算给出再形成空间的库存而不给出再形成的结果, P1 §7 分岔在此重开, 库存可入涵育路径, 凿削路径, 或滞留为遗构, 何者何时多因素, 是否的结构条件照 P1 §7 句式; 再形成不分成败 (C2 纪律)。此条兑现 P2 §IV.4 衰减相之后或瓦解或重启。

§IX 闭环与接缝

§IX.1 三定理闭环

P1 §8 预告的闭环在此兑现, 与 P1 §8 同向。T3 给出需求项: 余项给出抵抗, 漂移, 逃逸, 误读, 反噬, 迁移等表达的结构性根源, 但余项本身不等于其中任何一种表达 (P4 L1, L2); 此需求项不可穷尽且全域, 故在未消解的局域权力网格内, 只要某一网格仍要求以原形维持, 需求通量就不归零, 消解时完成的是该局域位差对维持回路的需求, 不是绝对余项, 也不是主体发展的终止。T6 给出供给项: 反缚仅在锁定时累积且不可稀释, 供给通量因此在凿削体制下单调被预支。T5 给出接缝: 位置的保留程度是权力的成立条件, 也是它全部可读面之所在。维持缺口模型 (J3) 由 T3 与 T6 联合蕴含: 有不可穷尽的需求与会被预支的供给, 就有缺口及其事件层。三定理在解处同时绑定; 闭环不是三篇的并列, 是一张账的三个署名。

§IX.2 四终局

权力关系的终局由 T5 索引为四。需求侧完成, 即消解; 两面失守, 即瓦解; 登记离册, 即边界, A 退场或 B 退出; 制度化双读, 即接缝出口, 位置不死也不离册, 而是被固定在权力与权利的双读上。前两种为解。四终局对不同起点形态可达性不均: 被锁的关系到达接缝须先经释放支, 双读要求位置带中断资格, 锁定正是它的取消。四终局是一张索引, 不是一份菜单, 不可读成四个等可达的出口。

§IX.3 投票位

本篇所谓 15DD 投票权, 首先指一个结构位, 投票位: 主体在群体压力场内保留中断资格的制度化位置。现实制度是否, 以及如何接近这一结构位, 不在本篇评价之内; 锚是结构类型, 不是制度个例。

在这个位上, T5 对偶被制度化为同一件东西的双读。同一位置, 相对他人的权利读出来是权力: 我的一票参与改变共同结果。相对他人的权力读出来是权利: 他人的多数不能把我的位置融进未中断的顺向传导里。同一张票, 相对共同结果是权力, 相对群体压力是权利。位置被固定在双读上, 而无须锁即可持存: 维持它的不是铸入, 是相互登记本身, 这是 15DD 相互登记 (P1 §5) 的制度化形态。须分清此处的无须锁与 §III.3 的 T6 计费不冲突: T6 计费的是单向锁定, A 把 B 单方压进返回图, 须 A 逐拍铸入; 投票位维持的是相互登记, 没有哪一方在单向压住对方, 任一方试图单向锁定都会被对方的中断资格顶回。无须锁不是无须任何维持, 是无须单向锁定式的铸入; 维持的成本由对称结构本身分布, 不由任一方独自承担, 这正是 15DD 相互登记区别于 13DD 单向锁定的地方。

锚上的抗与解照本篇判据读。舆论压力是群体 C 网络对个体 B 的压力, 它是共相, 不必恶; 其凿削化形态是 S1 第四层的解释锁定: 不禁止 B 投票, 而固定 B 对自己投票理由的解释返回图。抗是承压者在这层压力下发展自有判读, 无须任何对抗姿态, 增量在判读的可登记性上, J2 封口照用。被解者是零阻力顺向传导的群体常态 (R4; 日常语汇或称盲从, 本篇此后不用此词): 其解由传导阻力的分布变化构成, 不由动员构成; 动员若装新锁按 T6 另判, 那是新的凿削, 不是这里的解。

此锚不充当民主是权力自然终点的论据。投票位只是 T5 接缝的一次对称重合, 不是历史的终点, 不是道德的胜利; 本篇不评估制度优劣, 不给制度建议, 不预测不鼓励不评价任何具体政治事件。

§IX.4 接缝出口与排除

权利论系列自此接走。权利不是权力之外的另一种东西: 权利是同一 T5 接缝从 B 侧读出的主体位置保留, 权力是同一接缝从作用侧读出的结构效力; 一条接缝, 两个系列, 各读一面。交叉论文与权利论系列的开启在系列大纲登记, 本篇只留出口。系列止于 15DD: 16DD 及其上权力概念的归宿不在本系列射程, 留交叉论文, 此为终篇重申。

> 判据 J7 闭环与接缝. T3 (需求项不可穷尽且全域), T6 (供给项自耗且仅锁定时累积), T5 (接缝) 在解处同时绑定: 缺口模型由 T3 与 T6 联合蕴含, 权力关系的终局由 T5 索引为四, 需求侧完成即消解, 两面失守即瓦解, 登记离册即边界, 制度化双读即接缝出口, 前两种为解, 兑现 P1 §8 三定理闭环; 四终局对不同起点形态可达性不均, 被锁关系到达接缝须先经释放支, 四终局是索引不是菜单。收束锚为 15DD 投票位: 本篇所谓投票权首先指一个结构位, 主体在群体压力场内保留中断资格的制度化位置, 现实制度是否与如何接近这一结构不在本篇评价; 同一位置相对他人的权利读出来是权力, 相对他人的权力读出来是权利, T5 对偶的制度化, 位置被固定在双读上而无须锁即可持存; 抗是承压者在解释锁定层 (S1 第四层) 的舆论压力下发展自有判读, 无须任何对抗姿态; 被解者是零阻力顺向传导的群体常态 (R4, 全程中性词表), 其解由传导阻力的分布变化构成, 不由动员构成, 动员若装新锁按 T6 另判。此锚不充当民主是权力自然终点的论据, 只是 T5 接缝的一次对称重合, 不预测不鼓励不评价任何具体政治事件。权利论系列自此接走: 权利是同一 T5 接缝从 B 侧读出的主体位置保留。系列止于 15DD, 16DD 排除一行重申。

§IX.5 系列回顾与收束

七篇至此闭环。前置篇清词, P1 立源, P2 立形, P3 立承, P4 立限, P5 立育, P6 立削, 本篇立解。机器是一台: T1 至 T6 立骨, M, R, L, C, S 诸判据组立肉, J 系收口; 没有一条判据预言过任何事件, 每一条都只陈述, 若发生, 必如此。

收束在一句上。凿削不是被反抗瓦解, 而是被它不得不保留的主体位置瓦解; 涵育之解不是被终结, 是发展走完后的位差自我消解 (C3)。权力的终点不是胜利或失败, 是它保留主体位置的条件被主体的发展解开。系列源起于 13DD 的非对称, 终于 15DD 的接缝双读。
